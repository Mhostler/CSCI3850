<TITLE> CLINTON ORDERS REVIEW OF $310 MILLION SPY FACILITY</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> CLINTON ORDERS REVIEW OF $310 MILLION SPY FACILITY</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-USA-SECRET-CLINTON </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  President Clinton Tuesday ordered an immediate inquiry into how the U.S. government began building a $310 million spy satellite headquarters without telling Congress it would be so big or cost so much. </P>
<P> ``The president believes the American public deserves a full accounting of how their tax dollars are being spent on this construction project,'' White House spokeswoman Dee Dee Myers told reporters. </P>
