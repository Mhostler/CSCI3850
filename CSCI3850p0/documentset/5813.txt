<TITLE> Earthquake hits southwestern Iran oil province</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Earthquake hits southwestern Iran oil province</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-IRAN-EARTHQUAKE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>NICOSIA, April 18 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  An earthquake measuring 5.3 on the Richter scale, powerful enough to cause heavy damage, hit Iran's oil-producing southwestern province of Khuzestan on Tuesday, the Iran news agency IRNA reported. </P>
<P> It said the epicentre of the quake, which struck at 10:43 local (06:13 GMT), was 500 km (300 miles) southwest of Tehran. </P>
<P> There were no immediate reports of casualties or damage. </P>
