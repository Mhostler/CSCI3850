<TITLE> Students hold anti-U.S. marches across Iran</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Students hold anti-U.S. marches across Iran</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-IRAN-USA </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>TEHRAN, Iran (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Thousands of students across Iran demonstrated Wednesday for a second day against a U.S. trade embargo, Tehran Radio said. </P>
<P> Tens of thousands of high school and university students marched in the city of Hamadan in western Iran, chanting ``Death to America,'' ``Death to Israel,'' and ``Economic sanctions have no effect anymore,'' the radio said. </P>
<P> Other demonstrations were held in the oil city of Agha Jari in the southwest and several other towns, it said. </P>
<P> Students also held marches Tuesday in 11 cities and towns, setting fire to American flags and calling for a boycott of U.S. goods, the daily Jomhuri Eslami said Wednesday. </P>
<P> Washington announced plans for an economic embargo on Iran earlier this month, alleging that it supported international terrorism and was seeking a nuclear bomb. Iran has denied both charges. </P>
