<TITLE> At least 9 dead, 11 missing in Mexico bus mishap</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> At least 9 dead, 11 missing in Mexico bus mishap</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-MEXICO-BUS </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>MEXICO CITY (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A bus plunged into a river in the eastern state of Veracruz, killing at least nine people and leaving 11 others missing, a government spokesman said Saturday. </P>
<P> Rescue workers were attempting to find the missing in the rain-swollen waters of the Huxanapan River near the town of Las Choapas, a spokesman for the nearby city of Minatitlan told Reuters. </P>
<P> The spokesman said the accident occurred Friday when the bus plunged into water about 30 feet deep after its brakes failed as it approached a small ferry that was to take it across the river. </P>
<P> Twenty-four people were rescued from the bus and were in good condition, the spokesman said. </P>
<P> Las Choapas is located 27 miles east of Minatitlan on the border with the state of Tabasco. </P>
