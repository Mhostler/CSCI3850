<TITLE> Postal Governors back rise in postage prices</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Postal Governors back rise in postage prices</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-POSTAL-RATE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The governors of the U.S. Postal Service Monday approved a rate increase that includes raising the cost of a first-class stamp to 32 cents from 29 cents. </P>
<P> The increase, the first in four years, takes effect January 1. In taking the action the board adopted the recommendations issued by the independent Postal Rate Commission at the end of last month. </P>
