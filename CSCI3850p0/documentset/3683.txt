<TITLE> Fired House historian strikes back</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Fired House historian strikes back</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CONGRESS-HISTORIAN-STATEMENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The new House historian, hired and fired by House Speaker Newt Gingrich, assailed the press Tuesday, saying ``the charges against me are slanderous and outrageous.'' </P>
<P> Historian Christina Jeffrey issued a brief statement the morning after Gingrich said through a spokesman that she was fired from her $85,000-a-year job after reports that she criticized a federal education program on the Holocaust in 1986 for not presenting the views of Nazis and the Ku Klux Klan. </P>
