<TITLE> Carter leaves Zagreb for Bosnia on peace mission</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Carter leaves Zagreb for Bosnia on peace mission</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-YUGOSLAVIA-CARTER-DEPARTURE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>ZAGREB (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Former President Jimmy Carter left Zagreb to fly to Sarajevo Sunday to pursue a peace mission in Bosnia. </P>
<P> Carter and his wife, Rosalynn, took off at 2.55 p.m. (local) in a U.N. military plane from Croatia after consultations with U.N. peacekeeping leaders, the Croatian president, Bosnian prime minister Haris Silajdzic and the U.S. ambassador to Zagreb. </P>
