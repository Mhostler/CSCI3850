<TITLE> Sacked Oslo bourse director found dead</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Sacked Oslo bourse director found dead</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-NORWAY-BOURSE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>OSLO, Norway (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The director of the Oslo Bourse has been found dead the day after he was fired from his job because of financial irregularities, the Oslo Bourse said Tuesday. </P>
<P> ``The Oslo Bourse has with deep sorrow received the message that Bourse Director Erik Jarve died during the night'' a terse statement said. </P>
<P> It gave no cause for the death of Jarve, 50, but the same panel Monday decided to fire its director for mixing his private economy and the economy of the bourse. </P>
<P> The Norwegian news agency NTB quoted police as saying Jarve was found drowned in a lake near his summer house at 1:25 a.m. (local time) in the southern province of Telemark. </P>
<P> The bourse statement lauded Jarve's ``outstanding work for Oslo Bourse and the Norwegian equities market in the past 25 years.'' </P>
<P> Monday, the bourse in an announcement that stunned Norway's business community said it has decided to dismiss Jarve immediately as a consequence of irregularities linked to his work as bourse director. </P>
