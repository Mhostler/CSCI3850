<TITLE> Italian police arrest 15-year-old crime ``boss''</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Italian police arrest 15-year-old crime ``boss''</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-ITALY-BOY </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>NAPLES, Italy (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Police have arrested one of the youngest members of the Naples-area Mafia, a 15-year-old boy who pushed heroin for the mob. </P>
<P> The boy, identified only by his first name, Ciro, had been a full member of the Camorra for at least two years. Police said Ciro, who was arrested Monday, controlled drug sales in his neighborhood. </P>
<P> He worked for the Camorra, the local version of the Mafia, on the streets of the run-down town Ercolano, near the site of the ancient town of Herculaneum, which was destroyed in 79 A.D. by the eruption of Mount Vesuvius. </P>
<P> Ciro was one of the youngest known members of the Camorra. His two older brothers are in jail for organized crime offenses. </P>
