<TITLE> Frustrated Greek basketball fans clash with police</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Frustrated Greek basketball fans clash with police</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-GREECE-BASKETBALL </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>ATHENS (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Frustrated Greek fans clashed with police in the port of Piraeus Thursday after home team Olympiakos lost 73-61 to Real Madrid in the final of the European basketball clubs' championship in Spain. </P>
<P> Police said some 1,000 fans threw flares, set fire to cars and smashed dozens of shop windows in the Pasalimani area south of Athens shortly after the end of the match in Zaragoza. </P>
<P> There were no immediate reports of arrests or injuries. </P>
<P> It was the second straight season that Olympiakos reached the European basketball championship final and lost both times to Spanish clubs. </P>
