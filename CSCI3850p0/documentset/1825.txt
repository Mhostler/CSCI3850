<TITLE> Mexico drug baron's brother held in assassination case</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Mexico drug baron's brother held in assassination case</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-MEXICO-SHOOTING-DETENTION </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>MEXICO CITY (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The brother of one of Mexico's top suspected drug cartel chiefs has been detained and is being questioned about the cartel's possible involvement in the murder last week of a top ruling party official, the attorney general's office said Wednesday. </P>
<P> ``Humberto Garcia Abrego, brother of the ringleader of the Gulf drug cartel Juan Garcia Abrego, has been detained...and is being questioned about his possible links with the Gulf cartel and the suspected participation of that group in the murder of Francisco Ruiz Massieu,'' the attorney general's office said in a statement. </P>
