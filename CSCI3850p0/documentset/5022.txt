<TITLE> Colombian accused of smuggling condoms of cocaine</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Colombian accused of smuggling condoms of cocaine</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-AUSTRALIA-COCAINE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>SYDNEY, March 20 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A Colombian man appeared in a Sydney court on Monday charged with trying to smuggle into Australia 79 cocaine-filled condoms in his stomach. </P>
<P> Jose Moreno, 29, was detained by customs officials at Sydney airport on Friday night after arriving on a flight from the Colombian capital, Bogota, a customs official told Reuters. </P>
<P> Moreno was taken to a Sydney hospital where he passed the condoms containing 790 grams (1.7 lb) of cocaine, which has an estimated street value of A$120,000 (US$87,550). </P>
<P> ``People have swallowed a lot more condoms, but not with that much cocaine,'' said the customs official. </P>
<P> Moreno was remanded in custody to appear in court again next Wednesday. He did not enter a plea. </P>
