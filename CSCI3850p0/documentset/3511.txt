<TITLE> Clinton lands in South Carolina for New Year's Eve</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Clinton lands in South Carolina for New Year's Eve</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CLINTON-ARRIVE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>BEAUFORT, S.C. (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  President Clinton arrived by presidential jet at Beaufort Marine Air Station en route to Hilton Head island, where he and his family will celebrate the new year. </P>
<P> The presidential party was to travel by car to the island, where the Clintons will take part in ``Renaissance Weekend,'' an annual gathering of individuals interested in public policy. </P>
<P> Clinton was accompanied by his wife on the trip. Their daughter, Chelsea, arrived at Hilton Head earlier. The family returns to the White House Sunday. </P>
