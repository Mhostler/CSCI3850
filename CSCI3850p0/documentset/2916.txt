<TITLE> North Sea cod yields up lost dentures</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> North Sea cod yields up lost dentures</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-DUTCH-TEETH </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>AMSTERDAM (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A set of false teeth lost in the North Sea have been returned to their Dutch owner after being found in the belly of a cod. </P>
<P> Dutch newspapers said Wednesday that 60-year-old Cor Stoop lost his teeth overboard when he was seasick on a pleasure trip three months ago. The dentures were discovered in a cod caught by an Amsterdam angler over the weekend. </P>
<P> Stoop heard about the find in a broadcast and went to the radio station to try the teeth on. They were a perfect fit. </P>
