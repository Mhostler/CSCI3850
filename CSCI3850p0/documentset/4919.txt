<TITLE> Mexico army to pull back to encourage Chiapas peace</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Mexico army to pull back to encourage Chiapas peace</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-MEXICO-CHIAPAS </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>MEXICO CITY (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The Mexican government Tuesday said it will pull army troops back from villages inside the conflict zone in the southern state of Chiapas to encourage guerrilla leaders to agree to peace talks. </P>
<P> ``The positioning of military contingents will be mantained outside the rural communities and villages in the conflict zone,'' the Interior Ministry said in a statement. </P>
