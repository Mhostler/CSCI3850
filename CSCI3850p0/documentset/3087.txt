<TITLE> Brazil troops find bodies in shanty-town drug hunt</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Brazil troops find bodies in shanty-town drug hunt</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-BRAZIL-RIO </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>RIO DE JANEIRO (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Brazilian troops searching for drug traffickers in a Rio shanty-town have discovered decomposing remains of two people, one of them thought to be a child, a news agency reported Thursday. </P>
<P> The remains were buried in a clearing on a hill close to the Gamba slum, one of nine shanty-towns under occupation by soldiers since early Wednesday, Estado news agency reported. </P>
<P> Soldiers believe further bodies may be buried at the site, the agency said. </P>
<P> Victims of fighting between drug gangs in Rio de Janeiro are sometimes buried in clandestine graves close to shanty-towns. </P>
<P> Soldiers also discovered a sack of marijuana in a hut next to an area used for voodoo ceremonies and two machine guns and other firearms, Estado said. </P>
<P> The military is in charge of a crackdown on drug crime and corruption in Rio. </P>
