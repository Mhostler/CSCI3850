<TITLE> Israelis wounded in West Bank ambush -- settlers</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Israelis wounded in West Bank ambush -- settlers</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-MIDEAST-SHOOTING </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>JERUSALEM (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Two Israelis were wounded, one critically, when their car was ambushed Sunday near a Jewish settlement in the occupied West Bank, security sources said. </P>
<P> They said unidentified gunmen fired at an Israeli car headed for Utniel settlement in the Hebron area, wounding the two. The car, hit several times, overturned, they said. </P>
