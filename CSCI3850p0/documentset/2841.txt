<TITLE> French intellectuals rally for Bosnia</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> French intellectuals rally for Bosnia</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-YUGOSLAVIA-FRANCE (PICTURE) </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>PARIS (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Several hundred people demonstrated in central Paris Saturday, accusing the United Nations of failing to protect Bosnian civilians from Serb attacks. </P>
<P> Protesters carried banners which read: ``Bihac: new extermination zone protected by the U.N.,'' denouncing the latest Serb offensive around the U.N.-declared ``safe area'' in the Muslim Bihac enclave in northwest Bosnia. </P>
<P> Other banners declared: ``Answer the pleas of the people of Sarajevo.'' </P>
<P> The demonstrators were led by former Health Minister Leon Schwarzenberg and authors Bernard-Henri Levy and Andre Glucksmann. </P>
