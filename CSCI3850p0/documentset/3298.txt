<TITLE> Eleven dead, 27 hurt on Christmas shopping bus trip</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Eleven dead, 27 hurt on Christmas shopping bus trip</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-BRAZIL-BUS </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>RIO DE JANEIRO, Brazil (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Eleven people on a Christmas shopping trip were killed when their bus collided in heavy rain with two trucks in southern Brazil Saturday, Globo television reported. </P>
<P> Twenty-seven other passengers were injured in the accident near the town of Foz do Iguacu, Parana state, Globo said. </P>
<P> Because the accident happened at 7 a.m., many of the 43 passengers aboard were sleeping, Globo said. </P>
<P> They had been bound for Christmas shopping in Paraguay from the Sao Paulo town of Sao Jose dos Campos. </P>
