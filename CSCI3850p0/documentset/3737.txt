<TITLE> Clinton to meet U.S. Congress leaders on Mexico</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Clinton to meet U.S. Congress leaders on Mexico</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-MEXICO-USA-CLINTON </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  President Bill Clinton will meet leaders of Congress on Thursday to discuss ways to help ease the financial crisis in Mexico, administration officials and congressional sources said. </P>
<P> The meeting was expected to take place at 4:30 p.m. EST , they said. </P>
<P> Senate Majority Leader Bob Dole of Kansas, House of Representatives Speaker Newt Gingrich of Georgia, Senate Democratic Leader Tom Daschle of South Dakota and House Democratic Leader Richard Gephardt of Missouri will attend the meeting, the sources said. </P>
<P> U.S. Treasury Secretary Robert Rubin may also attend the talks. </P>
<P> A senior Clinton administration official, who declined to be identified, said the group would discuss ``steps they can take to restore confidence in the Mexican financial markets.'' </P>
