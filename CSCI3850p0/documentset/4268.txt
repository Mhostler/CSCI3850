<TITLE> Rescue under way for sinking trawler off Britain</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Rescue under way for sinking trawler off Britain</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-BRITAIN-BOAT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>LONDON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  British rescuers were scrambled Friday morning to rescue crew members of a Danish trawler who said they were sinking off the southwestern coast of England. </P>
<P> Coast guards said the captain of the coaster Marianne Danica had radioed for help, saying the boat was expected to sink within 15 minutes. He said there were six people on board. </P>
<P> Rescuers said a navy helicopter was being sent to the scene, along with a lifeboat from the port of Plymouth. </P>
<P> The 240-foot boat reported it was eight miles southeast of Plymouth. </P>
