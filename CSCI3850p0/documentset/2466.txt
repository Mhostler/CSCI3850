<TITLE> Olympic flight to Salonika hijacked-state TV</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Olympic flight to Salonika hijacked-state TV</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-GREECE-HIJACKING </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>ATHENS, Greece (Reuters) </DATELINE>
<P>  An Olympic Airways plane carrying about 70 passengers was hijacked over Belgrade and is expected to land in the northern Greek city of Salonika, Greece's state television reported. </P>
<P> The pilot indicated in code that the airliner, flying from Dusseldorf to Athens, was hijacked and was landing in Salonika for negotiations, it said. </P>
<P> Police, ambulances and fire engines rushed to Salonika's Macedonia airport, the television said. </P>
