<TITLE> Turkey says 26 PKK rebels killed in Iraq clash</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Turkey says 26 PKK rebels killed in Iraq clash</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-TURKEY-IRAQ </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>ANKARA, April 24 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Turkish troops killed 26 Kurdistan Workers Party (PKK) rebels in a clash in northern Iraq on Sunday, Anatolian news agency reported on Monday. </P>
<P> Two Turkish soldiers were killed and two wounded in the clash, the official agency said, quoting military officials. No further details were given. </P>
<P> It said 490 PKK rebels had been killed and 13 captured since Turkey sent more than 30,000 troops into northern Iraq on March 20 to attack bases used by the PKK in its separatist insurgency in southeast Turkey. </P>
<P> The agency did not give a figure for overall military casualties but official reports last week said over 50 soldiers had died in the operation. </P>
<P> Turkish Prime Minister Tansu Ciller said in Washington last week that Turkish forces would withdraw ``very soon'' from northern Iraq, but did not set a date. </P>
