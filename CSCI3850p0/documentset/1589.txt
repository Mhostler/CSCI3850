<TITLE> U.S. military chief meets Cedras after gunfight</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> U.S. military chief meets Cedras after gunfight</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-HAITI-MEETING </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>PORT-AU-PRINCE (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The head of U.S. military forces in Haiti, Lieutenant General Hugh Shelton, met military leader Raoul Cedras in an unscheduled meeting Sunday, hours after a gunfight in Haiti's second city. </P>
<P> Witnesses said Cedras and Shelton met at the U.S. controlled airport early Sunday morning. </P>
<P> On Saturday night, U.S. Marines shot dead nine Haitian men after shots were fired from the police headquarters in Cap Haitien, slightly wounding a U.S. sailor. </P>
<P> It was unclear who the dead Haitians were. </P>
<P> After the meeting beteen Shelton and Cedras, a Reuters photographer saw Cedras board a U.S. helicopter which took off heading north. </P>
