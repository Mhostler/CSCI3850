<TITLE> At least 10 killed in South Korean explosion</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> At least 10 killed in South Korean explosion</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-KOREA-EXPLOSION 1STLD </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES>(Updates with death toll, details)</NOTES>
<DATELINE>SEOUL, South Korea (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  At least 10 people were killed and some 100 injured in a rush-hour explosion at a subway construction site in the South Korean city of Taegu Friday, state radio said. </P>
<P> ``A powerful gas explosion shook the construction site, killing at least 10 commuters and injuring about 100 others,'' Korea Broadcasting System said. </P>
<P> The radio said it was believed the explosion was caused by leaking gas from a supply pipe. </P>
<P> The blast smashed about 30 buses and other vehicles, hurling several into a construction pit. At least 10 houses nearby were badly damaged. </P>
<P> Dozens of ambulances rushed the injured to hospitals but busy morning traffic added to the confusion, the national Yonhap news agency said. </P>
