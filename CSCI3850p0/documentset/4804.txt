<TITLE> U.S. ambassador back to Moscow for Kremlin talks</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> U.S. ambassador back to Moscow for Kremlin talks</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-RUSSIA-USA </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>MOSCOW (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  U.S. ambassador Thomas Pickering has cut short a trip to Russia's far east for talks with Kremlin officials on important bilateral issues, the American embassy said Thursday. </P>
<P> An embassy statement said Pickering had shortened his program by a day ``at the request of Washington and is returning to Moscow today to discuss with Russian authorities important questions of U.S.-Russian relations.'' </P>
<P> Embassy officials said nothing about the nature of the talks and gave no clue about the identity of the people Pickering intended to meet. </P>
<P> They said Pickering had been in the port city of Vladivostok as part of U.S. efforts to broaden contacts in Russia's regions. </P>
