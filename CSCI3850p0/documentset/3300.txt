<SUBJECT> BC-YUGOSLAVIA-CARTER-FLIGHT -2 FRANKFURT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE></DATELINE>
<P> Asked by reporters whether there could be peace by Christmas in Bosnia, Carter said: ``I hope so. I don't know.'' </P>
<P> He did not comment further before boarding the plane at the U.S. Rhein-Main airbase, where he arrived earlier after a transatlantic commercial flight from Atlanta to Frankfurt. </P>
<P> At the airbase, Carter met former U.S. special envoy to ex-Yugoslavia and now ambassador to Germany, Charles Redman, and his successor as special envoy, Charles Thomas. </P>
