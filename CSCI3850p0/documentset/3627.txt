<TITLE> King Hussein meets Greece's Papandreou</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> King Hussein meets Greece's Papandreou</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-MIDEAST-JORDAN-GREECE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>AMMAN, Jordan (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  King Hussein and visiting Greek Prime Minister Andreas Papandreou met Saturday for talks on the Middle East peace process and bilateral cooperation, the state-run news agency Petra said. </P>
<P> Papandreou arrived on an official visit Friday, after first visiting Syria. </P>
