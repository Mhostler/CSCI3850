<TITLE> Terry Norris disqualified, loses WBC superwelter title</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Terry Norris disqualified, loses WBC superwelter title</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-BOXING SUPERWELTERWEIGHT RESULT URGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>MEXICO CITY (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  World Boxing Council superwelterweight champion Terry Norris lost his title at two minutes and two seconds of the fifth round on a disqualification after fouling Luis Santana of the Dominican Republic Saturday. </P>
<P> Santana was declared champion but was taken out of the ring on a stretcher several minutes after Norris appeared to have hit him on the back of the head or neck. </P>
