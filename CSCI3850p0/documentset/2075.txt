<TITLE> Five die in Iowa tavern blast</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Five die in Iowa tavern blast</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-BLAST-TAVERN </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WATERLOO, Iowa (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Five people were killed Monday when a natural gas explosion demolished a roadside tavern, sending a giant fireball into the air and leaving a large hole in the ground, police said. </P>
<P> In addition to the four men and one women who died inside Buzz's Bar, a one-story frame building, two others were injured, one seriously. </P>
<P> ``We think it was a natural gas explosion,'' said Lt. Bob Greenlee of the Waterloo Police Department. </P>
<P> ``The bar was open for business. There's nothing left now but a hole in the ground. One of our officers was patrolling on the interestate at the time and saw it. He said a fireball about 150 feet wide shot acoss the road,'' he added. </P>
