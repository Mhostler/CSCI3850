<TITLE> Sarajevo sniper kills French peacekeeper</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Sarajevo sniper kills French peacekeeper</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-YUGOSLAVIA-FRENCH </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>SARAJEVO, April 14 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A sniper killed a French U.N. peacekeeper in the Sarajevo suburb of Dobrinja on Friday, U.N. spokeswoman Miriam Suchaki said. </P>
<P> The man, the commander of an armoured vehicle, was hit in the neck as it drove between the airport and the city and died shortly after reaching the U.N. hospital. </P>
