<TITLE> Paramilitary group linked to Oklahoma bombing</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Paramilitary group linked to Oklahoma bombing</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-BLAST-ARREST-MILITIA </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Federal agents have arrested a suspect in the Oklahoma City bombing and U.S. television networks reported he was a member of a paramilitary group called the Michigan Militia. </P>
<P> CNN identified the suspect as Timothy McVeigh, a member of the Michigan Militia and added that police were seeking two brothers, Terry and James Nichols, who were also members of the group in Decker, Michigan. </P>
