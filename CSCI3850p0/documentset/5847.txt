<TITLE> Spanish minister blames ETA for car-bomb attack</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Spanish minister blames ETA for car-bomb attack</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-SPAIN-BLAST-ETA </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>MADRID, Aoril 19 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Spain's Justice and Interior Minister Juan Alberto Belloch blamed Basque guerrillas of ETA for a car bomb attempt to kill opposition leader Jose Maria Aznar in Madrid on Wednesday. </P>
<P> He told reporters at a hospital where Aznar was being treated for slight head injuries that the blast was a ``strike against democracy, as Senor Aznar is one of the most important representatives of our democracy.'' </P>
