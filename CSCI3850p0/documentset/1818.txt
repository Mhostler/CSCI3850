<TITLE> Italian government rejects minister's resignation</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Italian government rejects minister's resignation</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-ITALY-MINISTER-REJECTION </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>ROME (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The Italian government Wednesday unanimously rejected an offer by Justice Minister Alfredo Biondi to resign, cabinet spokesman Giuliano Ferrara said. </P>
<P> Biondi tendered his resignation to Prime Minister Silvio Berlusconi earlier Wednesday following a dispute with magistrates about Milan's elite pool of anti-corruption investigators. </P>
