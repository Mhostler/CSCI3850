<TITLE> Tyson may be released from prison next month-CNN</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Tyson may be released from prison next month-CNN</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-TYSON </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Former world heavyweight boxing champion Mike Tyson, serving a six-year sentence for raping a 19-year-old woman, could be released from an Indiana prison early as next month, CNN reported Monday. </P>
<P> CNN said Tyson had earned the possible early release for good behavior in prison. </P>
<P> Tyson, 32, became the youngest world heavyweight champion in history at the age of 20. His career ended March 26, 1992, when he was sentenced to six years in jail for the rape of Desiree Washington, a 19-year-old beauty contestant from Rhode Island. </P>
