<TITLE> NATO backs U.N. decision on air strikes</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> NATO backs U.N. decision on air strikes</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-YUGOSLAVIA-NATO-ENDORSEMENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>BRUSSELS (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  NATO Saturday endorsed a Security Council decision to authorize air strikes against targets in Croatia used by Serbs to launch air raids on U.N.-designated safe areas in Bosnia, alliance sources said. </P>
<P> NATO envoys took the decision at an emergency session in Brussels. The Security Council unanimously authorized strikes in response to air attacks from the Serb-controlled Krajina area of Croatia against the Muslim enclave of Bihac in north Bosnia. </P>
