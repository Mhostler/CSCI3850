<TITLE> MISSOURI MAN CHARGED IN ABDUCTION, SLAYING OF GIRL</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> MISSOURI MAN CHARGED IN ABDUCTION, SLAYING OF GIRL</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-USA-KILLING </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>KEOKUK, Iowa (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A 33-year-old Alexandria, Missouri man is being charged with the kidnapping and murder of a nine-year-old girl whose body was found early Thursday in Lee County, Iowa. </P>
<P> Anna Marie Emry's disappearance five days ago prompted a major search that spread through several counties. </P>
<P> Washington County Sheriff Yale Jarvis said in a statement that suspect Larry Lane Morgan was to appear today before a Washington County Magistrate. </P>
<P> Jarvis said officials are continuing their investigation, which will include an autopsy and further search warrants at Keokuk, near rural Alexandria, Missouri, and Warsaw, Illinois. </P>
