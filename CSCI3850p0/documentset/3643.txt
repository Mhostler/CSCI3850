<TITLE> China's ``robber king'' gets life for crime school</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> China's ``robber king'' gets life for crime school</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CHINA-SCHOOL </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>SHANGHAI, China (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A Chinese peasant who turned young boys into thieves and rapists in a school for crime has been sentenced to life in prison, the Wen Hui daily reported Monday. </P>
<P> Hu Shicai styled himself the ``robber king'' of Henan Province in central China after being let out of jail, where he served 15 years for robbery and sex offenses. </P>
<P> The 68-year-old rogue charged fees to school dropouts to teach them his crime skills. </P>
<P> His graduates netted $9,500 and raped eight women and three girls, the newspaper said. Hu said he got the idea for his school from his neighbors, who ran classes teaching embroidery. </P>
