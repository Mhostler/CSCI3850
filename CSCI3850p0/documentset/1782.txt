<TITLE> Agriculture Secretary Espy quits Clinton Cabinet</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Agriculture Secretary Espy quits Clinton Cabinet</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CLINTON-ESPY-RESIGN URGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Agriculture Secretary Mike Espy, under federal ethics investigation for accepting gifts, announced Monday he is resigning from President Clinton's Cabinet ``to overcome the challenge to my good name.'' </P>
<P> ``I must personally overcome the challenge to my good name that continues to distract me from my work in the U.S. Department of Agriculture. I have therefore decided to tender my resignation to the president effective December 31 of this year,'' Espy said at a news briefing. </P>
