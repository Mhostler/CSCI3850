<TITLE> CUBA READY TO DISCUSS ALL ISSUES WITH U.S.</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> CUBA READY TO DISCUSS ALL ISSUES WITH U.S.</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CUBA-UN </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>UNITED NATIONS (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Cuba's U.N. envoy said Wednesday his country was ready to discuss with the United States the current mass migration of Cubans, but only as part of negotiations on all outstanding issues, including the U.S. blockade and the Guantanamo naval base. </P>
<P> Ambassador Fernando Remirez de Estenoz Barciela told a news conference such negotiations would have to be on the basis of full respect for the principle of equality among nations and of Cuba's sovereignty. </P>
