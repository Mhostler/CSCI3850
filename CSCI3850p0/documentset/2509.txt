<TITLE> Independent Sanders re-elected to Vermont House seat</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Independent Sanders re-elected to Vermont House seat</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-ELECTION-VERMONT-INDEPENDENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>MONTPELIER, Vt.(Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Vermont Rep. Bernie Sanders, the only independent and the only socialist in Congress, survived a tough Republican challenge backed by the National Rifle Association Wednesday. </P>
<P> Republican John Carroll conceded the race to Sanders early Wednesday with 91 percent of the precincts counted showing Sanders, the former socialist mayor of Burlington, ahead by 52 to 48 percent. Democrats did not field a candidate in the race. </P>
