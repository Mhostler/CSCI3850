<TITLE> Clinton hails trade accord with China</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Clinton hails trade accord with China</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-TRADE-CHINA-CLINTON </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  President Clinton Sunday hailed an accord with China on U.S. copyright protection, saying it was a strong agreement that would help U.S. companies and workers. </P>
<P> ``U.S. action in China is part of the broader economic strategy of my administration to create high-paying jobs for Americans,'' Clinton said in a written statement. </P>
