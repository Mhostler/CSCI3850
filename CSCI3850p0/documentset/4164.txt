<TITLE> Simpson called wife ``fat pig'' during pregnancy - sister</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Simpson called wife ``fat pig'' during pregnancy - sister</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-SIMPSON-INSULT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>LOS ANGELES (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Nicole Brown Simpson's sister tearfully testified Monday during O.J. Simpson's murder trial that the football legend humilated his wife while she was pregnant by calling her a ``fat pig'' in front of family and friends. </P>
<P> Returning to the witness stand following Friday's dramatic testimony, Denise Brown said, ``He hated fat women. He would always comment about her weight.'' </P>
<P> Denise Brown -- who has said publicly that she believes Simpson murdered her younger sister -- testified earlier that Simpson once grabbed his wife's crotch in a crowded bar and bragged ``This belongs to me.'' </P>
