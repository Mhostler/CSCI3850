<TITLE> U.N. COUNCIL MEETS TO ADOPT HAITI RESOLUTION</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> U.N. COUNCIL MEETS TO ADOPT HAITI RESOLUTION</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-HAITI-UN-MEETING </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>UNITED NATIONS (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The Security Council opened a meeting at 11:53 a.m. EDT Sunday to adopt a resolution clearing the way for a U.S. invasion to topple Haiti's military rulers, if President Clinton decides to launch one. </P>
<P> Nine speakers were listed to address the council before the vote. </P>
