<P> In his first public defense of his record, Foster told ABC television's ``Nightline'' Wednesday night that he had performed 39 abortions as ``physician of record'' and had overseen 55 others as part of an experimental study. </P>
<P> Sen. Orrin Hatch, R-Utah, said at a news conference that he had not seen the program, but had heard about Foster's comments. </P>
