<TITLE> Coastguard says 838 missing after ferry disaster</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Coastguard says 838 missing after ferry disaster</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-FINLAND-FERRY-MISSING </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>TURKU, Finland (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The leader of rescue operations in the sinking of the Baltic ferry Estonia said Wednesday that 838 people were still missing. </P>
<P> ``We have rescued 126 and we have found 42 dead persons. There are still 838 missing persons and the search is continuing,'' Raimo Tiilikainen of the Finnish coastal command told a news conference. </P>
