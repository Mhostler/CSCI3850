<TITLE> French U.N. troops battle Serbs for bridge</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> French U.N. troops battle Serbs for bridge</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-YUGOSLAVIA-BOSNIA-FRENCH URGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>SARAJEVO, May 27 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  French U.N. soldiers fought a pitched battle with Bosnian Serbs on Saturday to regain control of a Sarajevo city centre bridge seized during the night by Serbs dressed in stolen French uniforms, a U.N. official said. </P>
<P> At least one Bosnian Serb soldier was killed and three were taken prisoner by the French in the fighting, U.N. spokesman Alexander Ivanko said. </P>
