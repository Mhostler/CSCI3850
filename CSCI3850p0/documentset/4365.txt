<TITLE> Peru says ceasefire being respected</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Peru says ceasefire being respected</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-PERU-ECUADOR-CEASEFIRE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>LIMA (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Peru's President Alberto Fujimori said Wednesday that a day-old cease-fire is holding in a disputed border zone in the Amazon jungle where Ecuadoran and Peruvian force clashed for 20 days. </P>
<P> ``The cease-fire is being respected by the Ecuadoran side,'' Fujimori told journalists who accompanied him to a Peruvian border post on the banks of the Cenepa River. </P>
