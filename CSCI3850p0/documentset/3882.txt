<TITLE> At least ten dead in Israel blast -- police</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> At least ten dead in Israel blast -- police</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-ISRAEL-EXPLOSION-TOLL URGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>BEIT LID, Israel (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  At least 10 people were killed in an apparent Muslim suicide bombing Sunday at a crowded bus stop in central Israel, a police spokesman said. </P>
<P> ``At least ten are dead and 52 wounded,'' the spokesman told reporters at the scene. </P>
