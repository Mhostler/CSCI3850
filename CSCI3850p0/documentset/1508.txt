<TITLE> Dredge spills oil in New York's East River</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Dredge spills oil in New York's East River</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-USA-SPILL </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>NEW YORK (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A harbor dredge spilled about 8,000 gallons of fuel oil in New York's East River Tuesday, the Coast Guard said. </P>
<P> The spill stretched from Governors Island, southeast of Manhattan, up the East River to Roosevelt Island, just north of the United Nations. </P>
<P> The Coast Guard said the vessel, The Sugar Island, apparently lost diesel fuel during transfer operations while traveling north on the river. </P>
<P> The vessel is owned by North American Trailing Co. Ltd of Oakbrook, Ill. </P>
