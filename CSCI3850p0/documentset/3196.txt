<TITLE> Segregationist Arkansas Governor Faubus dead at 84</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Segregationist Arkansas Governor Faubus dead at 84</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-FAUBUS </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>LITTLE ROCK, Ark.(Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Orval Eugene Faubus, who as Governor of Arkansas used National Guardsmen to block the integration of Little Rock Central High School in 1957, died Wednesday morning. </P>
<P> The cause of death was not immediately announced. However, in several interviews Faubus, 84, acknowleged suffering bone cancer, had twice undergone surgery for prostate cancer and wore a pacemaker to regulate his heartbeat. </P>
