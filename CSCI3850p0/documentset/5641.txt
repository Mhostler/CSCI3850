<TITLE> UN still seeks Iraqi biological arms program</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> UN still seeks Iraqi biological arms program</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-IRAQ-UN-WEAPONS </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>UNITED NATIONS (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  U.N. officials on Monday dismissed Iraq's claim it had imported huge quantities of biological material for hospital laboratories, saying all signs pointed to its use in a germ warfare program, according to a major report on Iraqi weapons. </P>
<P> In concluding that Iraq still had not come clean on biological materials, the U.N. Special Commission in charge of scrapping Baghdad's weapons of mass destruction dashed Iraqi hopes for a speedy end to sanctions on oil exports. </P>
