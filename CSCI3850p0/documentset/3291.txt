<TITLE> Clinton security adviser arrives in Mozambique</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Clinton security adviser arrives in Mozambique</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-MOZAMBIQUE-LAKE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>MAPUTO, Mozambique (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  President Clinton's national security adviser Anthony Lake arrived in Maputo on Saturday for two days of talks with the Mozambican government. </P>
<P> The talks will cover regional and world issues as well as bilateral relations, a U.S. embassy statement said. </P>
