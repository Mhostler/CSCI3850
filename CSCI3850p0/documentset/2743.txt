<TITLE> About 30 planes take part in NATO strike</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> About 30 planes take part in NATO strike</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-YUGOSLAVIA-STRIKE URGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>ZAGREB, Croatia (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  About 30 United States, British, French and Dutch warplanes carried out a NATO strike on the rebel Serb airbase at Udbina in occupied Croatia Monday, NATO allied commander Adm. Leighton Smith told CNN television. </P>
<P> He said the strike, launched from Italy, was successful and all aircraft returned to base. </P>
