<TITLE> ZAIREAN SOLDIERS FINALLY CLOSE BORDER WITH RWANDA</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> ZAIREAN SOLDIERS FINALLY CLOSE BORDER WITH RWANDA</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-RWANDA-CLOSURE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>BUKAVU, Zaire (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Zairean soldiers finally closed the border with Rwanda Saturday afternoon, halting the flood of thousands of Hutu refugees into Zaire, eyewitnesses said. </P>
<P> Reuters reporter Julian Bedford saw thousands of Rwandans waiting patiently in line at Ruzizi, the main bridge crossing between the two countries, in a line monitored by French legionnaires. </P>
<P> The French said the border had been closed at 2 p.m. local time. </P>
<P> Zairean authorities had told aid agencies in Bukavu Saturday morning that the border was officially closed but thousands of refugees continued to stream across while officials at the bridge waited for orders. </P>
