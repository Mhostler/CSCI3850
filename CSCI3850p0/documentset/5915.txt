<TITLE> None hurt as minor quake rattles French Riviera</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> None hurt as minor quake rattles French Riviera</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-QUAKE-FRANCE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>NICE, France (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A minor earthquake rattled the French Riviera city of Nice Friday, causing no casualties and only slight damage, officials said. </P>
<P> The main French seismological center at Strasbourg said the tremor, centring on Menton near the Franco-Italian border just east of Nice, recorded the quake at 4.7 on the Richter scale. </P>
<P> Residents reported cracks in walls, furniture moving about and water splashing in fish tanks in their homes. </P>
