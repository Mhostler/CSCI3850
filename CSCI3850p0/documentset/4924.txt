<TITLE> Clinton aide presses Adams on arms dismantlement</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Clinton aide presses Adams on arms dismantlement</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-IRISH-USA-ARMS </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A top aide to President Clinton met with Sinn Fein President Gerry Adams at the White House Tuesday and urged him to follow through quickly on a promise to discuss IRA disarmament with the British. </P>
<P> Adams, on the day the Irish Republican Army's political wing Sinn Fein opened an office in Washington, met with White House National Security Adviser Anthony Lake for about a half hour. </P>
