<TITLE> 90 rescued after ferry disaster</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> 90 rescued after ferry disaster</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-FINLAND-FERRY-RESCUED URGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>HELSINKI (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  About 90 people have been rescued after a ferry carrying 867 people sank in a storm off southwest Finland early Wednesday, a rescue official said. </P>
<P> ``We have now rescued 90 people...,'' commodore Raimo Tiilikainen, head of the rescue operation, told Finnish television. </P>
