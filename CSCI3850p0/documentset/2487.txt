<TITLE> New York's Moynihan wins re-election to Senate</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> New York's Moynihan wins re-election to Senate</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-ELECTION-NEWYORK-MOYNIHAN </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>NEW YORK (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Democratic Senator Daniel Patrick Moynihan won re-election Tuesday, defeating Republican challenger Bernadette Castro, local television stations said. </P>
<P> WABC and New York 1 News projected that Moynihan, chairman of the Senate Finance Committee, would defeat Castro, who trailed in the opinion polls throughout the campaign. </P>
