<TITLE> GUATEMALA CANDIDATE KILLED IN HELICOPTER CRASH</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> GUATEMALA CANDIDATE KILLED IN HELICOPTER CRASH</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-GUATEMALA-CRASH </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>GUATEMALA CITY (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A rightist presidential candidate and three other people were killed Tuesday in a helicopter crash in northern Guatemala, party officials said. </P>
<P> Jose Pablo Donado, presidential candidate of the Movement for National Liberation (MLN) for 1995 elections, died when the helicopter exploded in mid-air and crashed in the northern province of Baja Verapaz, MLN secretary-general Edgar Figueroa said. </P>
<P> A friend of the candidate, a pilot and a television cameraman were also killed. </P>
<P> No reason was given for the crash but Figueroa told reporters his party did not rule out foul play. </P>
<P> Donado was not running in legislative elections to be held this weekend. </P>
