<TITLE> Israel's army chief Shahak to Washington for talks</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Israel's army chief Shahak to Washington for talks</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-MIDEAST-USA-CHIEF </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>JERUSALEM (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Israeli army chief Amnon Shahak leaves for a 10-day visit to the United States Monday, a military spokesman said. </P>
<P> He said Shahak, who will be the guest of senior U.S. defense officials, would visit military installations and inspect new defense technology. </P>
<P> The spokesman declined to comment when asked if Shahak, a senior Israeli negotiator in peace talks with the PLO, would meet Syrian officials in Washington. </P>
<P> Last December, Shahak's predecessor held talks in Washington with Syrian chief of staff Hikmet Shihabi. </P>
<P> Years of Syrian-Israeli talks have remained largely at a standstill in wrangling over the Israeli-held Golan Heights. </P>
