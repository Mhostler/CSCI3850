<TITLE> Hamas claims it kidnapped Israel soldier</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Hamas claims it kidnapped Israel soldier</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-MIDEAST-KIDNAP URGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>GAZA (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The Islamic Resistance Movement Hamas said Tuesday it had kidnapped an Israeli soldier two days after it carried out a bloody attack in Jerusalem. </P>
<P> In a handwritten statement and video tape sent to an international news agency, Hamas said it would kill the soldier unless the Israeli government met its demands to release its leaders held prisoner by Friday evening. </P>
