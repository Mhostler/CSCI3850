<TITLE> Crosby of Stills, Nash &AMP; Young in hospital</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Crosby of Stills, Nash &AMP; Young in hospital</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CROSBY </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>LOS ANGELES (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Singer-guitarist David Crosby was resting in fair condition at UCLA Medical Center, where he was admitted for liver failure, the hospital said Friday. </P>
<P> Crosby, a member of the folk-rock group Crosby, Stills, Nash & Young in the 1960s and 1970s, was evaluated for a liver transplant at UCLA several weeks ago, the hospital said. He was admitted Wednesday. </P>
<P> According to a statement released by UCLA, Crosby recently ''experienced a moderate decline in his condition.'' Doctors decided to admit him to the hospital while considering treatment options. </P>
<P> Before joining Crosby, Stills, Nash & Young, Crosby was a member of the group the Byrds, which he helped form in 1965. </P>
