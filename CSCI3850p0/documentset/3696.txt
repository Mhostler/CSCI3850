<TITLE> LA jewelers robbed of $5m in gems and jewelry</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> LA jewelers robbed of $5m in gems and jewelry</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-ROBBERY </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>NEW YORK (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Two Los Angeles jewelers riding in a New York limousine were stopped by a group of armed gunmen and robbed of diamonds and gems valued at about $5 million, police said Tuesday. </P>
<P> The jewelers were heading toward JFK International Airport from Manhattan's Jacob Javits Center when their vehicle was forced to the side of the road by another car carrying four or five armed male Hispanics wearing ski masks, police said. </P>
<P> The jewelers were forced from their car and robbed, and the suspects fled in the two vehicles with the limousine driver, police added. </P>
