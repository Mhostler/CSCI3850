<TITLE> Bodies of four boys found in Philadelphia river</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Bodies of four boys found in Philadelphia river</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-BOYS </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>PHILADELPHIA (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The bodies of four boys, aged 11 to 14 years old, have been found in Philadelphia's Schuykill River, police said Wednesday. </P>
<P> ``Divers recovered all four of the juveniles last (Tuesday) night,'' said Philadelphia police officer Ben Frazier. </P>
<P> Frazier said the four juveniles were reported missing Monday. Tuesday evening, a police officer was approached by a male who told him he had had found clothing, bicycles, sneakers and shoes on the river bank. </P>
<P> ``These items matched what the juveniles were reported to have been wearing,'' Frazier said. </P>
<P> He said the bodies were at the city's medical examiner's officer and the cause of their death was under investigation. </P>
