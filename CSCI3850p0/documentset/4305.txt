<TITLE> Deng Xiaoping in good health, daughter says</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Deng Xiaoping in good health, daughter says</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CHINA-DENG </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>HONG KONG (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Chinese paramount leader Deng Xiaoping is in good health for a 90-year-old, one of his daughters Deng Rong was quoted Sunday as saying. </P>
<P> The Hong Kong-based Ta Kung Pao newspaper quoted Deng Rong as saying, in an interview with an overseas Chinese newspaper in New York Saturday, that Deng was still able to walk. </P>
<P> She said he spent a happy Spring Festival in Beijing watching his children and grandchildren playing and took family pictures with them. </P>
<P> Deng had spent the previous seven Spring Festivals in Shanghai, the pro-Beijing daily reported. </P>
<P> The latest edition of the German news weekly Der Spiegel quoted one of Deng's doctors, Wu Jieping, as saying Deng had circulation problems and his health could face a ``rapid change'' at any time. </P>
<P> Deng's health has been the subject of intense speculation in recent weeks. </P>
<P> Deng Rong also alleged she was misquoted in an interview with the New York Times last month, which quoted her as saying her father's health was declining day by day. </P>
