<TITLE> SKATER'S FORMER BODYGUARD SENTENCED FOR ATTACK</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> SKATER'S FORMER BODYGUARD SENTENCED FOR ATTACK</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-USA-HARDING </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>PORTLAND, Ore.(Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Figure skater Tonya Harding's former bodyguard Shawn Eckardt was sentenced Monday to serve 18 months in prison for his role in the attack on her rival Nancy Kerrigan. </P>
<P> Eckardt struck a deal with prosecutors under which he pleaded guilty May 3 to conspiracy to commit assault. He could have been sentenced to up to 20 years in prison, if he had been found guilty after trial. </P>
