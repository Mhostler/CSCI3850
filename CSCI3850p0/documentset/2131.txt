<TITLE> GM reports $552 million profit for third quarter</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> GM reports $552 million profit for third quarter</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-GM </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES>(Eds: also moved to financial news desks)</NOTES>
<DATELINE>DETROIT (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  General Motors Corp., benefiting from a stronger vehicle market, reported Thursday a profit of $552 million for the third quarter, although its North American automotive operation continued to lose money. </P>
<P> The world's largest automaker said the profit, which followed a $113 million loss in the year-ago period, came on strong revenues of $34.5 billion, up from $30.1 billion. </P>
<P> GM's North American car business, which recorded $328 million in red ink for the period, trimmed its deficit from the $1.1 billion it lost in the 1993 quarter. </P>
<P> The automaker's international vehicle business was less profitable than last year, with earnings of $240 million, compared to $403 million. </P>
