<TITLE> SAUDI ARABIA BEHEADS NINE PEOPLE</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> SAUDI ARABIA BEHEADS NINE PEOPLE</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-SAUDI-BEHEADING </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>DUBAI, United Arab Emirates (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Nine people were beheaded in Saudi Arabia Friday, including five Saudi men who sexually assaulted and killed a young boy, Persian Gulf news agencies reported. </P>
<P> Saudi Arabia's official SPA news agency quoted an Interior Ministry statement as saying two convicted Pakistani drug smugglers and a murderer were beheaded Friday. </P>
<P> Gulf news agencies said later Friday that another Pakistani man was beheaded for drug smuggling. </P>
<P> SPA did not mention the five others, but Persian Gulf agencies quoted Riyadh Radio as saying that an Interior Ministry statement announced that five Saudis were beheaded in Jeddah after being found guilty of sexually assaulting and killing a young boy. </P>
<P> The statement said the five, who had been drinking alcohol, physically assaulted the boy before putting him in the trunk of their car and driving him into the desert where they committed ``immoral sexual acts'' with him until he died. </P>
<P> It did not give the age or nationality of the boy. </P>
<P> According to strict sharia (Islamic) laws in force in Saudi Arabia, convicted rapists, murderers and drug traffickers are executed, usually in public after Friday prayers. </P>
