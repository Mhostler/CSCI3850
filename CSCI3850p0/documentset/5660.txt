<TITLE> Algerian forces kill 20 Moslem rebels</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Algerian forces kill 20 Moslem rebels</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-ALGERIA-VIOLENCE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>TUNIS, Tunisia (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Algerian forces have killed 20 Muslim guerrillas in four days of sweep and search operations in seven regions across the country, the official Algerian news agency APS said Tuesday. </P>
<P> Quoting an official security statement, APS, monitored in Tunis, said 12 people, suspected of assassinations and hiding arms and explosives, were arrested in Algiers during the operations carried out between Friday and Monday. </P>
<P> About 40,000 people perished in Algeria's three-year-old civil strive pitting armed Muslim militants against the country's army-backed government troops after the authorities scrapped general election in which Islamists were poised to win. </P>
