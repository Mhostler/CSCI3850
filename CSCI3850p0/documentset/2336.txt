<TITLE> Clinton attends fund raiser for Sen. Robb</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Clinton attends fund raiser for Sen. Robb</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-POLITICS-CLINTON-ROBB </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  President Clinton attended a fund-raising dinner on Monday for Sen. Charles Robb, D-Va., who is in a close race against former Iran-Contra figure Oliver North in the U.S. Senate campaign in Virginia. </P>
<P> The president spent over one-and-one-half hours at Robb's private residence in the affluent Virginia suburb of McLean. The event was closed to press coverage. </P>
<P> Clinton spent the early part of Monday in Pennsylvania to try and sway the neck-and-neck race between incumbent Democratic Senator Harris Wofford and Republican Rep. Rick Santorum in the U.S. Senate race there. </P>
<P> The president, with Election Day just eight days away, continues his grueling campaigning schedule Tuesday with a trip to Michigan and Ohio before returning to Washington for a Democratic candidates' reception at a downtown hotel. </P>
