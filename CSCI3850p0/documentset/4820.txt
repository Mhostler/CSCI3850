<TITLE> Figure Skating-Stojko wins men's gold</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Figure Skating-Stojko wins men's gold</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-FIGURE SKATING MEDALS </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>BIRMINGHAM, England (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Elvis Stojko of Canada won the gold medal in the men's event at the world figure skating championships Thursday. </P>
<P> American Todd Eldredge took the silver medal and Philippe Candeloro of France the bronze. </P>
