<TITLE> COLOMBIA REBELS KILL 45 SOLDIERS IN AMBUSH - RADIO</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> COLOMBIA REBELS KILL 45 SOLDIERS IN AMBUSH - RADIO</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-COLOMBIA-ATTACK URGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>BOGOTA (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Colombian Marxist guerrillas have killed at least 45 soldiers in the country's southern jungle in what has been described as the worst rebel attack against the army in the last two years, the Caracol radio network reported Friday. </P>
<P> Caracol said a column of the Revolutionary Armed Forces of Colombia (FARC) ambushed the soldiers near the oil refinery town of Orito, about 500 km southwest of Bogota near the Ecuadoran border. </P>
