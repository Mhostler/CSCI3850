<TITLE> Jordan ratifies cooperation accord with PLO</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Jordan ratifies cooperation accord with PLO</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-JORDAN-PALESTINIANS </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>AMMAN, Jordan (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Jordan has ratified a cooperation accord concluded last week with the Palestinian self-rule authority, officials said Wednesday. </P>
<P> Officials said the accord, which ends a year of tension between Jordan and the PLO over their separate peace deals with Israel, was ratified by the Jordanian cabinet late Tuesday. </P>
<P> PLO leader Yasser Arafat and Prime Minister Sharif Zeid Bin Shaker signed the accord last Thursday in Amman during Arafat's first visit to see King Hussein since May. </P>
<P> The accord has seven detailed annexes on economic, cultural and political cooperation and a preamble designed to end a bitter debate over custody of Islamic shrines in Arab East Jerusalem. </P>
