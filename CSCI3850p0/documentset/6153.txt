<TITLE> UN envoy arrives in Sarajevo on peace mission</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> UN envoy arrives in Sarajevo on peace mission</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-YUGOSLAVIA-AKASHI </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>SARAJEVO, April 30 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  U.N. special envoy Yasushi Akashi arrived in the Bosnian capital Sarajevo on Sunday in a last-ditch attempt to persuade warring factions to agree an extension of the current ceasefire which expires on Monday. </P>
<P> Akashi told reporters at Sarajevo airport that he would do his best ``to get some kind of agreement to stabilise the situation here.'' </P>
