<TITLE> Twenty-three dead in shootings in Rio -TV report</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Twenty-three dead in shootings in Rio -TV report</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-BRAZIL-DEATHS </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>RIO DE JANEIRO, Brazil (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Eleven suspected drug traffickers were killed in shootouts with police in two Rio shanty-towns, Brazilian television reported Tuesday. </P>
<P> A further 12 deaths were reported in other incidents across the city, Globo said. </P>
<P> Police invaded the shanty-towns of Nova Brasilia and Caixa d'Agua early Tuesday to arrest suspected drug dealers thought to be in the area and were met with gunfire, Globo said. </P>
<P> It added that 11 suspected drug traffickers were killed by police when they returned the fire and that four stolen cars, weapons and cocaine were recovered by police. </P>
