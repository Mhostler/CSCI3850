<TITLE> ALBANIAN LOSES WIFE IN WORLD CUP BET</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> ALBANIAN LOSES WIFE IN WORLD CUP BET</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CUP-ALBANIA </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>TIRANA, July 5 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  An Albanian short of cash for gambling lost his wife after betting her on the outcome of a World Cup soccer match, the daily newspaper Koha Jone (Our Time) said on Tuesday. </P>
<P> The unidentified gambler was so sure that Argentina would beat Bulgaria that he staked his spouse, who disappeared with the winner when Bulgaria won 3-2. The loser complained to police. </P>
