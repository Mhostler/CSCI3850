<TITLE> Clinton says no change in Iraqi troop movements</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Clinton says no change in Iraqi troop movements</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-IRAQ-USA-CLINTON URGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  President Bill Clinton said on Tuesday that the United States has seen ``no change'' in the pattern of Iraqi military activity near its border with Kuwait and that America was continuing with its troop deployments to the region. </P>
