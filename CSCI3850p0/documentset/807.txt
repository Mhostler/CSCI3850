<TITLE> MCENTIRE LANDS SIX COUNTRY MUSIC AWARD NOMINATIONS</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> MCENTIRE LANDS SIX COUNTRY MUSIC AWARD NOMINATIONS</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-USA-COUNTRY NOMINEES </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>NASHVILLE, Tenn (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Reba McEntire landed six nominations Tuesday for the Country Music Association awards including the top ``Entertainer of the Year'' prize for which she will compete against an otherwise all-male lineup. </P>
<P> Other stars vying for the honor are Alan Jackson, Vince Gill, Garth Brooks and Brooks & Dunn. </P>
<P> McEntire was also nominated for Female Vocalist, Album of the Year, Single of the Year, Music Video of the Year and Vocal Event. </P>
<P> The nominees were announced by Pam Tillis and George Jones at the Grand Ole Opry. The 28th Annual Country Music Association Awards show will be held at the Opry October 5 and will be aired on CBS-TV. </P>
<P> Jackson, along with Vince Gill, who will host the October 5 awards show, each earned four nominations in addition to the ''Entertainer'' nomination. </P>
<P> Jackson, who won three CMA awards last year, is nominated for Male Vocalist, Song of the Year (Chattahoochee) and two albums. Gill, last year's ``Entertainer'' winner, is up for male vocalist and three albums. </P>
