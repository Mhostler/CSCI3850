<TITLE> Russia agrees to join NATO partnership-Clinton</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Russia agrees to join NATO partnership-Clinton</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-SUMMIT-NATO URGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>MOSCOW, May 10 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Russia has agreed to join NATO's Partnership for Peace programme but there has been no agreement on the West's proposed expansion of the military alliance, U.S. President Bill Clinton said on Wednesday. </P>
<P> ``While there was no agreement between us on the details of the expansion of the NATO alliance, Russia did agree to enter into the Partnership for Peace,'' Clinton told a news conference after talks with President Boris Yeltsin. He did not say when Russia would sign the Partnership for Peace programme. </P>
