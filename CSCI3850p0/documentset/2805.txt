<TITLE> UK government to resign if it loses Euro-vote</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> UK government to resign if it loses Euro-vote</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-BRITAIN-CONFIDENCE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>LONDON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The British Cabinet unanimously agreed Thursday that the government would resign if it lost next Monday's crucial parliamentary vote on payments to the European Union. </P>
<P> ``If the government were defeated, the government would resign and the prime minister would ask the queen to dissolve parliament,'' the Cabinet said in a statement issued by Major's office. </P>
