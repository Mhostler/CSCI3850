<TITLE> Tourism on the increase in Cambodia</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Tourism on the increase in Cambodia</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CAMBODIA-TOURISM </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>PHNOM PENH, May 1 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The number of foreign visitors travelling to Cambodia has increased sharply generating millions of dollars in revenue for the impoverished Southeast Asian country, Tourism Minister Veng Sereyvuth said on Monday. </P>
<P> The minister said earnings from tourism totalled at least $45 million in 1994 based on the calculation each visitor spent between $250 and $400. </P>
<P> Cambodia is home of the thousand-year-old Angkor Wat temple complex. </P>
