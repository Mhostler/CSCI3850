<TITLE> China to build 72 lb gold Buddha statue</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> China to build 72 lb gold Buddha statue</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CHINA-BUDDHA </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>BEIJING (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  China is to use 72 lbs of gold, 22 lbs of platinum and 132 lbs of silver to build a gem-encrusted statue of Buddha next year, the Xinhua news agency said. </P>
<P> It said the nine-foot high statue would be crafted by the Jinsheng Jewelry Co and the Royal Jewelry and Arts Co of the southern boomtown of Shenzhen under the auspices of the Bureau of Religious Affairs, the State Council (cabinet) and the People's Bank of China. </P>
<P> ``It will be a rare treasure both in China and the world,'' Xinhua quoted an expert as saying. </P>
<P> The golden Buddha will be encrusted with diamonds, rubies and pearls. Xinhua did not say where it would be placed. </P>
