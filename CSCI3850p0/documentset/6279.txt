<TITLE> Paul McCartney's son in lucky escape</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Paul McCartney's son in lucky escape</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-BRITAIN-MCCARTNEY </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>LONDON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Former Beatle Paul McCartney's 17-year-old son James suffered a fractured ankle after his Land Rover overturned, trapping his leg. </P>
<P> He was freed by firemen after 25 minutes and then airlifted to hospital after the crash Wednesday night near his father's farm at Rye in southeast England. </P>
