<TITLE> S.C. grand jury indicts Susan Smith</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> S.C. grand jury indicts Susan Smith</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-MOTHER </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>UNION, S.C. (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A grand jury Monday indicted Susan Smith for murder in the drowning of her two young sons in a nearby lake. </P>
<P> The 18-member jury investigated the charges against the 23-year-old secretary and charged her with two counts of murder. Smith, who has confessed to killing three-year-old Michael and 14-month-old Alex, was not in the Union County Court Monday. </P>
