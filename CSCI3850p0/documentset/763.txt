<TITLE> BOMB WOUNDS PRO-ISRAELI MILITIAMAN, CHILDREN</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> BOMB WOUNDS PRO-ISRAELI MILITIAMAN, CHILDREN</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-MIDEAST-LEBANON-SOUTH </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>MARJAYOUN, Lebanon (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A bomb exploded next to the house of an Israeli-backed militiaman in south Lebanon early Sunday, wounding him and his three children, security sources said. </P>
<P> No group claimed responsibility for the pre-dawn attack in the village of Sheba'a inside Israel's border occupation zone in the south. </P>
<P> The victims were taken to a hospital in the town of Marjayoun where their wounds were described as light to moderate, the sources added. </P>
<P> Pro-Iranian Hizbollah guerrillas and other opponents of peace with Israel stage regular attacks on the Israeli occupation zone. </P>
