<TITLE> Haitian police clash again with protesters</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Haitian police clash again with protesters</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-HAITI-VIOLENCE 1STLD </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES>(Eds: Adds details)</NOTES>
<DATELINE></DATELINE>
<P> The onlookers said soldiers chased the protesters and tried to grab photographer's cameras. At least two people were beaten. </P>
<P> Reporters said the trouble began when soldiers attempted to head off the protesters who had gathered to demand the resignation of army leader Lieutenant General Raoul Cedras. </P>
<P> The crowd scattered at the sight of the soldiers and the blue uniformed police fired tear gas at them as they ran off. </P>
<P> The soldiers hauled four men into the army headquarters and beat one man with rifle butts, witnesses said. </P>
<P> U.S. troops patrolling the capital were not at the scene. </P>
