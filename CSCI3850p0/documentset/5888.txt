<TITLE> Two feared dead in Athens explosion</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Two feared dead in Athens explosion</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-GREECE-EXPLOSION URGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>ATHENS (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A powerful explosion rocked a northern Athens suburb Thursday and police said up to two people were feared killed in the blast. </P>
<P> They could not say whether the explosion in the three-story building at the posh Paleo Psychiko suburb, where many embassies are located, was caused by a bomb or a gas leak. </P>
