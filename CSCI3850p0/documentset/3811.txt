<TITLE> S.African youths held after witchcraft murders</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> S.African youths held after witchcraft murders</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-SAFRICA-WITCHCRAFT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>JOHANNESBURG, South Africa (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Eight South African youths have been arrested in connection with the deaths of four women who were tortured and set alight after being accused of witchcraft, police said Tuesday. </P>
<P> Police spokesman Phuti Setati told Reuters a mob of youths accused the four women, between the ages of 30 and 65, of practicing witchcraft on a 19-year-old man who died after a short illness. The women were dragged out of their homes at Bochum village in the northern Transvaal province east of Johannesburg and sentenced to death by the youths. </P>
<P> ``The women were tortured, bound with wire against a tree in a field and petrol poured over them. They were stoned before being set alight,'' Setati said. </P>
<P> The eight suspects will appear in court this week. </P>
