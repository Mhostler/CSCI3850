<TITLE> Yeltsin ``outraged'' by US stance on arms embargo</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Yeltsin ``outraged'' by US stance on arms embargo</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-YUGOSLAVIA-RUSSIA-USA </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>MOSCOW (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  President Boris Yeltsin said Monday Moscow is ``outraged'' by the possibility that the United States will lift an arms embargo against Bosnian Muslims. </P>
<P> ``(Foreign Minister Andrei) Kozyrev recently visited France. He talked to the foreign ministers of other European countries, who were also outraged by the U.S. action,'' he told reporters. ``I think we will act together with them to ensure peace in the Balkans.'' </P>
