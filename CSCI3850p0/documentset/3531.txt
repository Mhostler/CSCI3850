<TITLE> Mexico police seized 11 tons cocaine in 1994</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Mexico police seized 11 tons cocaine in 1994</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-DRUGS-MEXICO </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>MEXICO CITY (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Mexican police seized more than 11 tons of cocaine, 365 tons of marijuana and 653 pounds of heroin in 1994, the attorney general's office said in a statement Sunday. </P>
<P> Mexico is a major trans-shipment point for drug smugglers sending cocaine and other illegal drugs to the United States. Around 60-70 percent of the cocaine consumed in the United States is estimated to pass through Mexico. </P>
<P> The attorney general's office gave no comparative figures for 1993. </P>
