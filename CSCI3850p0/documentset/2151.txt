<TITLE> Major in Belfast, Irish talks statement expected</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Major in Belfast, Irish talks statement expected</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-IRISH-MAJOR-ARRIVAL </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>BELFAST, Northern Ireland (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  British Prime Minister John Major arrived in Northern Ireland Friday and he was expected to announce the government has accepted an Irish Republican Army cease-fire as permanent, opening the way for historic talks with the IRA's political wing, Sinn Fein. </P>
<P> Reporters said Major arrived at Stormont Castle, seat of British power in Northern Ireland, at 10:25 a.m. (local time). </P>
