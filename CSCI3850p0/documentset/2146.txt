<TITLE> Boutros-Ghali to visit Europe, Caucasus</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Boutros-Ghali to visit Europe, Caucasus</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-UN-BOUTROS </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>UNITED NATIONS (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Secretary-General Boutros Boutros-Ghali leaves Friday for London at the start of a trip that will also take him to Paris, Bucharest, Baku (Azerbaijan), Tbilisi (Georgia), Yerevan (Armenia) and Chisinau (Moldova), a U.N. source said. </P>
<P> He is expected to return to New York on Nov. 7. </P>
<P> In London he will address diplomatic writers while in Paris he will confer with French Prime Minister Edouard Balladur and other senior officials. </P>
<P> During his visit to the Caucasian republics he is expected to discuss the conflicts between Azerbaijan and Armenia over the disputed enclave of Nagorno-Karabakh, and between Georgia and Abkhazian separatists. </P>
