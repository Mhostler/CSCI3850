<TITLE> Jury gets case in ``Hollywood Madam'' trial</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Jury gets case in ``Hollywood Madam'' trial</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-FLEISS-JURY </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>LOS ANGELES (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The jury in the Heidi Fleiss trial was handed the case by a judge Tuesday and began deliberations to determine if the so-called ``Hollywood Madam'' is guilty of running a high-priced call girl ring catering to Hollywood's rich and famous. </P>
<P> Fleiss, 28, has pleaded not guilty to five charges of pandering -- recruiting prostitutes -- and one charge of possessing cocaine. She faces up to 11 years in prison if convicted. </P>
