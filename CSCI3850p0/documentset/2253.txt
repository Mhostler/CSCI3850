<TITLE> One policeman killed, eight injured in Egypt</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> One policeman killed, eight injured in Egypt</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-EGYPT-MILITANTS </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>ASSIUT, Egypt (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  One policeman was killed and eight injured in fighting in southern Egypt between police and Muslim militants battling to install a stricter Islamic state, security sources said Thursday. </P>
<P> They said the fighting erupted at dawn Wednesday when police began a hunt for militants hiding in thick sugar cane fields in the village of Abu al-Ras in the Minya province, some 155 miles south of Cairo. </P>
<P> The clashes lasted all day after the militants opened fire on the police, the sources said. </P>
<P> Seven policemen, including three officers, were wounded and a civilian was also injured. </P>
<P> Police called in reinforcements from the nearby southern province of Assiut and surrounded the fields. Bulldozers were used to clear the fields. </P>
<P> Militants opened fire on a police checkpoint in the nearby village of Dayrut Um Nakhle in the province of Mallawi, killing one policeman and wounding another. A civilian was injured in the attack. </P>
