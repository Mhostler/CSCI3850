<TITLE> German President Herzog arrives in Israel</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> German President Herzog arrives in Israel</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-ISRAEL-GERMANY </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>TEL AVIV (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  German President Roman Herzog arrived in Israel Tuesday for a two-day visit, the second by a German head of state since World War II. </P>
<P> Herzog, who will visit Israel's memorial for the six million Jews killed by Nazi Germany during World War II, was met by Israeli Foreign Minister Shimon Peres at Tel Aviv's airport. </P>
<P> The two men and Herzog's wife, Christiane, were whisked off to Jerusalem and did not speak to reporters. </P>
<P> Herzog, who was to visit the Yad Vashem Holocaust memorial later Tuesday, said last week he would not shirk from addressing Nazi evils nor neo-Nazi violence in modern Germany. </P>
