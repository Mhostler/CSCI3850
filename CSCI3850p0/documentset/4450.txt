<TITLE> Escaped convicts kill U.S. tourist in Panama</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Escaped convicts kill U.S. tourist in Panama</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-PANAMA-TOURIST </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>PANAMA CITY (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A U.S. tourist was murdered Sunday in Panama by an escaped group of convicts who hijacked his yacht, police said Monday. </P>
<P> Robert Messner, 45, died from gunshot wounds after his boat was commandeered by four men who escaped from the Coiba island penal colony, some 30 miles off the southwestern coast of Panama, a police spokesman told Reuters. </P>
<P> After the convicts shot Messner, they ordered his wife Vicky to drop them off on the mainland coast and then escaped, police spokesman Daniel Carrasco said. </P>
<P> Police did not reveal Messner's hometown and the U.S. embassy was unavailable for comment. The police said his wife was unharmed. </P>
