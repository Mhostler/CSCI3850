<TITLE> ''Baby Bells,'' Ovitz to announce joint venture</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> ''Baby Bells,'' Ovitz to announce joint venture</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-BELLS </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>LOS ANGELES (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Hollywood super-agent Michael Ovitz and three regional Bell telephone companies will announce plans Monday for a joint entertainment venture to compete with the nation's cable television systems, a source close to the negotiations said Sunday. </P>
<P> The venture will include Bell Atlantic Corp., NYNEX Corp., and Pacific Telesis Group, who have hired Ovitz, head of Creative Artists Agency, to help organize the enterprise, the source said. </P>
