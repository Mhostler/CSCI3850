<P> ``We have reports of an increase in the number of (rebels), killed from the 128 which we announced last night,'' the spokesman said. He gave no further details. </P>
<P> Turkey, which moved in with 35,000 men backed by tanks and planes, has come under pressure from the United States and European countries to move out of the region as early as possible. </P>
<P> European Union ministers in Ankara on Thursday urged Turkey to withdraw as soon as possible and to take necessary measures to avoid harming civilians. </P>
<P> Turkey's prolonged military presence in the region and any reports of civilian casualties would endanger the approval in the European Parliament of Turkey's early March customs union deal, they said. </P>
