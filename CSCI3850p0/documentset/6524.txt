<TITLE> Tokyo region shaken by moderate earth tremor</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Tokyo region shaken by moderate earth tremor</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-QUAKE-JAPAN </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>TOKYO (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A moderate earthquake shook wide areas of the Kanto region surrounding Tokyo Saturday, but there were no reports of damage or injuries, Japan's National Meteorological Agency said. </P>
<P> The agency said the earthquake measured 4.5 on the Richter scale, struck at 10:27 p.m (9:27 a.m. EDT and was centered in the Izu Islands, 75 miles south of Tokyo. </P>
<P> There was no threat of tidal waves, it said. </P>
