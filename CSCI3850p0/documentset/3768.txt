<TITLE> Four dead in Liberia riot, U.N. compound stormed</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Four dead in Liberia riot, U.N. compound stormed</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-LIBERIA-RIOT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>MONROVIA, Liberia (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  At least four people were killed in riots in Liberia's capital Monrovia Saturday after the latest peace initiative collapsed, security sources said. </P>
<P> An angry crowd stormed the United Nations compound, they said. The dead included a girl run over by a vehicle of the African peacekeeping force, two civilians and a security guard killed in a grenade attack on a political party headquarters. </P>
