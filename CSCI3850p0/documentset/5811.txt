<TITLE> Military plane crashes in Alabama killing 8 - report</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Military plane crashes in Alabama killing 8 - report</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-AIRCRASH URGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A military Learjet crashed and exploded in Alexander City, Alabama, narrowly missing houses but killing eight people on board, CNN reported Monday. </P>
<P> A local radio station disk-jockey told CNN that a very loud crash was heard as the jet hit the ground in a residential area of Alexander City, 40 miles north of Montgomery. </P>
