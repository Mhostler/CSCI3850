<TITLE> Russians said ready for Chechen peace talks</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Russians said ready for Chechen peace talks</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CIS-CHECHNYA-CHERNOMYRDIN </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>MOSCOW, April 21 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Russian Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin has offered to start talks with rebels in Chechnya on agreeing a ceasefire without preconditions, Itar-Tass news agency said on Friday. </P>
<P> Tass said Chernomyrdin made the offer in an appeal to the Chechen people read out at a conference in their capital Grozny. ``There will be no preconditions, and furthermore no repression,'' it quoted the message as saying. </P>
