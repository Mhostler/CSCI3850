<TITLE> Six people of Russian descent shot near Paris</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Six people of Russian descent shot near Paris</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-FRANCE-KILLINGS </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>PARIS (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Six people of Russian descent were shot to death overnight in a wealthy Paris suburb, justice sources said Monday. </P>
<P> They said police found the bodies of three couples in a house in the town of Louveciennes, in the Yvelines area. </P>
<P> It was not immediately clear whether the victims were Russians or French residents of Russian descent. The motives for the killings were not known. </P>
<P> The sources said the house owners were among those killed. Their teen-age son found the bodies when he came home at 3 a.m. </P>
<P> Four of the bodies were in the living room, another in a corridor and the sixth upstairs. </P>
<P> A 3-year-old girl found asleep upstairs was taken to the hospital for checks. </P>
