<TITLE> Clinton to host Japan's prime minister next month</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Clinton to host Japan's prime minister next month</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CLINTON-JAPAN </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  President Clinton will host Japanese Prime Minister Tomiichi Murayama Jan. 11 for a working visit that will deal with trade and other issues, White House Press Secretary Dee Dee Myers said Thursday. </P>
<P> Myers said the two leaders will discuss a ``variety of bilateral issues,'' including such economic matters as the sale of auto parts, financial services and deregulation. </P>
