<TITLE> Two pilots freed in Russian hijack drama</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Two pilots freed in Russian hijack drama</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-RUSSIA-HIJACK-RELEASE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>LONDON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The hijacker of a Russian aircraft in the southern republic of Dagestan has released the two pilots of a Yak-40 plane, Itar-Tass news agency reported Thursday. </P>
<P> Tass, monitored by the British Broadcasting Corporation, said security forces had begun implementing Operation Nabat (Alarm Bell), which includes storming the plane, at Makhachkala Airport. </P>
