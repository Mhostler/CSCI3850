<TITLE> DEATH TOLL RISES TO 12 IN ARGENTINE BLAST-OFFICIAL</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> DEATH TOLL RISES TO 12 IN ARGENTINE BLAST-OFFICIAL</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-ARGENTINA-EXPLOSION-TOLL 2NDLD </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES> (Eds: updates death toll)</NOTES>
<DATELINE>BUENOS AIRES (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  At least 12 people died in a presumed bomb blast Monday at the offices of Argentina's main Jewish organization, an Argentine official said. </P>
<P> The official, Federal Judge Juan Jose Galeano, who is in charge of investigating the blast, told reporters he did not know how many people were injured. He spoke at his offices after visiting the site of the explosion. </P>
