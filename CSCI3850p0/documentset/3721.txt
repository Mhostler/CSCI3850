<TITLE> Colombia plane crashes with 52 aboard - official</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Colombia plane crashes with 52 aboard - official</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-COLOMBIA-PLANE 1STLD </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>BOGOTA, Colombia (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P> (Changes number on board)  A passenger DC9 jet airliner on a domestic flight crashed in northern Colombia Wednesday evening with 47 passengers and five crew aboard, an air traffic control official told Reuters. </P>
<P> There was no immediate word on the fate of the passengers. </P>
