<TITLE> Clinton takes on NRA in speech to police</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Clinton takes on NRA in speech to police</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-BLAST-CLINTON-NRA </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  President Clinton took on the National Rifle Association Monday in a speech commending former President George Bush's criticism of the NRA and saying no one has a right to ``run down'' federal law officers. </P>
<P> ``Law enforcement officers in this country deserve our respect and support. No one has the right to run them down or to suggest that somehow it is all right for them to be put in harm's way,'' Clinton said in a speech to police officers in Washington. </P>
