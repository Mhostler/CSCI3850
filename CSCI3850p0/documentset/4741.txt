<TITLE> Bomb kills SLA soldier in south Lebanon</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Bomb kills SLA soldier in south Lebanon</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-LEBANON-SOUTH </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>MARJAYOUN, Lebanon (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  One South Lebanon Army (SLA) militiaman was killed and two were wounded when a roadside bomb exploded in Israel's South Lebanon occupation zone Monday, SLA security sources said. </P>
<P> The sources said the bomb exploded near an SLA post in the village of Houla. No group claimed responsibility for the attack. </P>
<P> Muslim guerrillas, seeking to oust the Israelies from Lebanon, have been attacking the SLA which helps Israel defend the occupation zone. </P>
