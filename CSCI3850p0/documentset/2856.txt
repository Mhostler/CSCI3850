<TITLE> Russia could withdraw Bosnia peacekeepers-Grachev</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Russia could withdraw Bosnia peacekeepers-Grachev</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CIS-YUGOSLAVIA-RUSSIA </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>MOSCOW (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Defense Minister Pavel Grachev said Monday that Russia may consider withdrawing its peacekeepers from former Yugoslavia, where they are part of the U.N. force, Interfax news agency reported. </P>
<P> The agency said Grachev accused ``a certain country'' of unilateral actions over Bosnia -- a clear reference to the United States. ``If things go on like this, the question of withdrawing Russian ``blue helmets' from former Yugoslavia would go from words to deeds,'' he was quoted as telling reporters. </P>
