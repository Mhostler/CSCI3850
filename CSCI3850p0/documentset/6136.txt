<TITLE> China's Deng reported ill, but out of danger</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> China's Deng reported ill, but out of danger</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CHINA-DENG-HONGKONG </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>HONG KONG, April 29 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  China's aged paramount leader Deng Xiaoping was ill with a high temperature earlier this week but was now out of danger, the South China Morning Post newspaper said on Saturday. </P>
<P> Speculation about the frail 90-year-old leader's health intensified this week after reports that his children were staying close to home. </P>
<P> The Post quoting unnamed sources as saying Deng's condition had been poor but had now stabilised. </P>
<P> He was still able to walk with assistance but had trouble speaking, it said. </P>
