<TITLE> Bomb goes off in Iranian mosque, agency says</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Bomb goes off in Iranian mosque, agency says</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-IRAN-BOMB </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>NICOSIA, Cyprus (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A bomb went off in a mosque in a suburb east of Tehran Saturday night, shattering windows and sending people from nearby houses rushing outside, the Iranian news agency IRNA said. There were no casualties. </P>
<P> IRNA said the bomb was planted in a library staircase of the Al Nabi mosque in Narmak, east of Tehran. Police told IRNA a person accused of planting the device was arrested. </P>
<P> Iran marked the 16th anniversary of its Islamic revolution Saturday. </P>
