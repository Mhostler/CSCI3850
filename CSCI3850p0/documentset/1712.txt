<P> At least two other people were believed hit by gunfire which broke out at the office of the main para-military group, FRAPH. </P>
<P> The gunfire broke out when hundreds of demonstrators marking the third anniversary of the 1991 military coup that overthrew President Jean-Bertrand Aristide gathered outside FRAPH headquarters to protest. </P>
<P> The gunfire appeared to come from both sides. One reporter said he saw an Aristide supporter shooting at FRAPH headquarters. </P>
