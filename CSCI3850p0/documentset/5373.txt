<TITLE> Austria names new finance minister in reshuffle</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Austria names new finance minister in reshuffle</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-AUSTRIA-FINANCE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>VIENNA, April 1 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Austria named a 38-year-old chartered accountant Andreas Staribcher as finance minister on Saturday, succeeding Ferdinand Lacina, who announced his resignation earlier this week. </P>
<P> The appointment was announced together with replacements for the social, women's affairs and interior ministers, who all resigned on Friday. </P>
