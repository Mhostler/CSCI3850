<TITLE> CHRISTOPHER IN EGYPT TO SEE MUBARAK</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> CHRISTOPHER IN EGYPT TO SEE MUBARAK</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-MIDEAST-CHRISTOPHER-ARRIVAL </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>ALEXANDRIA, Egypt (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Secretary of State Warren Christopher arrived in the Egyptian city of Alexandria Saturday at the start of a Middle East tour designed to bring about progress toward peace between Israel and Syria. </P>
<P> He came first to Egypt to brief President Hosni Mubarak, who has also been trying to speed up Israeli-Syrian peace talks. </P>
<P> On the flight from Washington, a senior U.S. official said Israel and Syria would never make peace without returning to direct negotiations but for now the U.S. role was critical. </P>
<P> The official said ``wide gaps'' remained in their positions and it was unclear how soon peace might be achieved. </P>
<P> PLO leader Yasser Arafat is expected in Alexandria later Saturday morning, also to see Mubarak. </P>
