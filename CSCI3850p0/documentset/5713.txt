<TITLE>  Kabul reports 100 killed by Russian bombing</TITLE>
<HEADLINE>  Kabul reports 100 killed by Russian bombing</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-TAJIKISTAN-BOMBING URGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>ISLAMABAD (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Russian jets based in Tajikistan heavily bombed the northern Afghan town of Taloqan Thursday, killing 100 people and wounding 200, the official Kabul Radio said. </P>
<P> The bombing of the town, which is the capital of Takhar province bordering Tajikistan, also destroyed many houses and shops, said the broadcast monitored in Islamabad. </P>
