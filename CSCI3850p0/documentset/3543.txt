<TITLE> British police on drugs raid stuck in elevator</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> British police on drugs raid stuck in elevator</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-BRITAIN-POLICE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>LONDON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Nine British police officers on a narcotics raid failed to reach their target after they squeezed into an elevator made for eight -- and it ground to a halt. </P>
<P> The police were trapped for 45 minutes in the elevator in Coventry in central England before their cries for help were heard, the Sun newspaper reported Tuesday. </P>
<P> ``I told them I would get the police and they shouted: ``We are the bloody police -- get the fire brigade,'' said Eddie Laidle, a resident of the apartment building where the police were trapped. </P>
