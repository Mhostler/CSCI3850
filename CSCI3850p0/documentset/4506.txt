<TITLE> Algiers prison mutiny official toll 100 dead</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Algiers prison mutiny official toll 100 dead</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-ALGERIA-MUTINY-TOLL </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>TUNIS, Tunisia (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  One hundred detainees and prison wardens died in an Algiers prison mutiny that was crushed Wednesday, Algerian state-run television said Thursday night. </P>
<P> An official statement quoted by the television said there were 96 dead among the prisoners, 81 of them Muslim fundamentalists charged with ``terrorist'' acts, and 15 of them common criminals. </P>
<P> It said four prison warders were killed by the mutineers. </P>
<P> There were also 12 wounded, two of them prison warders and 10 detainees it added. </P>
