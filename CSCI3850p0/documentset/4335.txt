<TITLE> U.S. January retail sales rose modestly</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> U.S. January retail sales rose modestly</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-USA-ECONOMY-SALES </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Sales at retail stores rose modestly in January, the Commerce Department said Tuesday, an eighth straight monthly rise despite a dip in demand for new cars. </P>
<P> Total retail sales were up 0.2 percent to a seasonally adjusted $193.2 billion after a sharply revised 0.2 percent gain in December that previously was reported as a 0.1 percent decline. </P>
