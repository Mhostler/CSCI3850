<TITLE> British police hold Israeli embassy bomb suspects</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> British police hold Israeli embassy bomb suspects</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-BRITAIN-ARRESTS </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>LONDON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  British police have detained five people in connection with bomb attacks on the Israeli embassy and a Jewish charity office last July, the Press Association news agency said Tuesday. </P>
<P> It said the five were being detained under the Prevention of Terrorism Act. </P>
<P> A car bomb explosion outside the Israeli embassy in London last July 26 injured 14 people. </P>
<P> The second bomb exploded the following night outside a north London building used by several Israeli and Jewish organisations, casuing minor injuries to five people. </P>
<P> Police had no immediate comment. </P>
