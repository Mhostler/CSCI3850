<TITLE> Britain to make N.Ireland troop cuts-sources</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Britain to make N.Ireland troop cuts-sources</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-IRISH-TROOPS </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>LONDON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Britain will announce a reduction in the number of its troops on duty in Northern Ireland later Tuesday, British government sources said. </P>
<P> The government has been steadily lowering the profile of security forces on the streets of the British-ruled province since the Irish Republican Army and Protestant guerrilla groups declared cease-fires six months ago. </P>
