<TITLE> Lennon lyrics, McCartney guitar sold at auction</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Lennon lyrics, McCartney guitar sold at auction</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-WALKER..BRITAIN-BEATLES </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>LONDON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A bass guitar signed by Paul McCartney and unpublished lyrics by another one-time member of the Beatles pop group, the late John Lennon, fetched thousands of dollars Thursday at a top London auction house. </P>
<P> More than 2,000 negatives and contacts dating both from the Beatles' early days and the height of their fame raised nearly $23,900, 10 times the expected asking price, said Bonhams auctioneer. </P>
<P> The Lennon lyrics fetched more than $12,700. </P>
<P> A hand-drawn design by McCartney sold for just over $3,180, while the guitar carrying his signature went for $9,145. </P>
<P> The sale was part of a three-day auction of rock and pop memorabilia, with more than 3,000 lots up for sale. </P>
