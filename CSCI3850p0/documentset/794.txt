<TITLE> HIZBOLLAH FIGHTERS KILL THREE PRO-ISRAELI GUNMEN</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> HIZBOLLAH FIGHTERS KILL THREE PRO-ISRAELI GUNMEN</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-MIDEAST-LEBANON-SOUTH 1STLD </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES>(Eds: Adds three SLA killed</NOTES>
<DATELINE>MARJAYOUN, Lebanon (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Pro-Iranian Hizbollah guerrillas ambushed a convoy of Israeli-backed militiamen in south Lebanon Tuesday, killing three militiamen and wounding two, security sources said. </P>
<P> They said guerrillas fired machine guns and anti-tank rockets at the convoy as it drove near the Zemraya crossing point into Israel's occupation zone in the south. </P>
<P> Hizbollah (Party of God) said in a statement in Beirut its guerrillas attacked a convoy of two cars and a jeep in Zemraya, killing and wounding all of its members. </P>
<P> Israeli and SLA forces retaliated to the attack, shelling Hizbollah-held area in the southern edge of the eastern Bekaa Valley. </P>
<P> Earlier Tuesday, Hizbollah guerrillas attacked the SLA position in Toumat Niha with machineguns and anti-tank rockets and fired mortar bombs at the Israeli army position of Dalafeh on the edge of the zone, the sources said. </P>
<P> Israeli and SLA gunners returned fire, shelling a guerrilla-held area to the north. There were no reports of casualties in the earlier exchanges. </P>
<P> Israeli warplanes on Monday bombed a hill used by Hizbollah guerrillas to attack Israel's self-styled buffer strip. </P>
