<TITLE> Japan officials cut estimate of quake injured</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Japan officials cut estimate of quake injured</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-QUAKE-JAPAN-INJURED </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>TOKYO (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Japanese officials sharply reduced their estimate of the injured in Tuesday's massive earthquake to 475 from an earlier figure of 13,085. </P>
<P> In a sign of the chaos and confusion surrounding the worst earthquake to hit Japan in nearly half a century, a Fire Defense Agency spokesman revised the number of injured people to 475 from 13,085. </P>
