<TITLE> South China bus crash kills 28, 32 seriously hurt</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> South China bus crash kills 28, 32 seriously hurt</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CHINA-CRASH </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>BEIJING, April 10 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A bus racing down a highway in southern China plunged more than 100 metres (yards) into a ravine, killing 28 people and seriously injuring 32, the Wen Hui newspaper said on Monday. </P>
<P> The military-licensed bus shot off a section of road in Qingxin county of southern Guangdong province that has been the scene of several serious accidents recently, the newspaper said. </P>
<P> It said 26 people died immediately and another two in hospital. Three of the 32 seriously hurt were in critical condition. The bus was carrying 61 passengers. </P>
<P> The Yangcheng Evening News said the driver was tired and lost control of the vehicle. </P>
<P> More than 80 people have been killed and 200 injured in accidents along the same section of the road in the last three months, the newspaper said. </P>
