<TITLE> Russian dies in clash near Chinese border-agency</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Russian dies in clash near Chinese border-agency</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CIS-RUSSIA-BORDER </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>MOSCOW, April 12 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A Russian border guard officer was shot dead on Wednesday during a clash with a gang of armed men near Russia's far eastern frontier with China, Itar-Tass news agency said. </P>
<P> It said a patrol had come across the gang and was trying to detain them when they opened fire, killing the commander of the local border post and wounding other soldiers. The gang was eventually arrested. </P>
<P> The agency, quoting Russian Far Eastern border guard commander Vitaly Sedykh, gave no other details. </P>
