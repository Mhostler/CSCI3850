<TITLE> Arafat, Rabin to get Nobel Peace prize-newspaper</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Arafat, Rabin to get Nobel Peace prize-newspaper</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-NOBEL-PEACE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>OSLO, Norway (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Israeli Prime Minister Yitzhak Rabin and PLO chairman Yasser Arafat will share the 1994 Nobel Peace Prize, but the decision has caused a row within the committee that awards it, the Norwegian daily Aftenposten said Tuesday. </P>
<P> The newspaper said one pro-Israeli member of the five-member Nobel committee would step down in protest when the prize is announced Friday because he was against making Arafat a Nobel laureate. </P>
