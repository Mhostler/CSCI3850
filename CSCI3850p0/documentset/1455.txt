<TITLE> Perry says Haiti leaders to leave country</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Perry says Haiti leaders to leave country</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-HAITI-USA-PERRY </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Defense Secretary William Perry said Sunday that Haiti's military leaders will have to leave the country when they step down. </P>
<P> ``They have to step down from their present positions. There is no practical way they can stay in the country once that happens, so they will be leaving the country'', he said on ABC television's ``This Week With David Brinkley.'' ``The modality of leaving is under discussion as we speak.'' </P>
