<TITLE> Quake jolts parts of northern Japan</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Quake jolts parts of northern Japan</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-QUAKE-JAPAN </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>TOKYO (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  An earthquake measuring 6.0 on the open-ended Richter scale jolted parts of northern Japan at 8:12 p.m. (6:12 a.m. EST) Tuesday, but there were no immediate reports of casualties or damage. </P>
<P> The Meteorological Agency said there was no danger of ``tsunami'' or tidal waves following the quake. </P>
<P> It said the quake's epicenter was about 25 miles under the sea near Kunashiri, one of four Soviet-held islands about 550 miles north of Tokyo. </P>
