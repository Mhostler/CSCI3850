<TITLE> CBS says carter still seeking final agreement</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> CBS says carter still seeking final agreement</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-HAITI-CBS </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>NEW YORK (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  CBS television news reported from Port-au-Prince on Sunday evening that no final arrangement had been hammered out for a peaceful resolution of the Haiti crisis. </P>
<P> CBS television's Dan Rather reported that this information had come from a source who had been in the room during negotiations between U.S. emissary Jimmy Carter and Haiti's military leader Raoul Cedras. Rather said Carter was still trying to get a final agreement. </P>
