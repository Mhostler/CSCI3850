<TITLE> Clinton names new US envoy to United Arab Emirates</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Clinton names new US envoy to United Arab Emirates</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CLINTON-EMIRATES </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  President Clinton Monday announced the nomination of David Litt, a career foreign service officer, as the new U.S. ambassador to the United Arab Emirates. </P>
<P> The White House said Litt, who speaks both Arabic and Persian, had devoted most of his 21-year career to the Middle East, and served as consul general in Dubai, United Arab Emirates, from 1985-1988. </P>
<P> He has also served in Saudi Arabia, Syria, Afghanistan, Italy and Niger, and is currently director of the Office of Northern Gulf Affairs, overseeing Iran and Iraq. </P>
