<TITLE> TWO SHELLS HIT AIRPORT AT CROATIAN RESORT</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> TWO SHELLS HIT AIRPORT AT CROATIAN RESORT</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-YUGOSLAVIA-DUBROVNIK </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>ZAGREB (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Two artillery shells hit the airport at Dubrovnik on the Croatian coast early Thursday, state police officials said. </P>
<P> The Cilipi airport later reopened after two artillery rounds landed close to the runway at 6:12 a.m. local time (12:12 am EDT), Zdravko Zidovec of the ministry of internal affairs in Zagreb told Reuters. </P>
