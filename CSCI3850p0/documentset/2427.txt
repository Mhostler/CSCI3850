<TITLE> Turkish PM meets Arafat in Gaza</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Turkish PM meets Arafat in Gaza</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-MIDEAST-TURKEY 1STLD </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES> (Eds; Adds Ciller's promise to finance Palestinian projects)</NOTES>
<DATELINE>GAZA (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Turkish Prime Minister Tansu Ciller Saturday became the first head of government to visit the Palestinian self-rule areas, meeting PLO leader Yasser Arafat in Gaza. </P>
<P> She promised to help finance Palestinian self-rule. </P>
<P> ``Turkey will start housing projects immediately. This is Turkey's contribution. We are with you,'' she said at a news conference after the meeting. </P>
<P> Arafat said Turkey would also help build a harbor and an airport in Gaza and contribute to other projects. </P>
<P> A red carpet was rolled out at the entrance to Arafat's office as Ciller arrived in pouring rain. The PLO leader greeted her outside while security officials held umbrellas over the two leaders. </P>
<P> Ciller is on an official visit to Israel. </P>
<P> Earlier Saturday, she visited holy sites in East Jerusalem including the Al-Aqsa Mosque, Islam's third holiest shrine, and the Dome of the Rock. </P>
