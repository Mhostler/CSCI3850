<TITLE> UN chief approves takeover in Haiti March 31</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> UN chief approves takeover in Haiti March 31</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-HAITI-UN </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>UNITED NATIONS (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Secretary-General Boutros Boutros-Ghali, in a report to the Security Council, recommended the United Nations takeover the Haiti operation from the United States on or around March 31. </P>
<P> ``I expect that UNMIH will be able to take over on or around 31 March 1995,'' he said in the report obtained by Reuters late Tuesday. </P>
