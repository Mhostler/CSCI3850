<TITLE> S.Korea minister says formal power transfer likely</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> S.Korea minister says formal power transfer likely</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-KOREA-KIM </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>SEOUL, South Korea (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  South Korean Unification Minister Rha Woong-bae predicted Monday North Korea's Kim Jong-il may formally take over the national leadership from his late father Kim Il-sung in July or August. </P>
<P> Rha said in a report submitted to parliament that July and August ``drew special attention with regards to succession of power in the North.'' </P>
<P> Rha noted that July 8 is the first anniversary of Kim Il-sung's death and Aug. 15 the 50th anniversary of Korea's liberation from Japanese colonial rule. </P>
<P> Kim Jong-il has been hailed by the North Korean media as his father's successor but has yet to be officially elevated to the key posts he held -- state president and general secretary of the all-powerful communist party. </P>
