<SUBJECT> BC-CIS-RUSSIA-CRASH -2 MOSCOW </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE></DATELINE>
<P> The spokesman said the civilian aircraft crashed 25 miles from Vanavara. Two rescue helicopters were dispatched to the site and found the plane was completely destroyed. </P>
<P> This was the fourth plane crash in Siberia this year. In August, 47 people were killed when an Antonov-12 military transport crashed while coming into land near the Chinese border. </P>
<P> On January 3, more than 120 people died when a Tupolev-154 belonging to a domestic airline plunged into farmland near Irkutsk in central Siberia. </P>
<P> On March 23, an Airbus A-310, on a flight from Moscow to Hong Kong, crashed into a wooded hillside near Novokuznetsk in western Siberia, killing 70 people. </P>
