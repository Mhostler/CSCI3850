<TITLE> CHER RETURNS TO BIG SCREEN AFTER FOUR YEARS</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> CHER RETURNS TO BIG SCREEN AFTER FOUR YEARS</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-USA-CHER </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>LOS ANGELES (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Academy Award-winning singer-actress Cher is at work on her first film in four years, a comedy-thriller titled ``Faithful,'' in which she stars with Ryan O'Neal and Chazz Palminteri, a production company spokesman said Tuesday. </P>
<P> Frank Lomento, spokesman for Savoy Pictures, said principal photography was under way in New York City under the direction of Paul Mazursky, who directed the hits, ``Bob & Carol & Ted & Alice,'' and ``Down and Out in Beverly Hills.'' </P>
<P> Cher, 48, who won an Oscar for her role in ``Moonstruck,'' last appeared on the big screen in 1990 in ``Mermaids.'' </P>
<P> In ``Faithful,'' she plays a woman who is held captive in her home by an intruder, played by Palminteri. But she turns the table on him in a tale of deceit and seduction. </P>
<P> The film is scheduled for release next summer. </P>
