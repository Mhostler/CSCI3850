<TITLE> Senate approves wording change to amendment</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Senate approves wording change to amendment</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-BUDGET-CHANGE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The Senate passed Sen. Sam Nunn's change to the balanced budget constitutional amendment Tuesday by a vote of 92-8. </P>
<P> Nunn's change bars federal courts from doing anything to enforce the balanced budget amendment if Congress violates it unless Congress agrees to surrender authority to the courts. </P>
