<TITLE> NATO planes fly over Bihac but find no targets</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> NATO planes fly over Bihac but find no targets</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-YUGOSLAVIA-UN-ANNAN 1STLD </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES>(Eds: NATO air mission called off, planes return safely)</NOTES>
<DATELINE>UNITED NATIONS (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  NATO aircraft flew over Bihac Friday but did not locate any targets and the request for close support was cancelled, U.N. Under Secretary-General Kofi Annan told reporters. </P>
<P> The planes were shot at by two or three SAM-6 missiles but were not hit and all the aircraft returned to base, he added. </P>
