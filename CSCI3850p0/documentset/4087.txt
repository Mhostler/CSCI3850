<TITLE> Norwest raises prime rate half-point to 9 percent</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Norwest raises prime rate half-point to 9 percent</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-ECONOMY-RATES-PRIME URGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>NEW YORK (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Following quickly on the Federal Reserve's hike in short-term interest rates Wednesday, Norwest Corp. raised its prime rate by half a percentage point to 9 percent. </P>
<P> The move came after the Fed announced a long-anticipated move to boost interest rates as part of its quest to slow, and thus prolong, the economic expansion. The Minneapolis-based Norwest's move was expected to be followed by other banks. </P>
