<TITLE> Japan governor rejects entry for nuclear ship</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Japan governor rejects entry for nuclear ship</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-NUCLEAR-SHIP-REFUSAL  </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>MUTSU OGAWARA, Japan, April 25 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The governor of northern Japan's Aomori prefecture on Tuesday refused to allow the entry of a British nuclear ship carrying radioactive waste which arrived off Aomori port after a two-month voyage from France. </P>
<P> ``We cannot accept this vague explanation from the government (on future nuclear storage),'' governor Morio Kimura told a news conference. ``I have decided not to allow the ship to enter port.'' </P>
