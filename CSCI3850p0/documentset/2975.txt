<TITLE> At least 20 killed in Hungarian train crash</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> At least 20 killed in Hungarian train crash</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-HUNGARY-TRAIN 1STLD </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES>(Eds: Updates death toll)</NOTES>
<DATELINE>SZAJOL, Hungary (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  At least 20 people died in a major train accident in central Hungary when an express train derailed and smashed into buildings at a provincial railway station, police said Friday. </P>
<P> ``At least 20 people have died,'' police spokesman Laszlo Garamvolgyi told journalists at the scene of the accident. </P>
<P> Rescue workers carrying bodies from one of the buildings were overheard saying that at least 21 bodies had been found. </P>
<P> The express, from Nyiregyhaza near the Hungarian-Romanian border to Budapest, was traveling through the station when five of its cars derailed. </P>
<P> One car slammed into a three-story hostel for railway workers, bringing the upper floors and roof down. Two other cars cut into the station's waiting room with one of the cars was crushed to about a third of its normal length. </P>
<P> Szajol is 75 miles east of Budapest. </P>
