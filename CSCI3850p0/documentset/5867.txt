<P> Reno told reporters at a White House briefing that federal officials were told 550 people were assigned to the government building that was bombed and that only 250 of them had been accounted for. </P>
<P> ``There may be as many as 100 to 250 people to account for,'' she said. ``The casualty figures are climbing.'' </P>
<P> Meanwhile, an official with the Oklahoma City Ambulance Service said he has been told to expect as many as 80 bodies to be brought to a morgue set up 10 blocks from the gutted building. </P>
<P> Paul Homan, communications supervisor with the Oklahoma City Ambulance Service, said that about 800 people normally work in the Alfred Murrah building.. </P>
<P> He said he did not know how many workers were in the building at the time of the explosion, but that so far only 150 have been accounted for. He said a lot of people left the scene and so officials were unable to get a proper count. </P>
