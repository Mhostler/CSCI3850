<TITLE> Agassi to lead off Davis Cup for U.S. against Italy</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Agassi to lead off Davis Cup for U.S. against Italy</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-DAVIS-DRAW </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>PALERMO, Sicily (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  World No. 2 Andre Agassi will open for the United States against Italian Andrea Gaudenzi Friday in their Davis Cup tennis quarterfinal tie. </P>
<P> Pete Sampras, the world's top-ranked player who was beaten by Agassi in the Key Biscayne final Sunday, will take on Renzo Furlan in the second singles. </P>
<P> In Saturday's doubles, Richey Reneberg and Jared Palmer, the Australian Open doubles champions, meet Stefano Pescosolido and Cristian Brandi. The reverse singles are set for Sunday. </P>
