<TITLE> Seven hurt in Hague bomb explosion-police</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Seven hurt in Hague bomb explosion-police</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-DUTCH-EXPLOSION </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>THE HAGUE, Netherlands (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Seven people were slightly injured after a bomb exploded in mid-afternoon Friday at a downtown shopping mall in The Hague, police said. </P>
<P> It was not yet clear whether the bomb, placed in a metal litter bin, was a professionally manufactured device or made from fireworks, a police spokeswoman said. </P>
<P> ``The bomb exploded at 2:22 p.m. (9:22 a.m. EDT). A technical investigation has started,'' she said, adding no one had claimed responsibilty for the explosion. </P>
<P> The injured were taken to local hospitals. Several windows were shattered by the explosion. </P>
