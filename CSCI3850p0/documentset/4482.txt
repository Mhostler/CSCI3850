<TITLE> Libya asks U.N. to probe rights in U.S.</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Libya asks U.N. to probe rights in U.S.</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-LIBYA-USA </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>TUNIS, Tunisia (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Libya said Wednesday it had asked the United Nations to probe ``grave human rights violations'' in the United States. </P>
<P> The official Libyan news agency JANA said Libya's representative made the demand in a letter to U.N. Secretary-General Boutros Boutros-Ghali. It did not elaborate. </P>
<P> JANA this month rejected U.S. State Department charges of human rights violations in Libya. </P>
