<TITLE> Peru, Ecuador agree on ceasefire in border dispute</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Peru, Ecuador agree on ceasefire in border dispute</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-ECUADOR-PERU-CEASEFIRE URGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>RIO DE JANEIRO (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Delegations from Peru and Ecuador attending peace talks in Rio de Janeiro announced Wednesday a ceasefire to their armed border dispute but the end to hostilities will take time to put into effect, a Brazilian official said. </P>
<P> ``The ceasefire has been officially announced by the two sides...but between the announcement and the implementation of the ceasefire there are a series of steps that have to be taken,'' Brazil's Deputy Foreign Minister Sebastiao do Rego Barros said. </P>
