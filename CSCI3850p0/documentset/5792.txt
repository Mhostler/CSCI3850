<TITLE> Chinese woman severs husband's penis, kills self</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Chinese woman severs husband's penis, kills self</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CHINA-SUICIDE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>BEIJING, April 17 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A Chinese woman cut off her husband's penis because he was addicted to gambling and then killed herself, a Tianjin newspaper said in an edition seen in Beijing on Monday. </P>
<P> The woman, identified only as Zhang, took her life by drinking pesticide in her home in mountainous Xinyang county of central Henan province, the Today Evening News said. </P>
<P> The mutilation and suicide occurred in February, but the newspaper gave no reason for the delay in reporting them. </P>
<P> Zhang committed suicide after cutting off her husband's penis while he was sleeping, the newspaper said. </P>
<P> Hu Changfa, a 27-year-old farmer, had lost the family's fortune and run up a gambling debt of more than 10,000 yuan ($1,187) -- 30 times China's average monthly salary. </P>
