<P> The comments about a possible candidacy were the strongest yet by Lugar, a leading voice in the Senate on foreign affairs and agricultural policy. </P>
<P> ``I believe the United States has to lead the world and the president has to be the personification of that leadership,'' Lugar said, citing the need to work with other nations to control nuclear weaponry as a step toward global security. </P>
<P> ``Only the president can offer the vigorous leadership which is required to forge these alliances, to forge the world-wide circumstances in which Americans feel a sense of safety and security, in which American economic interests thrive,'' he said. </P>
<P> ``I believe I'm the person who can best provide that leadership.'' </P>
