<TITLE> Police kill suspected militant in south Egypt</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Police kill suspected militant in south Egypt</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-EGYPT-MILITANT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>ASSIUT, Egypt (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Police killed a suspected Muslim militant in a raid on a house in the southern Egyptian province of Assiut, security sources said Sunday. </P>
<P> Security officials said police went to the house in Assiut city, 200 miles south of Cairo, late Saturday to arrest Mohammed Sherif al-Wazzan, 21, wanted in connection with militant attacks against policemen in the south. </P>
<P> Police opened fire in response to shooting from inside, and Wazzan was killed,the sources said. There were no police casualties. </P>
<P> Police believe Wazzan was a member of the militant group the Gama'a al-Islamiya (Islamic Group), which targets policemen as part of its campaign to overthrow the Egyptian government and set up a strict Islamic state. </P>
<P> More than 500 people, mainly militants and policemen, have been killed in political violence in Egypt since 1992. </P>
