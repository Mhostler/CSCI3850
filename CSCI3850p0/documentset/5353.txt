<TITLE> Kozyrev leaves Lebanon after five-hour visit</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Kozyrev leaves Lebanon after five-hour visit</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-MIDEAST-KOZYREV-DEPARTURE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>BEIRUT, Lebanon (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Russian Foreign Minister Andrei Kozyrev Friday left Beirut for Israel after a five-hour visit during which he held talks with President Elias Hrawi and Foreign Minister Faris Bouez. </P>
<P> Israel and the Palestinian-ruled Gaza Strip are the last stops on Kozyrev's five-stop tour to bolster the Middle East peace process. </P>
