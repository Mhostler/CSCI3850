<TITLE> JAPAN EMPEROR TO VISIT FRANCE AND SPAIN IN OCTOBER</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> JAPAN EMPEROR TO VISIT FRANCE AND SPAIN IN OCTOBER</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-JAPAN-EMPEROR </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>TOKYO (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Japan's Emperor Akihito and Empress Michiko will go on a 13-day tour of France and Spain in October, business daily Nihon Keizai Shimbun said Saturday. </P>
<P> It said they will stay overnight in Frankfurt on October 2 prior to the start of the visit, before going to Paris for an official reception with President Francois Mitterrand. </P>
<P> They will then travel south to Toulouse, followed by a visit to Spain taking in King Juan Carlos's palace in Majorca and stays in Madrid and Barcelona. </P>
<P> In June they went on a 16-day tour of the United States, and last September visited Germany, Italy, the Vatican and Belgium. </P>
