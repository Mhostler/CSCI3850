<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES>:</NOTES>
<DATELINE></DATELINE>
<P> The dead included three officers and at least seven commandos were missing, Special Task Force Commander Lionel Karunasena said. </P>
<P> The attack was five miles (eight kilometres) southwest of Amparai. Liberation Tigers of Tamil Eelam members surrounded the commandos who were on a rebel flushing-out operation called Golden Waters Phase Three, police sources said. </P>
<P> At least 200 to 300 commandos were involved in the operation, which was continuing, source said. </P>
<P> The LTTE are fighting for a separate homeland in the north and east of the country in a war which has killed more than 30,000 people since 1983. </P>
