<TITLE> Palestinian killed by Israeli army flare</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Palestinian killed by Israeli army flare</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-MIDEAST-FLARE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>JERUSALEM (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A Palestinian man was killed in his sleep when Israeli soldiers fired flares at his home in the occupied West Bank, relatives said. </P>
<P> They said a flare crushed Ghazi Ahmed Qabb's head and set fire to his house in Deir al-Ghusoun village, near the West Bank town of Tulkarm. The 30-year-old man's wife suffered some burns, but his two children were rescued unharmed. </P>
<P> An Israeli army spokeswoman said she was checking the report. Palestinians said the army arrived to investigate the incident and took Qabb's body for autopsy. </P>
<P> Israeli soldiers on night patrol in occupied territory use flares to illuminate rural areas. </P>
