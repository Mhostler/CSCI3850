<TITLE> Summary of G7 conclusions</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Summary of G7 conclusions</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-GROUP-CONCLUSIONS </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>BRUSSELS, Belgium (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The following is a summary of the principles agreed to by ministers of the Group of Seven (G7) industrialized nations Sunday at their conference on the information society. </P>
<P> ``The smooth and effective transition towards the information society is one of the most important tasks that should be undertaken in the last decade of the 20h century ... </P>
<P> ``Our action must contribute to the integration of all countries into a global effort. </P>
<P> ``To succeed, governments must facilitate private initiatives and investments and ensure an appropriate framework aiming at stimulating private investment and usage for the benefit of all citizens.'' </P>
<P></P>
<P></P>
<P> - Recognizing the necessity of worldwide cooperation with particular attention to less developed countries. </P>
