<TITLE> Hizbollah bomb attack in south Lebanon</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Hizbollah bomb attack in south Lebanon</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-LEBANON-SOUTH </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>MARJAYOUN, Lebanon (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A Lebanese civilian was killed and one was wounded in Israel's south Lebanon occupation zone Saturday when a roadside bomb blew up their car, pro-Israeli security sources said. </P>
<P> In Beirut, the pro-Iranian Hizbollah (Party of God) whose guerrillas are fighting Israeli troops and their South Lebanon Army (SLA) allies in the zone, said its men planted the bomb. </P>
<P> It said the explosion destroyed two cars in an SLA security convoy, killing four people and injuring three, including an SLA official named Ali al-Khalil. </P>
