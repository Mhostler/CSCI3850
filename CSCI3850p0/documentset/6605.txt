<TITLE> Pakistani airliner under bomb threat at Kennedy</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Pakistani airliner under bomb threat at Kennedy</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-PLANE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>NEW YORK (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A Pakistan International Airlines plane was detained at Kennedy Airport Tuesday while police searched for a possible bomb on board, authorities said. </P>
<P> PIA flight 703, which originated in Karachi, landed safely just after 4 p.m. EDT with 218 people on board and was taken to a remote location because of a bomb threat, said D. Joy Faber of the Port Authority police. </P>
<P> The plane, a 747, would be searched once all the passengers disembarked, she said. They were still on board about 30 minutes after the plane landed, she said. </P>
<P> It was not immediatly clear how the bomb threat had been made. </P>
