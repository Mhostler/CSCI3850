<TITLE> U.S. worried about safety of Nigerian airlines</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> U.S. worried about safety of Nigerian airlines</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-NIGERIA-USA </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES>(Also moved to business news desks)</NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The United States said Saturday it was concerned by reports from Lagos that the maintenance and operational procedures for Nigerian airlines may not be adequate to ensure safety. </P>
<P> The U.S. State Department said in a statement that periodic strikes may also affect flight scheduling and raise additonal concerns about the reliability and saftey of Nigerian aviation. </P>
<P> The department said the U.S. Embassy in Lagos was discussing those concerns with the Nigerian government. </P>
