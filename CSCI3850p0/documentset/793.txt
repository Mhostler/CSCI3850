<TITLE> GUERRILLAS KILL THREE ISRAELI-BACKED MILITIAMEN</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> GUERRILLAS KILL THREE ISRAELI-BACKED MILITIAMEN</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-MIDEAST-LEBANON-SLA </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>MARJAYOUN, Lebanon (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Pro-Iranian Hizbollah guerrillas ambushed a convoy of Israeli-backed militiamen in south Lebanon Tuesday, killing three militiamen and wounding two, security sources said. </P>
<P> They said guerrillas fired machine guns and anti-tank rockets at the convoy as it drove near the Zemraya crossing point into Israel's occupation zone in the south. </P>
