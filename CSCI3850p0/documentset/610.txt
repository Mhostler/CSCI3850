<TITLE> MICHAEL JACKSON AND BRIDE TO PERFORM ELVIS TRIBUTE</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> MICHAEL JACKSON AND BRIDE TO PERFORM ELVIS TRIBUTE</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-USA-JACKSON </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>LOS ANGELES (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Newlyweds Michael Jackson and Lisa Marie Presley-Jackson are planning to perform together in a tribute to her father, the late Elvis Presley, in October, according to a report published Thursday. </P>
<P> Quoting unnamed sources, The Hollywood Reporter, an entertainment industry newspaper, said the concert would be performed at Elvis' Graceland estate in Memphis October 8 and would be seen on pay-per-view television. </P>
<P> The star-studded special, called ``Elvis Aaron Presley -- The Tribute'' will also feature Elton John, Willie Nelson, the Judds and rock group Bon Jovi, among others, the paper said. </P>
<P> It said the entertainers will sing Elvis tunes, and Jackson and his bride will perform a duet of at least one Elvis classic. </P>
<P> Lisa Marie announced last week that the couple were married in secret outside the United States about 11 weeks ago. </P>
