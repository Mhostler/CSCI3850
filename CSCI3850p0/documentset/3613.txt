<TITLE> U.N. members owed $1.3 b. at end of 1994</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> U.N. members owed $1.3 b. at end of 1994</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-UN-ARREARS </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>UNITED NATIONS (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  United Nations members owed the organization some $1.8 billion at the end of 1994, with Russia the top debtor closely followed by the United States. </P>
<P> Figures issued Friday showed members owed $1.3 billion for peacekeeping and $480 million in regular budget arrears. </P>
<P> Russia's $507 million debt was all for peackeeping. The United States' arrears of $469 million included $248 million in regular budget dues and $221 million for peacekeeping. </P>
<P> Other major debtors included: Ukraine ($178 million - $137 million peacekeeping, $41 million regular), South Africa ($104 million - $57 million regular, $47 million peacekeeping) and France ($83 million -- all for peacekeeping). Japan owed the United Nations $50 million, all for peacekeeping. </P>
