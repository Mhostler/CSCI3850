<TITLE> Dead Rwandan refugees washed ashore in Greece</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Dead Rwandan refugees washed ashore in Greece</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-GREECE-REFUGEES </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>ATHENS, Greece (Reuter</DATELINE>
<P>  The bodies of eight Rwandan refugees were washed ashore a Greek island Saturday and the coast guard was searching for more bodies, police said. </P>
<P> ``They are from Rwanda and were probably brought into Greek waters on a boat from Turkey. We believe it was a Turkish vessel carrying illegal refugees and we are searching for more bodies,'' a police officer in Kalymnos said. </P>
<P> The eight were found drowned off the coast of the island of Kalimnos in the southern Aegean Sea near the Turkish coast. </P>
<P> The officer said authorities were trying to determine whether the vessel sank or the refugees were thrown overboard. </P>
<P> Refugees from Pakistan, Iraq and African nations are often brought illegally into Greece on boats from Turkey. </P>
