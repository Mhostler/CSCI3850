<TITLE> Jet crashes at Paris airport, 10 dead</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Jet crashes at Paris airport, 10 dead</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-FRANCE-CRASH 2NDLD </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES>    (Updates with confirmation of deaths)</NOTES>
<DATELINE>PARIS (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A small jet crashed at a Paris airport Friday killing all 10 aboard, airport officials said. </P>
<P> The Falcon 20 crashed just after takeoff from Le Bourget airport, north of the French capital with seven passengers and three crew, the officials said. </P>
<P> Officials initially said the aircraft was a Falcon 50. </P>
<P> The engine burst into flames soon after the plane left the ground. The pilot attempted to turn the aircraft around, but it crashed onto the runway, they said. </P>
<P> The Leadair Unijet was carrying seven businessmen to Romania, one airport spokeswoman said. </P>
<P> ``They may have been Americans, but this is not confirmed,'' she added. </P>
<P> A Leadair official said the company had no details on the crash or its passengers. </P>
