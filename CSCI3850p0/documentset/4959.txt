<TITLE> Russian plane crash kills six</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Russian plane crash kills six</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-RUSSIA-PLANE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>MOSCOW (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Six crew members were killed Thursday when a light cargo plane crashed on Kamchatka peninsula in the Russian Far East, the emergencies ministry said. </P>
<P> A ministry spokesman said the Ukrainian airlines An-26 slammed into a hillside six miles outside the locality of Ossora as it tried to land. </P>
<P> Two people accompanying the cargo and a seventh crew member were treated for injuries, the spokesman said. </P>
