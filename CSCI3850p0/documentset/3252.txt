<TITLE> Republicans say Clinton's vision looks like their own</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Republicans say Clinton's vision looks like their own</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CLINTON-REPUBLICANS </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The Republicans said tax-cut proposals outlined Thursday by President Clinton looked a great deal like their own and pledged to work with him to enact new legislation, but only if his new position was based on ``real conviction.'' </P>
<P> ``If the president's new position tonight represents a real change of heart, we say: 'Welcome aboard,''' Sen. Fred Thompson, the newly elected Republican senator from Tennessee, said in a televised response to Clinton's speech. </P>
