<TITLE> U.S. sells $48 million in missiles to Seoul</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> U.S. sells $48 million in missiles to Seoul</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-KOREA-USA-MISSILES </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The Clinton administration said Friday that the United States has sold $112 million in military missiles to South Korea for potential use against enemy ships and radar sites. </P>
<P> The package includes 136 AGM-88B high-speed anti-radiation missiles (HARM), at a cost of $64 million. They are made by Texas Instruments Inc and can be launched by aircraft to seek and destroy radar transmitters on the ground. </P>
<P> The sale also includes 32 ``Harpoon'' anti-ship missiles, made by McDonnell Douglas Corp, at a cost of $48 million. </P>
