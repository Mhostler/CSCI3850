<TITLE> Mortar bomb hits U.N.-controlled Mogadishu airport</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Mortar bomb hits U.N.-controlled Mogadishu airport</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-SOMALIA-MORTAR URGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>MOGADISHU, Somalia (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A mortar bomb slammed into the runway of the U.N.-controlled airport in Mogadishu Sunday as rival Somali militias battled in the streets outside. </P>
<P> The mortar bomb appeared to be a stray round from a battle that flared between gangs of gunmen in the streets overlooking the seaside airfield. </P>
