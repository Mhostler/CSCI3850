<TITLE> U.S. Housing Secretary Cisneros will fight charges</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> U.S. Housing Secretary Cisneros will fight charges</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CISNEROS-FIGHT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Housing Secretary Henry Cisneros said Tuesday he has done no wrong and will remain on the job while an independent counsel investigates whether he lied to the FBI about money he paid to a former mistress. </P>
<P> ``I intend to stay and to fight for our nation's commitment to people who need help and to reform HUD so that it will be a positive, more effective force in America's cities and communities,'' Cisneros said at a news conference. </P>
