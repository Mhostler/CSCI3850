<TITLE> Medium earthquake shakes northern Japan</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Medium earthquake shakes northern Japan</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-QUAKE-JAPAN-HOKKAIDO </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>TOKYO (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  An earthquake measuring 6.2 on the Richter scale shook Japan's northern Hokkaido Island Saturday, the Meteorological Agency said. </P>
<P> The quake was strongest in Kushiro, an earthquake-prone port city on the Pacific coast of Hokkaido, but the focal point was far out under the ocean. There was no fear of a tidal wave, the agency said. </P>
<P> There were no immediate reports of damages or casualties. </P>
