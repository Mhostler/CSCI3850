<TITLE> MISSING ARM OF MADRID STATUE FOUND IN PLASTIC BAG</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> MISSING ARM OF MADRID STATUE FOUND IN PLASTIC BAG</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-SPAIN-STATUE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>MADRID, July 4 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The missing arm of a landmark Madrid statue, ripped off by soccer fans during wild celebrations to mark a weekend World Cup win by Spain, was found on Monday. </P>
<P> Police said a young couple found the left arm of the 212-year-old statue of the goddess Cibeles in a plastic bag in a city centre street. </P>
<P> Around 2,500 soccer fans swarmed around the statue, which dominates a fountain in the tree-lined Paseo de la Castellana, after Spain beat Switzerland on Saturday to secure a place in the World Cup quarter-finals. </P>
