<P> But Gray, speaking on the MacNeil/Lehrer News Hour, said Washington was still determined to put an end to the current regime in Haiti and reinstate exiled President Jean-Bertrand Aristide. </P>
<P> ``We're going to look at our options, we're going to work with our neighbors in the region and the United Nations to bring this cruel dictatorship to an end and to restore democracy,'' he said. </P>
<P> Gray said the United States had not so far approached other countries about taking part in any invasion of Haiti. </P>
<P> ``We have not asked our allies whether or not they would be a part of a military intervention at this point,'' he said. </P>
