<TITLE> Russian gas pipeline damaged in explosion-Interfax</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Russian gas pipeline damaged in explosion-Interfax</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-RUSSIA-FLAME-PIPELINE URGENT (REPEATING) </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>MOSCOW, April 27 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  An explosion at a major gas pipeline in northern Russia has set afire several square km (miles) of woodland, Interfax news agency quoted the Ministry for Emergency Situations as saying on Thursday. </P>
<P> Interfax said the damaged section of pipeline in the Komi Republic had already been sealed off. There were no reports of any casualties. A Japanese pilot flying over Russia had earlier reported a massive blaze rising up to 6,000 metres (four miles) high about 1,000 km (620 miles) northwest of Moscow. </P>
