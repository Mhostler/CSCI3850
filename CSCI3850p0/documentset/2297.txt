<TITLE> Leftists battle police outside rightist magazine</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Leftists battle police outside rightist magazine</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-GERMANY-CLASH </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>POTSDAM, Germany (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Leftist militants clashed with police Saturday during a protest rally against one of Germany's leading right-wing magazines. </P>
<P> Witnesses said masked protesters clad in the trademark black of Germany's militant anarchists attacked police near the editorial office of the weekly ``Junge Freiheit'' (Young Freedom). </P>
<P> The anarchists were among a crowd of several hundred demonstrators called out by a leftist anti-racism group in neighboring Berlin. </P>
<P> The magazine is run by young German nationalists who say they are promoting a conservative revival. </P>
