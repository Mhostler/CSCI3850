<TITLE> US Senate budget panel plan released</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> US Senate budget panel plan released</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-BUDGET-RELEASE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  U.S. Senate Budget Committee Chairman Pete Domenici, (R-N.M.), Tuesday released a budget proposal that would achieve a small surplus by 2002. </P>
<P> The plan would make deep cuts in Medicare and Transportation and would abolish the Department of Commerce, but would increase spending on the peacetime military. </P>
