<TITLE> Incumbent Roth wins Delaware Senate seat</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Incumbent Roth wins Delaware Senate seat</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-ELECTION-DELAWARE-WINNER URGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Republican William Roth won re-election to the Senate from Delaware Tuesday, allowing his party to hold onto a seat that had once been targeted by Democrats, U.S. television networks said. </P>
<P> CNN and ABC projected that Roth would defeat state Attorney General Charles Oberly. </P>
