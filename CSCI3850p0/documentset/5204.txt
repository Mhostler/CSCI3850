<SUBJECT> BC-TURKEY-IRAQ-REFUGEES -2 ANKARA </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES>:</NOTES>
<DATELINE></DATELINE>
<P> About 10,000 Turkish Kurdish refugees are camped at Atrush, which is outside the zone Turkish authorities have mapped out as the limit of their cross-border campaign against Kurdistan Workers Party (PKK) guerrillas. </P>
<P> The refugees are among about 13,000 people who streamed into Iraq last year, saying they fled fighting between the army and the PKK. Some say their villages were attacked by Turkish troops. </P>
<P> Ankara maintains they were forced over the border by the PKK in order to damage Turkey's image. </P>
