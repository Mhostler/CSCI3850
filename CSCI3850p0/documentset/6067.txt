<TITLE> Senate panel delays meeting to balance budget</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Senate panel delays meeting to balance budget</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-BUDGET-DELAY </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The Senate Budget Committee Wednesday abruptly delayed by one-and-one-half weeks -- until May 8 -- a meeting at which it was supposed to write a balanced budget. </P>
<P> The meeting had been set for Thursday. Senate Budget Committee Chairman Pete Domenici had planned to release his spending blueprint next week for a budget that reached balance by 2002, but has had difficulty getting backing from Republican members of his committee to make the tough sacrifices necessary for a balanced budget. </P>
