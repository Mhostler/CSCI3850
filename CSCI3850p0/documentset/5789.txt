<TITLE> Clintons attend Easter services Sunday morning</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Clintons attend Easter services Sunday morning</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CLINTON-EASTER (PICTURE) </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  President Clinton, his wife Hillary and their daughter Chelsea attended Easter services Sunday morning at the Camp David chapel, the White House said. </P>
<P> The Clintons are spending the weekend at the presidential retreat in the Maryland mountains. They will return to the White House Sunday night in time for Monday's traditional Easter egg roll on the lawn of the mansion. </P>
