<TITLE> Sri Lankan leader to choose mother as prime minister</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Sri Lankan leader to choose mother as prime minister</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-SRILANKA-PRIME MINISTER </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>COLOMBO, Sri Lanka (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Sri Lanka's president-elect Chandrika Bandaranaike Kumaratunga will choose her mother, Sirima Bandaranaike, as the next prime minister, a senior minister said Friday. </P>
<P> ``Sirima Bandaraniake will be the next prime minister of the country. She will probably take oath with the cabinet on the weekend,'' the minister, who declined to be named, told Reuters. </P>
