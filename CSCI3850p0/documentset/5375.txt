<TITLE> Twenty-two injured in French highway pile-up</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Twenty-two injured in French highway pile-up</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-FRANCE-PILEUP </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>PARIS (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Twenty-two people were injured Saturday, one seriously, in a 40-vehicle pileup on a foggy highway southeast of Paris, police said. </P>
<P> A spokesman said the accident, on the A6 highway near the town of Auxerre, was probably due to vehicles traveling too fast in poor visibility. </P>
