<TITLE> Party dissidents plan to vote out Rao in India</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Party dissidents plan to vote out Rao in India</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-INDIA-CONGRESS URGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>NEW DELHI, April 8 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Dissident deputies in India's ruling Congress Party will try to vote Prime Minister P.V. Nararsimha Rao's government out of office with the help of opposition groups when the budget session of parliament resumes on April 24, a former minister said on Saturday. </P>
<P> Rangarajan Kumarmangalam, former parliamentary affairs minister, told Reuters that at least 34 Congress lawmakers were ready to vote against Rao's budget with the opposition groups. </P>
