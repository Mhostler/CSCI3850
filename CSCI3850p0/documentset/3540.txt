<TITLE> Israeli soldiers shoot dead three West Bank Arabs</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Israeli soldiers shoot dead three West Bank Arabs</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-ISRAEL-UNREST-SHOOTING 1STLD </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES>(Updates with details from security sources)</NOTES>
<DATELINE>JERUSALEM (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Israeli soldiers shot and killed two armed Palestinians and wounded a third in a clash Monday in the Israeli-occupied West Bank, security sources and settlers said. </P>
<P> The sources said soldiers and Palestinians exchanged fire near a main road in the West Bank. They said the bodies of two Palestinians were later discovered with rifles at their sides. </P>
<P> Earlier Monday, Israeli soldiers shot dead a Palestinian gunmen near the West Bank city of Hebron. </P>
