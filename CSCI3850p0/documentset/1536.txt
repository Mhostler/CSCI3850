<TITLE> Germans evacuated as wartime bomb found</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Germans evacuated as wartime bomb found</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-GERMANY-BOMB </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WILHELMSHAVEN, Germany (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  About 10,000 people in the northern city of Wilhelmshaven were evacuated Wednesday night after an unexploded World War Two bomb was found buried in a building site, police said Thursday. </P>
<P> The owner of the land ordered a search of the site because he suspected explosives could have been left over from Allied bombing raids in the 1940s. </P>
<P> Experts defused the bomb which was buried five yards under the earth. </P>
<P> Three people were killed and 17 were injured when a bomb exploded on a building site in Berlin last week. </P>
