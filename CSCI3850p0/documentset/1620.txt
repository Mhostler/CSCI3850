<TITLE> Yeltsin arrives at White House for summit</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Yeltsin arrives at White House for summit</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CLINTON-RUSSIA-ARRIVE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  President Clinton welcomed Russian President Boris Yeltsin to the White House Tuesday, saying the old superpower rivals now meet ``not as adversaries but as partners.'' </P>
<P> ``Today we meet not as adversaries but as partners in the quest for a more prosperous and more peaceful planet,'' Clinton said at a red-carpet welcoming ceremony opening a two-day summit. ``The Russian-American relationship is at last, remarkably, a normal one, full of real accomplishment and genuine promise.'' </P>
