<TITLE> Turkish security forces kill 16 rebels</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Turkish security forces kill 16 rebels</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-TURKEY-KURDS </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>ANKARA, Turkey (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Turkish security forces killed 16 Kurdish guerrillas in the eastern province of Tunceli where the army has launched a big offensive, the Anatolian news agency said Sunday. </P>
<P> The Kurdistan Workers Party (PKK) rebels were killed in a clash in Hozat district about 45 miles east of the provincial capital Tunceli city, the agency reported. It did not say when the incident took place. </P>
<P> Around 2,000 troops were sent to the mountainous province late last week to reinforce 40,000 who have been taking part in a drive against the PKK since mid-September. </P>
<P> Security officials have described the operation to flush out several thousand well-entrenched guerrillas as possibly the biggest military operation in Turkey's history. </P>
<P> More than 13,000 people have been killed in the PKK's 10-year-old campaign to set up an independent Kurdish homeland in eastern and southeastern Turkey. </P>
