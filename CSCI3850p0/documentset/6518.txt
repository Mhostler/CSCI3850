<TITLE> Explosion reported at Tokyo Narita Airport - NHK</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Explosion reported at Tokyo Narita Airport - NHK</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-JAPAN-EXPLOSION URGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>TOKYO, May 13 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A loud explosion shook a men's rest room at Tokyo's Narita International Airport on Saturday afternoon and caused a small fire but no injuries, NHK public broadcasting reported. </P>
<P> Investigators found a long iron pipe in a third floor men's room of the second terminal of Narita Airport, suggesting the perpetrators were attempting to fire a projectile, NHK said. </P>
