<TITLE> Haitians loot food warehouse, woman trampled</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Haitians loot food warehouse, woman trampled</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-HAITI-LOOTING </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>PORT-AU-PRINCE, (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Thousands of hungry Haitians looted a European-funded food warehouse Tuesday and carried off bags of rice and beans in an impoverished slum of the Haitian capital, eyewitnesses said. </P>
<P> A elderly woman was seriously injured when the crowd of people running from the building trampled over her, a Reuter photographer on the scene said. </P>
