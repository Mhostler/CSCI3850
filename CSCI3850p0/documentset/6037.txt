<TITLE> Catastrophic forest fire raging in China's west</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Catastrophic forest fire raging in China's west</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CHINA-BLAZE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>BEIJING (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Thousands of people are fighting a ``catastrophic'' forest fire in China's southwestern Yunnan province, the China News Service reported Tuesday. </P>
<P> More than 4,000 local garrisons, forest policemen and local residents were trying to put out the blaze, which broke out April 17 in Anning County, the agency said. </P>
<P> The southern perimeter of the fire had been ``basically extinguished,'' it said. It was not immediately known if there were any casualties. </P>
<P> Forest fires last year destroyed 80,000 acres of Chinese woodland and killed 25 people, according to official figures. </P>
