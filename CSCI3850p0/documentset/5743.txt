<SUBJECT> BC-FISH-EUROPE-BREAK -2 BRUSSELS </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES>:</NOTES>
<DATELINE></DATELINE>
<P> ``Some progress has been made. We could reconvene at any time,'' said one diplomat. ``We are in touch with our national capitals.'' </P>
<P> Portugal frustrated final agreement in the dispute over fishing for Greenland halibut (turbot) off Newfoundland. </P>
<P> Lisbon said it was not happy with the way the EU's catch quota had been shared out. ``We are against the deal,'' a Portuguese spokesman said. </P>
<P> Portugal could in theory be outvoted by the other 14 EU states, but EU sources said this was unlikely to happen as this would only create more problems. </P>
