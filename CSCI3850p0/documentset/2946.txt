<TITLE> Eight prisoners suspected in Dahmer prison murder</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Eight prisoners suspected in Dahmer prison murder</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-DAHMER-SUSPECTS URGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>MADISON, Wis.(Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Eight inmates are suspected to have taken part in the prison murder of serial killer Jeffrey Dahmer, state prison officials said Thursday. </P>
<P> Previously, the investigation into Monday's bludgeoning murders of Dahmer, 34, and fellow-inmate Jesse Anderson, 37, focused on Stephen Scarver, 25, an inmate working on the same janitorial detail. </P>
