<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>

<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE></DATELINE>
<P> ``We can confirm that Spain has submitted a case concerning the fisheries dispute with Canada,'' a court official said. </P>
<P> The court could make no further comment for the time being but would release a statement Wednesday, she added. </P>
<P> The fishing dispute between Canada and Spain and its EU partners began earlier this month when Canada seized the Spanish trawler Estai in international waters and charged its captain with illegal fishing. </P>
<P> Spain said at the time it was considering taking Canada before the World Court. </P>
