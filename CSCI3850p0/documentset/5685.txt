<TITLE> Chrysler says it will review Tracinda offer</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Chrysler says it will review Tracinda offer</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CHRYSLER-STATEMENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>DETROIT (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Chrysler Corp. said Wednesday its board and legal and financial advisers would review a takeover bid from investor Kirk Kerkorian's Tracinda Corp. worth more than $20 billion. </P>
<P> Chrysler, the nation's third largest car maker, said in a statement Tracinda had no financing commitments to complete the deal and had no investment banking adviser. It also said it had not received a formal offer. </P>
