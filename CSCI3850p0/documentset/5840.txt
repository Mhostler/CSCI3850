<TITLE> Clinton still optimistic on Mideast peace prospect</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Clinton still optimistic on Mideast peace prospect</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-MIDEAST-CLINTON </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  President Bill Clinton said on Tuesday he remained more hopeful of the outlook for a comprehensive Middle East peace now than he was 45 days ago even though Israeli-Syrian talks have hit snags. </P>
<P> ``I am more hopeful than I was, let's say, 45 days ago. We just have to keep at it,'' Clinton said at a news conference when asked if he thought Middle East peace prospects had become ``stalemated.'' </P>
