<TITLE> Nigerian military ruler given open-ended rule</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Nigerian military ruler given open-ended rule</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-NIGERIA-ABACHA </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>ABUJA, Nigeria (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A Nigerian constitutional conference Tuesday gave military ruler General Sani Abacha an open-ended term in power. </P>
<P> It passed a resolution reversing an earlier decision which had said Abacha, who took power in a coup in November 1993, should hand over power to an elected leader Jan. 1, 1996. </P>
