<TITLE> Ex-Pennsylvania gov Casey starts presidential bid</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Ex-Pennsylvania gov Casey starts presidential bid</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CLINTON-CASEY </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Former Pennsylvania Democratic governor Robert Casey took the first step Friday toward possibly challenging President Clinton for the 1996 Democratic presidential nomination. </P>
<P> He filed papers with the Federal Election Commission here through his law firm that he is organizing an exploratory committee to determine whether he will be a full scale candidate. </P>
