<TITLE> Utah Sen. Bennett endorses Bob Dole</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Utah Sen. Bennett endorses Bob Dole</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-POLITICS-DOLE-BENNETT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Sen. Robert Bennett of Utah Tuesday endorsed Senate Majority Leader Bob Dole for the 1996 Republican presidential nomination, saying Dole can provide the leadership the nation needs. </P>
<P> ``Bob Dole is a man whose time has uniquely come. Bob Dole can provide the leadership America's future now demands,'' Bennett said in a statement released by Dole's campaign committee. </P>
<P> Dole formally announced his candidacy Monday in his home state of Kansas. He has also been endorsed by Sens. Alfonse D'Amato of New York and Conrad Burns of Montana. </P>
<P> Three other Republican senators are also seeking the nomination: Phil Gramm of Texas, Richard Lugar of Indiana and Arlen Specter of Pennsylvania. Gramm and Specter have officially entered the race and Lugar will later this month. </P>
