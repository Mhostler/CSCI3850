<TITLE> 68 ARRESTED IN INDEPENDENCE DAY MELEE</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> 68 ARRESTED IN INDEPENDENCE DAY MELEE</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-USA-MELEE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>HUNTINGTON BEACH, Calif (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  More than 60 people were arrested during a late night melee as 4th of July revelers became unruly and hurled firecrackers, rocks and bottles at police, authorities said Tuesday. </P>
<P> Police Lieutenant William Stewart said 68 people were arrested before police brought the situation under control. </P>
<P> ``Rocks and bottles were thrown at the police as well as M-80s and other large firecrackers before the streets were cleared,'' he said. </P>
<P> No one was hurt in the melee in the beach community, which has been the site of similar Independence Day disturbances. </P>
