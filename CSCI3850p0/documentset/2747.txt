<TITLE> Sixteen dead as buses collide in Brazil</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Sixteen dead as buses collide in Brazil</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-BRAZIL-CRASH </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>RIO DE JANEIRO, Brazil (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Sixteen people were killed and 35 were injured when two buses collided head-on in southern Brazil early Monday, Brazilian television reported. </P>
<P> The collision occurred near the city of Porto Alegre, Globo television said. </P>
<P> Police investigating the accident believe one of the buses had a tire blowout and swerved into the path of the other vehicle, Globo said. </P>
