<TITLE> Talks expected between Russians and rebel Chechens</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Talks expected between Russians and rebel Chechens</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-RUSSIA-CHECHNYA-TALKS </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>MOSCOW (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A spokesman for the Russian Defense Ministry said Monday that talks had been ``tentatively scheduled'' for later in the day between the commander of Interior Ministry troops and the Chechen military. </P>
<P> A spokesman for the Interior Ministry troops was unable to confirm the report. He ruled out any talks with Chechen leader Dzhokhar Dudayev apart from those on ``surrender of arms and capitulation.'' </P>
<P> The Defense Ministry spokesman said the talks would be attended by Gen. Anatoly Kulikov, commander of the Interior Ministry troops who is in charge of the operation in Chechnya, a Defense Ministry representative and ``Chechen military representatives.'' </P>
<P> He gave no further details. </P>
<P> Moscow has repeatedly ruled out talks with Dudayev, but said it was ready to talk with field commanders. All previous attempts to set up talks and arrange a cease-fire have failed. </P>
