<TITLE> Israeli helicopters hit Hizbollah in Lebanon</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Israeli helicopters hit Hizbollah in Lebanon</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-LEBANON-RAID </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>JBA'A, Lebanon (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Israeli helicopters blasted a Hizbollah guerrilla post in hills in south Lebanon Tuesday night, causing four casualties, security sources said Wednesday. </P>
<P> They said the helicopters fired five rockets at a post of the pro-Iranian Hizbollah (Party of God) near Jba'a village in Iqlim al-Toufah, a ridge 15 miles south of Beirut used by the group to attack Israel's occupation zone in the south. </P>
<P> The sources did not specify what the Hizbollah casualties were. It was the second air strike into Lebanon this year, both of them occurring Tuesday. </P>
<P> Earlier in the day, Israeli warplanes rocketed a Hizbollah post on Mlita hill in Iqlim al-Toufah hours after guerrillas wounded two Israeli-backed militiamen in the zone. </P>
