<TITLE> Botswana ruling party declared election winner</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Botswana ruling party declared election winner</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-BOTSWANA-WINNER </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>GABORONE (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The ruling Botswana Democratic Party (BDP) was Monday declared the winner of Botswana's general elections after taking 23 seats in the southern African nation's 40-member parliament. </P>
<P> Declaring the BDP the winner, Chief Justice Moleleki Mokama said the ruling party had 23 seats and the opposition Botswana National Front 10. Final results are expected later in the day. </P>
