<TITLE> France begins voting in presidential election</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> France begins voting in presidential election</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-FRANCE-POLL </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>PARIS, April 23 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  France began voting on Sunday in the first round of a two-stage election to choose a president to lead the nation into the 21st century. </P>
<P> Conservative Paris mayor Jacques Chirac started as the clear favourite after leading in opinion polls. </P>
