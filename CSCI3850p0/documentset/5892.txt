<TITLE> Oklahoma blast suspect returned to US from UK</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Oklahoma blast suspect returned to US from UK</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-BLAST-SUSPECT 2NDLD </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES>(Updates with statement from ministry)</NOTES>
<DATELINE>LONDON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Britain Thursday sent back to the United States a possible suspect in the Oklahoma bomb blast, the interior ministry said. </P>
<P> A Home office (interior ministry) spokeswoman said the man, carrying a U.S. passport, was identified by immigration staff at London's Heathrow Airport. He was on a flight from Chicago. </P>
<P> The man was arrested on information being pooled by U.S. and British authorities, she said without elaborating. </P>
<P> ``A possible suspect connected with the Oklahoma bomb has been returned to the United States by the U.K. immigration service,'' a ministry statement said. </P>
<P> ``He was interviewed, refused entry to the U.K., detained and returned to the United States this afternoon,'' it added. </P>
<P> There was no official word on which flight the suspect had been flown to the United States. But airport sources said it was possible he was on a British Airways flight to Chicago. </P>
