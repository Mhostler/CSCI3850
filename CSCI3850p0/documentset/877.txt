<TITLE> FIFTEEN PEOPLE DIE IN RUSSIAN BUS PLUNGE</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> FIFTEEN PEOPLE DIE IN RUSSIAN BUS PLUNGE</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CIS-RUSSIA-CRASH </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>MOSCOW (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Fifteen people were killed and another 21 injured early Friday when their bus plunged off a bridge outside Moscow, Itar-Tass news agency said. </P>
<P> Police said the bus was carrying tourists from the former Soviet republic of Armenia. </P>
