<TITLE> Hutus stoned and beaten to death in Rwanda</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Hutus stoned and beaten to death in Rwanda</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-RWANDA-STONING </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>KIGALI, Rwanda (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Some 14 Hutus were stoned and beaten to death in a village in southern Rwanda after returning home from camps forcibly closed by government troops, a U.N. official said Sunday. </P>
<P> ``They were stoned and beaten to death ... 100 others were forced to flee into the bush while women were subjected to indignities such as walking without their clothes on,'' said U.N. High Commissioner for Refugees (UNHCR) spokesman Fernando del Mundo. </P>
