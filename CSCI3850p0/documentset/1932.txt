<TITLE> Space shuttle detoured to California</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Space shuttle detoured to California</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-SPACE-SHUTTLE-DELAY 1STLD </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES>(Eds: Updates with diversion to California landing site)</NOTES>
<DATELINE>CAPE CANAVERAL, Florida (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The NASA space agency ordered six astronauts to land the shuttle Endeavour in California on Tuesday because it was too cloudy at the spaceship's runway in Florida. </P>
<P> Touchdown was rescheduled from 11:36 a.m. EDT to 1:02 p.m. EDT at Edwards Air Force Base in the Mojave Desert. </P>
<P> Endeavour was returning to Earth after an 11-day mission of environmental mapmaking. </P>
