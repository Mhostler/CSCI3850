<P> A highlight of the mission will be a link-up in June between Mir and the U.S. space shuttle Atlantis. Aboard the shuttle, the 100th U.S. manned space mission, will be five U.S. astronauts and two Russian cosmonauts. </P>
<P> One of Thagard's main tasks will be to study the effect of long stays in near-zero gravity -- a building block in creating a permanently manned multi-billion dollar orbiting space station dubbed Alpha. </P>
<P> The three-phase construction of the space station, due to be completed in June 2002, brings Moscow and Washington together with Canada, the European Space Agency and Japan in a program to create the orbiting science center. </P>
