<TITLE> No crisis in US-French relations - Gore</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> No crisis in US-French relations - Gore</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-USA-FRANCE-GORE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>BRUSSELS (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Vice President Al Gore said Saturday there was no crisis in U.S.-French relations despite France's accusation of American spying in Paris. </P>
<P> Asked about the allegations at a news conference after meeting European Commission President Jacques Santer, Gore said, ``This is a matter that is best dealt with privately. There is no crisis in U.S.-French relations. </P>
<P> ``The U.S.-French relationship is a very strong relationship which is very important to the American people.'' </P>
<P> Gore was speaking at the Group of Seven industrial nations conference on the information society. </P>
