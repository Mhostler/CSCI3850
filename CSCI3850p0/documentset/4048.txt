<TITLE> Paraguay offers to mediate in Peru, Ecuador conflict</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Paraguay offers to mediate in Peru, Ecuador conflict</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-PERU-ECUADOR-PARAGUAY </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>ASUNCION, Paraguay (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Paraguay has offered to mediate in the border jungle conflict between Peru and Ecuador, Foreign Minister Luis Ramirez Boettner said Monday. </P>
<P> ``President Juan Carlos Wasmosy expressed to presidents Sixto Duran Ballen (of Ecuador) and Alberto Fujimori (of Peru) his concern over the border conflict and offered them his mediation to achieve a peaceful understanding between both countries,'' Ramirez Boettner told journalists. </P>
<P> Tension over a vast disputed region of the Amazon jungle flared up into armed clashes between Peruvian and Ecuadorian forces last Thursday. </P>
