<TITLE> GOP senators attack Korea deal but oppose reneging</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> GOP senators attack Korea deal but oppose reneging</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-KOREA-USA </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Senate Republicans Thursday harshly criticized the deal freezing North Korea's nuclear program but said Congress should not renege and may find it hard to restrict the agreement. </P>
<P> Senator Frank Murkowski of Alaska, chairing a Senate Energy Committee hearing on the deal, said the deal has been described as nuclear blackmail but ``I do not believe that we should renege on this agreement.'' </P>
