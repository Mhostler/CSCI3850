<TITLE> Israeli health minister saves heart attack victim</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Israeli health minister saves heart attack victim</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-ISRAEL-DOCTOR </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>JERUSALEM, March 22 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  One Israeli will probably never grumble again about the government's administration of health care. </P>
<P> Felled by a heart attack in a Jerusalem hotel on Tuesday, the 72-year-old man was revived by Health Minister Ephraim Sneh, who answered a ``Is there a doctor in the house?'' call, a ministry spokeswoman said on Wednesday. </P>
<P> Sneh, who was about to give a political speech in the hotel, is a specialist in internal medicine and a former chief medical officer of the Israeli army. </P>
<P> The heart attack victim was reported to be in stable condition in hospital. </P>
