<TITLE> Grenade blast wounds 16 in Sierra Leone</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Grenade blast wounds 16 in Sierra Leone</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-LEONE-GRENADE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>FREETOWN, Sierra Leone (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A 7-year-old boy and 15 other people were seriously injured Sunday when a grenade exploded in a busy area of Sierra Leone's capital Freetown, police said. </P>
<P> A senior police official told reporters police had detained the soldier who dropped the grenade in Freetown's east end. They said it was not clear how the explosion happened. </P>
<P> The wounded were taken to Freetown's Connaught hospital for treatment. Doctors said the boy had serious head injuries. </P>
