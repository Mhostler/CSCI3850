<TITLE> U.S. officials say deal reached with Haiti leaders</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> U.S. officials say deal reached with Haiti leaders</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-HAITI-USA-AGREEMENT URGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The United States and Haitian military leaders reached agreement Sunday for the leaders to step down voluntarily and avoid a U.S. invasion to restore exiled President Jean-Bertrand Aristide to power, senior U.S. officials said. </P>
