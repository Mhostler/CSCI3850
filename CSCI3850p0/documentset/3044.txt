<TITLE> Iranian man, woman stoned to death on court order</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Iranian man, woman stoned to death on court order</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-IRAN-STONING </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>TEHRAN, Iran (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  An Iranian man and a married woman have been stoned to death by court order for killing the woman's husband in order to continue their love affair, a newspaper said Wednesday. </P>
<P> The pair, convicted and sentenced by an Iranian penal court, were stoned to death in the southwestern city of Ramhormoz, the daily Hamshahri said. </P>
<P> Iran's Islamic penal code sets death by stoning for adultery and some other sexual offenses. </P>
