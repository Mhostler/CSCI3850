<TITLE> Poland to bolster multi-national forces in Haiti</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Poland to bolster multi-national forces in Haiti</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-HAITI-POLAND </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WARSAW, Poland (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The Polish government Monday voted to send a 50-man contingent to join multinational peacekeeping forces in Haiti, PAP news agency said. </P>
<P> ``The Polish contingent will be dispatched shortly and its task will be to supervise the operations of the Haitian police,'' PAP quoted a government statement as saying. </P>
<P> At a cabinet meeting last week, ministers were told the Poles would serve in motorized patrols of two Polish military gendarmes, a Haitian policeman and an interpreter. </P>
