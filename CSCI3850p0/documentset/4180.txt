<TITLE> Sixteen killed, seven hurt in S.Korean ship fire</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Sixteen killed, seven hurt in S.Korean ship fire</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-KOREA-SHIP FIRE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>SEOUL (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  At least 16 people were killed and seven seriously injured in a fire aboard a container vessel being repaired in a South Korean shipyard Tuesday, a spokesman for the shipowner said. </P>
<P> The 17,682 gross tons ship, Hanjin Busan, caught fire in drydock in the southern port city of Pusan, he said. </P>
<P> ``The container vessel caught fire in its engine room this morning,'' said an official of Hanjin Shipping Ltd, the owners of ship. </P>
<P> ``I understand several more people are trapped inside the ship but we don't know their state,'' he said. </P>
<P> The ship was being repaired by Hanjin Heavy Industries Co Ltd. Both Hanjin Heavy and Hanjin Shipping are member companies of the Hanjin Group, one of South Korea's top 10 business conglomerates. </P>
<P> Hanjin Shipping officials said the vessel was still shrouded by dark smoke six hours after the fire broke out. </P>
