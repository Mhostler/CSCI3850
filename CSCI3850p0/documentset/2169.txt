<TITLE> Americans killed by high-speed train in Italy</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Americans killed by high-speed train in Italy</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-ITALY-AMERICANS </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>VICENZA, Italy (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Two young sons of U.S. military personnel based in northern Italy were killed Saturday when they were hit by a high-speed train on a level crossing, police said. </P>
<P> Daniel Scott, 20 and Jason Kossan, 19, died instantly when they were struck by the train, traveling about 60 mph. </P>
<P> Police said the two, who ignored the crossing's lowered safety bars, may have been wearing personal stereos which could have prevented them from hearing the approaching train. </P>
