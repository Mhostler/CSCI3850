<TITLE> German parcel bomb was addressed to Turk</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> German parcel bomb was addressed to Turk</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-GERMANY-EXPLOSION </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>FRANKFURT, May 4 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A parcel bomb which killed a Frankfurt post office worker on Wednesday was adressed to a Turk living in Germany but probably did not constitute a racist attack, the state prosecutor's office said on Thursday. </P>
<P> ``The man is known to police in connection with criminal circles,'' prosecutor Job Tilmann said in response to an inquiry. ``At this stage of our investigations we can exclude a racist motivation for the attack.'' </P>
