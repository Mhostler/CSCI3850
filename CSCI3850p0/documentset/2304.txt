<TITLE> Seventeen killed in India as truck overturns</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Seventeen killed in India as truck overturns</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-INDIA-ACCIDENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>NEW DELHI, India (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Seventeen people were killed and 17 others seriously injured when a truck overturned in southern India, the Press Trust of India (PTI) said Sunday. </P>
<P> The accident took place late Saturday at a village near Raichur, some 220 miles from Bangalore, the capital of the southern state of Karnataka, as the truck was carrying laborers returning from work, it said. </P>
