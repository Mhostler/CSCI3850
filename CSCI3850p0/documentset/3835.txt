<TITLE> Japan quake death toll reaches 3,081</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Japan quake death toll reaches 3,081</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-QUAKE-JAPAN-TOLL </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>TOKYO (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  At least 3,081 people were killed, 634 are missing and 14,672 injured after Tuesday's giant earthquake in central Japan, a spokesman for the National Police Agency said Thursday. </P>
<P> The earthquake, measuring 7.2 on the Richter scale, was the biggest to hit central Japan in half a century. </P>
