<TITLE> Milan blast death toll rises to seven</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Milan blast death toll rises to seven</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-ITALY-COLLAPSE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>MILAN, Italy (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The death toll in an explosion which ripped through an apartment building in Milan rose to seven overnight, police said Saturday. </P>
<P> The blast on Friday morning sent tons of debris crashing down onto a popular bar on the ground floor of the building. </P>
<P> Fire brigades and energy board officials have said the blast was almost certainly caused by a gas leak in one of the apartments. </P>
<P> The seventh victim was identified as the bar owner's son, Fabrizio Doblini, who was in his 20s. Fourteen people were injured in the blast, six of them seriously. </P>
<P> Rescue workers who searched throughout the night said they did not expect to find more bodies but excavations would continue over the weekend. </P>
