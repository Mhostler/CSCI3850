<TITLE> Member of Gucci family shot dead in Milan</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Member of Gucci family shot dead in Milan</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-ITALY-GUCCI </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>MILAN, Italy (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A man identified by Italian police as a member of the Gucci leather goods and fashion family was shot dead in Milan Monday. </P>
<P> Police said the victim, Maurizio Gucci, 44, was shot three times in the face by a man armed with a pistol as he was entering his offices in central Milan. A guard at the building was wounded in the shoulder, police said. </P>
<P> An officer at police headquarters in Milan told Reuters by telephone that the pistol used by the killer was equipped with a silencer. Witnesses saw the gunman, described as in his 30s, flee by car, the officer said. </P>
<P> A second guard at the building was unhurt. </P>
<P> Shoes, luggage and handbags designed under the world famous Gucci brand name are synonymous with luxury and style. </P>
<P> The Gucci family was not immediately available for comment on the killing. </P>
