<TITLE> China cotton farmer beats buyer to death</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> China cotton farmer beats buyer to death</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CHINA-COTTON </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>BEIJING (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A Chinese cotton farmer and six accomplices beat a state buyer to death with sticks and bricks after the official refused to pay the farmer's price, the China Business Daily reported Monday. </P>
<P> He Yanshan and his gang killed Zhu Fangrao, 42, in Xiantao city, in central China Oct. 25, when the official refused to be tricked into paying twice the cotton's value, the daily said. </P>
<P> Police captured two of the gang but the farmer was still at large, the paper said. </P>
<P> A cotton shortage has led to a flourishing black market in China with many farmers refusing to sell their crop to the government in the hope that prices will rise. </P>
