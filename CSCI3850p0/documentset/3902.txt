<TITLE> Cuban Foreign Minister sets off on Asia tour</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Cuban Foreign Minister sets off on Asia tour</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CUBA-ASIA </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>HAVANA (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Cuban Foreign Minister Roberto Robaina set off Monday for Japan at the start of a six-nation tour of Asia, state media said. </P>
<P> Robaina, who has traveled extensively since getting the job in 1993, was set to visit Japan from Jan. 25-28, followed by North Korea, Thailand, Nepal, China and the Philippines, returning home Feb. 12. </P>
