<TITLE> Russian oil pipeline ruptures in Siberia-Interfax</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Russian oil pipeline ruptures in Siberia-Interfax</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-OIL-RUSSIA-SPILL </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>MOSCOW (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A corroded pipeline has spilled 42,400 cubic feet of crude oil in western Siberia's Tyumen region, Interfax news agency quoted the Civil Defense Ministry as saying Tuesday. </P>
<P> The spill, which took place April 19, covered an area of 74 acres about 10 miles north of the city of Megion, it said. </P>
<P> The accident was the latest in a series highlighting the poor state of Russia's pipeline network. </P>
<P> Leading Russian environment official Alexei Yablokov has estimated total leaks at about 3 million tons a year. </P>
