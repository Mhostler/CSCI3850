<TITLE> Austrian kills five, shoots self</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Austrian kills five, shoots self</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-AUSTRIA-SHOTS 1STLD </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES>    (Updates with man killing himself)</NOTES>
<DATELINE>VIENNA, Austria (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A 64-year-old Austrian pensioner shot dead five people and wounded at least five more in the city court building in Linz, western Austria, Friday before killing himself with the same gun, police said. </P>
<P> They said that, after running amok in the courthouse, the man fled to his weekend cottage outside the city, where he shot himself. </P>
<P> Austrian radio said the pensioner had been inside a courtroom facing libel charges when he opened fire. </P>
<P> ``There are five dead and at least five injured in the shooting inside the building,'' a police spokesman told Reuters. </P>
<P> ``The man has killed himself with the same weapon in his weekend cottage outside Linz,'' he added. </P>
