<TITLE> Prisoners escape in Haiti</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Prisoners escape in Haiti</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-HAITI-PRISON </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>PORT-AU-PRINCE, Haiti (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A man implicated in the killing of two U.S. Embassy employees in Port-Au-Prince last November has escaped from prison with 10 others, officials said Tuesday. </P>
<P> College Francois was being held at the Petionville prison for alleged involvement in the killing of two Haitians employed by the embassy during an an armed robbery Nov. 11. </P>
<P> The 11 prisoners escaped from the prison in the hills above the capital Monday night when guards failed to properly lock three cell doors, a diplomatic source told Reuters. All 11 remained at large. </P>
