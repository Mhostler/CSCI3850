<TITLE> Spain to reactivate Iraq embassy with official</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Spain to reactivate Iraq embassy with official</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-SPAIN-IRAQ </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>MADRID, Spain Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Spain, which has maintained diplomatic relations with Iraq, is sending an administrator to Baghdad to reactivate non-political functions at its embassy there, the foreign affairs ministry said Wednesday. </P>
<P> The administrative attache, who will be dispatched within a few days, will be responsible for the small Spanish community and the two buildings Spain owns and rents in Iraq. </P>
<P> Spain withdrew its diplomatic personnel during the Gulf War, but the embassy technically remained open. </P>
