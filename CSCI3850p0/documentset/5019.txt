<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE></DATELINE>
<P> Police identified the toxic material as acetonitrile, a colorless liquid used in the synthesis of organic materials. It was not clear where the fumes originated. </P>
<P> The fumes were detected at 15 subway stations on the trunk Hibiya and Marunouchi lines. </P>
<P> At Kamiyacho station, firemen were seen descending to underground platforms using oxygen masks. </P>
