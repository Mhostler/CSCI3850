<TITLE> Senate panel passes telecommunications reform bill</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Senate panel passes telecommunications reform bill</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-TELECOMMUNICATIONS </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The Senate Commerce Committee approved 17-2 a sweeping reform of the nation's telecommunications law that will make it easier for telephone companies and cable television firms to compete with each other. </P>
<P> But the measure was approved with the provision that senators could still amend it prior to it going to the full Senate. </P>
