<TITLE> Former Wimbledon champion Fred Perry dies, aged 85</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Former Wimbledon champion Fred Perry dies, aged 85</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-TENNIS-PERRY-DEATH URGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>LONDON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Fred Perry, the last British man to win the Wimbledon singles title, has died in hospital in Australia, the All England tennis club said Thursday. </P>
<P> Perry, who was 85, was Wimbledon champion from 1934 to 1936. He had been in hospital in Melbourne after breaking four ribs in a fall in a hotel last week. </P>
