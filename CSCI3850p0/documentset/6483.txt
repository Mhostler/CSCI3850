<TITLE> Yachting-Stars &AMP; Stripes first at start in America's Cup Race 4</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Yachting-Stars &AMP; Stripes first at start in America's Cup Race 4</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-YACHTING-AMERICAS RACE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>SAN DIEGO (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Dennis Conner's Stars & Stripes team aboard Young America crossed the start line 7 seconds ahead of Peter Blake's Team New Zealand aboard Black Magic I in Race 4 of the best-of-nine America's Cup series on Thursday. </P>
<P> Stars & Stripes took the righthand side with Team New Zealand taking the left and sailing with greater speed. </P>
<P> Team New Zealand leads the series 3-0. </P>
