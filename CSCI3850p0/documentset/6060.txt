<TITLE> Clinton attends funeral of agent killed by bomb</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Clinton attends funeral of agent killed by bomb</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-BLAST-CLINTON-AGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>ROCKVILLE, Md.(Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  President Clinton joined mourners Wednesday at funeral services for Alan Whicher, a Secret Service agent killed in the bombing of a federal building in Oklahoma City. </P>
<P> Whicher, 40, had been part of the Secret Service detail that guarded Clinton, but was promoted last October to assistant special agent in charge of the field office in Oklahoma City. </P>
<P> The services were conducted at St. Patrick's Catholic Church, to be followed by burial at Gate of Heaven Cemetery in nearby Silver Spring.  Other agents also killed in the bombing were Cynthia Brown, 25; Donald Leonard and Mickey Maroney, both 50, and investigative assistant Kathy Seidl, 39. The office manager, Linda McKinney, is missing and presumed dead. </P>
