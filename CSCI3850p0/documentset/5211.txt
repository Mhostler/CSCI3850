<TITLE> At least three killed in Turkish mine blast</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> At least three killed in Turkish mine blast</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-TURKEY-MINE 1STLD </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES> (Updates with death toll, adds details)</NOTES>
<DATELINE>ANKARA, Turkey (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  At least three people were killed and up to 40 trapped after an explosion Sunday in a mine at the central Turkish town of Sorgun. </P>
<P> A mine official told Reuters by telephone: ``We have taken out three bodies. We fear somewhere between 25 to 40 workers may be trapped in the pit.'' </P>
<P> A local police official said six people were rescued with injuries, two of them seriously. </P>
<P> ``Rescue teams are here and they have started working. We are awaiting heavy rescue equipment to arrive from a nearby town and hope to reach the trapped miners by midnight,'' the mine official said. </P>
<P> The mine is owned by a private company and employes 200 workers in all. </P>
