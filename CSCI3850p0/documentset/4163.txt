<P> Returning to the witness stand following Friday's dramatic testimony, Denise Brown said her younger sister asked her to take the photos within days of a 1989 New Year's Day attack. Simpson later pleaded no-contest to domestic violence charges stemming from the incident. </P>
<P> In one photo, Nicole -- who was to divorce Simpson three years later -- was shown with a swollen right eye. In another, she was seen lifting her arm to reveal a large bruise near her armpit. </P>
<P> Simpson is on trial charged with murdering his ex-wife and her friend Ron Goldman last June 12. </P>
