<TITLE> Snipers kill French U.N soldier,woman in Sarajevo</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Snipers kill French U.N soldier,woman in Sarajevo</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-YUGOSLAVIA-SNIPER </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>SARAJEVO, Bosnia-Herzegovina (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Snipers shot and killed a French U.N. soldier and a 54-year old woman and wounded two other people Saturday in separate attacks in the Bosnian capital Sarajevo, hospital staff and state radio said. </P>
<P> It was the second fatal shoooting of a French peacekeeper in as many days. French Defense Minister Francois Leotard arrived in Sarajevo Saturday evening to discuss the security of peacekeeping forces with U.N. commanders. </P>
<P> The other death was a civilian, Munira Vila, shot in the head in government-held Sarajevo near the Dobrinja district. She died shortly afterward at a local hospital. </P>
<P> In a northern district near front lines, sniper rounds struck 16-year old Avdo Petogij in the chest, causing serious injury. He was undergoing emergency medical treatment, hospital staff said. </P>
<P> Nizama Ahmethodzic, 23, suffered face wounds after sniper fire raked a bus heading from Dobrinja into the city center but doctors said the injuries were not life-threatening. </P>
<P> The United Nations was investigating the incidents. </P>
<P> U.N. peacekeepers have blamed Bosnian Serb forces surrounding the city for a wave of sniping in recent weeks. </P>
