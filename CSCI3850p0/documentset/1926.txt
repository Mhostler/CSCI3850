<TITLE> U.S. forces start depolyment in Kuwait-source</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> U.S. forces start depolyment in Kuwait-source</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-IRAQ-KUWAIT-DEPLOYMENT URGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>KUWAIT (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  U.S. forces Tuesday started to draw equipment pre-positioned in Kuwait and to deploy in the desert to counter an Iraqi buildup north of the border, a Kuwaiti official said. </P>
<P> A U.S. military spokesman could not immediately be contacted for comment on the activities of troops who flew in Monday. </P>
