<TITLE> Belgian court jails cat torturer</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Belgian court jails cat torturer</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-BELGIUM-CATS </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>BRUSSELS, Belgium (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A Belgian man who hired prostitutes to torture and kill cats for his pleasure was jailed for two months Thursday. </P>
<P> The judge also fined the man $3,450, which he will have to pay if he commits another offense, the Belga news agency reported. </P>
<P> The 41-year-old man named only as Patrick K, was reported to police by a prostitute asked to torture a cat to death while he masturbated. </P>
<P> The Brussels man said he had been deeply disturbed as a child when he saw a cat playing with a small bird. </P>
