<TITLE> Lesotho's King Moshoeshoe to return to throne</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Lesotho's King Moshoeshoe to return to throne</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-LESOTHO-KING </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>MASERU, Lesotho (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Lesotho's King Moshoeshoe II will be reinstated to the throne Jan. 25, nearly five years after the military ousted him as monarch of the southern African mountain kingdom, the government said Tuesday. </P>
<P> A government statement said his son, King Letsie III, who has occupied the throne since November 1990, would abdicate. </P>
