<TITLE> Clinton reduces U.S. nuclear bomb-building stocks</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Clinton reduces U.S. nuclear bomb-building stocks</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CLINTON-NUCLEAR </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  President Clinton has decided to remove 200 tons of bomb-building material from U.S. nuclear stockpiles as a demonstration of his commitment to reduce nuclear weaponry, a U.S. official said Wednesday. </P>
<P> The official, who briefed reporters on condition that he not be identified, said Clinton would announce the move in a major foreign policy speech Wednesday night. </P>
