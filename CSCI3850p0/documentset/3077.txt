<TITLE> Price again honored by peers</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Price again honored by peers</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-GOLF-PRICE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>PONTE VEDRA BEACH, Fla (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Nick Price claimed another honor in a season of triumphs Thursday when he was named PGA Tour Player of the Year for the second year in a row by a vote of his fellow competitors on the U.S. tour. </P>
<P> Price won five tour events, including the PGA Championship, as he established a season earnings record of $1,499,927. </P>
<P> The Zimbabwean also won this year's British Open, making him the first player to win two majors in one season since Britain's Nick Faldo in 1990. </P>
<P> Price had eight top 10 finishes in 19 starts and now has 13 U.S. PGA Tour victories in the last four years. </P>
<P> Price will receive his award, along with the Arnold Palmer Award as the Tour's leading money winner, at an awards banquet Jan. 6 during the season-opening Mercedes Championships at La Costa Resort and Spa in Carlsbad, Calif. </P>
