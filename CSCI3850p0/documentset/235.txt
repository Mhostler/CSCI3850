<TITLE> BOMB DAMAGES OFFICE OF CORSICAN FARMERS' FEDERATION</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> BOMB DAMAGES OFFICE OF CORSICAN FARMERS' FEDERATION</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-FRANCE-BOMB </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>AJACCIO, Corsica (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A bomb explosion damaged the offices of a farmer's federation in northern Corsica, police said Friday. </P>
<P> Thursday night's bombing destroyed the entrance lobby and shattered the windows of the Haute-Corse departmental agriculture chamber in Bastia town. </P>
<P> No group claimed responsibility, but investigators found a pamphlet calling ``For a clean agriculture.'' </P>
<P> In March, an armed group announced the creation of a Corsican Farmers' Front to ``defend the true professionals of agriculture.'' </P>
