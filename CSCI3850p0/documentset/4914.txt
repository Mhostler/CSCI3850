<TITLE> Canada says Spanish fishing off disputed area</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Canada says Spanish fishing off disputed area</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-FISH-CANADA-SPAIN </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>OTTAWA (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Canada's Fisheries Minister Brian Tobin said Tuesday that Spanish vessels had resumed fishing in international waters off the coast of Newfoundland in a ''provocative'' manner. </P>
<P> ``I know there is some fishing activity in the area, just beyond the nose of the Grand Banks ... clearly they are being somewhat provocative,'' Tobin told reporters. </P>
<P> Canada last week seized a Spanish trawler fishing for turbot off its coast, setting off a bitter diplomatic dispute with the European Union. </P>
