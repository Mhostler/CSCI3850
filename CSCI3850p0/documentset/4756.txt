<TITLE> Two injured in Army helicopter crash in Texas</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Two injured in Army helicopter crash in Texas</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-COPTER </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>CONROE, Texas (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  An Army Reserve Apache helicopter crashed Monday night at an airport during a training mission, injuring two crewmen, a spokesman said. </P>
<P> The crewmen were in stable condition at Conroe Medical Center about 40 miles north of Houston, the Army Reserve spokesman said. </P>
<P> The helicopter, from a nearby Army Reserve base, was on a training mission at the Montgomery County Airport when the crash occurred near a runway around 9 p.m., the spokesman said. </P>
<P> No other details were immediately available. </P>
