<TITLE> Betty Buckley to star on Broadway next July</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Betty Buckley to star on Broadway next July</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-SUNSET </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>NEW YORK (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Betty Buckley, currently starring in the London production of Andrew Lloyd Webber's ``Sunset Boulevard,'' will be taking her act to Broadway next July to replace Glenn Close, sources close to the production said Friday. </P>
<P> The sources said Buckley will replace Close July 4 in the key role of Norma Desmond, the fading film star in the musical based on the classic 1950 film which starred Gloria Swanson. </P>
<P> Buckley won rave reviews in London for her performance, as did Close for her work in New York. </P>
<P> The sources said an official announcement will be made next Wednesday. </P>
