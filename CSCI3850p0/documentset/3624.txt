<TITLE> Gunman shoots two at Ford auto factory, kills self</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Gunman shoots two at Ford auto factory, kills self</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-SHOOTING </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>DETROIT (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A man shot and wounded his estranged wife and shot dead another worker at a Ford Motor Co components plant Saturday before turning the gun on himself, police said. </P>
<P> The gunman, who worked for Ford, walked into the plant around 5:20 a.m. EST and shot his wife and another man and then himself. Police said both men died and the woman was in critical condition. </P>
<P> Ford officials would not comment on the shooting but workers at the plant said the couple, who had been married for 16 years and had two children, were having marital problems. </P>
