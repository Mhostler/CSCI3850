<TITLE> At least 48 die in blaze in Taiwan</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> At least 48 die in blaze in Taiwan</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-TAIWAN-FIRE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>TAIPEI (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  At least 48 people were killed when fire swept through a karaoke parlor and restaurant complex in the Taiwan city of Taichung Wednesday, firefighters said. </P>
<P> ``At least 48 people have died. We are still looking for more,'' said a firefighter speaking by telephone from the central Taiwan city. </P>
