<TITLE> 33 injured in Algerian bombing</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> 33 injured in Algerian bombing</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-ALGERIA-EXPLOSION 1STLD </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>TUNIS, Tunisia (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A booby-trapped car exploded early Friday in an Algiers suburb, injuring 33 people including eight children and causing severe damage, Algeria's official news agency quotes security sources as saying. </P>
<P> The agency said the blast also damaged the fronts of two buildings. </P>
<P> State-run radio said the blast occurred in the Kouba district southeast of Algiers, which is a Muslim fundamentalist stronghold. </P>
<P> Residents in Kouba, reached by telephone, said they believed that policemen lived in the neighborhood. </P>
<P> They said a building and several cars were severely damaged and windows in the area were shattered. </P>
