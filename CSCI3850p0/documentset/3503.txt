<TITLE> Egypt executes Moslem militant</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Egypt executes Moslem militant</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-EGYPT-EXECUTION </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>CAIRO, Egypt (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Egypt Saturday executed a Muslim militant for belonging to an anti-government organization, security sources said. </P>
<P> They said Sherif Mohammed Hassan was hanged in a Cairo prison early Saturday. He was sentenced to death by an Alexandria court, the sources said. </P>
