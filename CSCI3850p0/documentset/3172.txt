<TITLE> Simpson defense seeks Jan 4 opening trial arguments</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Simpson defense seeks Jan 4 opening trial arguments</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-SIMPSON-TRIAL </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>LOS ANGELES (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Defense lawyers for O.J. Simpson asked a judge on Tuesday to begin opening statements in the football legend's double-murder trial no later than January 4. </P>
<P> In a motion filed on Tuesday, the defense team also asked to forgo pre-trial hearings on the admissibility of the prosecution's crucial DNA evidence that had been expected to last up to a month. </P>
