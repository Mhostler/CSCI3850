<SUBJECT> BC-VENEZUELA-BUS -2 CARACAS </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE></DATELINE>
<P> Radio Caracas Radio said the accident took place at 5 a.m. EST near the city of Maturin in the eastern state of Monagas. </P>
<P> The pipe blew up after the first bus hit it. A second bus pulled up to offer assistance, the radio reported. A reporter on the scene said there were probably more victims. </P>
<P> The pipe belonged to the state-run oil company Petroleos de Venezuela. </P>
