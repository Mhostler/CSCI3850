<TITLE> Tsk, tsk  Hillary, Jordan Queen wear same color</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Tsk, tsk  Hillary, Jordan Queen wear same color</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-MIDEAST-DRESS (PICTURE) </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>ARAVA, Israel-Jordan Border (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Hillary Rodham Clinton and Queen Noor of Jordan had more in common at the signing of the Israel-Jordan peace treaty Wednesday than just their American roots. </P>
<P> The U.S.-born queen and U.S. first lady drew gasps from a fashion-conscious crowd by stepping out in the desert in the same colored suit. </P>
<P> ``So now we know the color of peace is tourquoise,'' a woman in the crowd said. </P>
