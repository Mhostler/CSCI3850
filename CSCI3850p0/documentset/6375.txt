<TITLE> Conner leads at start of America's Cup race</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Conner leads at start of America's Cup race</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-YACHTING-AMERICAS-RACE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>SAN DIEGO (Reuter) </DATELINE>
