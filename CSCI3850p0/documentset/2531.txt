<TITLE> Two Haitians killed in U.S. embassy vehicle</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Two Haitians killed in U.S. embassy vehicle</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-HAITI-DEATHS URGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>PORT-AU-PRINCE (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Two Haitians were killed and another was severely injured when the American embassy car they were travelling in was hijacked, a presidential spokesman said Thursday. </P>
<P> ``President (Jean-Bertrand) Aristide communicated to Ambassador William Swing his shock and grief and sent his condolences to the families of the victims,'' spokesman Yvon Neptune said. </P>
