<TITLE> Basketball-NBA's Rolando Blackman joins Greece's AEK</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Basketball-NBA's Rolando Blackman joins Greece's AEK</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-SPORT-BASKETBALL BLACKMAN </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>ATHENS (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Panama's NBA veteran Rolando Blackman joined Greece's AEK Athens on Wednesday after signing a one-year contract worth $300,000, club officials said. </P>
<P> Blackman, 35, who played for 11 years with the Dallas Mavericks and two years with the New York Knicks, has scored 18,725 points in the NBA and is considered one of its best shooting guards. </P>
<P> He did not play for the Knicks in the NBA playoffs last season because of injury but was expected to help AEK in their effort to stay in Greece's premier league. </P>
