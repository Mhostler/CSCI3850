<TITLE> ARAB VILLAGE HEAD KILLED FOR AIDING ISRAEL</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> ARAB VILLAGE HEAD KILLED FOR AIDING ISRAEL</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-MIDEAST-HEBRON </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>HEBRON, West Bank (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Palestinian gunmen killed a West Bank Arab village leader whom they suspected of collaborating with Israel, Palestinians said Monday. </P>
<P> Palestinian sources said gunmen ambushed Hmaidan Mahmoud Zaareer, 47, mukhtar of Samua village near Hebron, Sunday night and shot him 12 times, killing him instantly. </P>
<P> Villagers said Zaareer was known for helping Israeli soldiers hunt Palestinian activists and said he had helped sell Arab land to Israelis. </P>
<P> In Hebron, Palestinians observed a general strike Monday, marking five months since a Jewish settler shot dead at least 30 Palestinians praying at a mosque there on February 25. Traffic in town was light. Stores were shuttered. </P>
