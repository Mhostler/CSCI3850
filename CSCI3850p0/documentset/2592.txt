<P> A police spokesman could not immediately be reached for comment. </P>
<P> Witnesses said scores of bullets were fired during the incident, which frightened residents in downtown San Francisco. </P>
<P> One witness described the scene as like ``war.'' </P>
<P> KCBS Radio said it had confirmed reports that one police officer and one paramedic had been injured. </P>
<P> There were conflicting reports about the gunman, with some reports saying the gunman was in custody and others saying he had been shot. </P>
<P> Several blocks around the area were blocked off to traffic by police. </P>
