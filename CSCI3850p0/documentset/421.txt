<SUBJECT> BC-BRITAIN-EXPLOSION -2 LONDON </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE></DATELINE>
<P> Police said they had received several reports about an explosion but had yet to confirm details. </P>
<P> A massive car bomb explosion outside the Israeli Embassy in London Tuesday injured 14 people and sparked fears of an international guerrilla campaign by Islamic fundamentalists opposed to the Middle East peace process. </P>
<P> Tuesday's bomb exploded at midday in the Kensington district of west London. The 44-66 pound bomb badly damaged the embassy and shattered windows in nearby buildings, including Kensington Palace, home of several members of Britain's Royal Family. </P>
<P> The attack came just 24 hours after Israel and Jordan signed a historic peace agreement in Washington ending 46 years of hostility and a week after 96 people were killed by a bomb in a Jewish community center in Buenos Aires. </P>
