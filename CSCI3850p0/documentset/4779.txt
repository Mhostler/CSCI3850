<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE></DATELINE>
<P> ``I can confirm that one American was killed immediately in this attack and another has since died of wounds suffered in the attack. A third person was wounded less seriously,'' the official said. The names of the Americans were being withheld pending notification of their families. </P>
<P> ``An investigation is underway and we won't have further comment until we have some additional details,'' the official said in a conference call with reporters. </P>
<P> The Americans were in a van en route from their residences to the consulate general in Karachi, the official said. </P>
