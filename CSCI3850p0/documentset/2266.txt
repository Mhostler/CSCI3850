<TITLE> Disgruntled worker holds Colonel, secretary, hostage</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Disgruntled worker holds Colonel, secretary, hostage</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-HOSTAGE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>FORT IRWIN, Calif.(Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  An armed man, believed to be a disgruntled ex-employee, was holding a colonel and a secretary hostage at the Fort Irwin Military Reservation National Guard base Thursday, authorities said. </P>
<P> A spokesman for the San Bernadino County Sheriff's Department said a Special Weapons and Tactics Team was headed to Fort Irwin, a sprawling army post 140 miles northeast of Los Angeles. </P>
<P> It was beleived the man had recently been fired from the base, spokesman Jim Bryant. </P>
