<TITLE> Venezuela teacher dies after classroom stabbing</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Venezuela teacher dies after classroom stabbing</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-VENEZUELA-STABBING </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>CARACAS (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A Venezuelan kindergarten teacher died after being stabbed seven times by a spurned former lover in front of 30 pupils, including her nine-year-old daughter, the official news agency Venpres said Tuesday. </P>
<P> Witnesses said Leticia Villegas, 40, crawled into the street outside her school in the southern city of Ciudad Bolivar, 375 miles south-east of Caracas, after the attack Monday. </P>
<P> She later died in hospital. </P>
<P> Police blamed the murder on taxi-driver Juan Merida, 45, who said Villegas had left him two weeks ago, Venpres reported. </P>
