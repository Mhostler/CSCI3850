<TITLE> Gunman kills one, wounds at least six at abortion clinics</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Gunman kills one, wounds at least six at abortion clinics</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-ABORTION-SHOOTING 2NDLD </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>BOSTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  One person was shot dead and at least six others were wounded Friday, when a gunman, dressed in black and wielding a rifle, opened fire on staff and patients at two separate abortion clinics in the Boston suburb of Brookline, police and eyewitnesses said. </P>
<P> Police and ambulances raced to the scenes of the shootings which occurred at the Planned Parenthood clinic and the Preterm Health Services clinic, located a short distance apart in the same street. The killing would be the fourth at abortion clinics in the U.S. </P>
