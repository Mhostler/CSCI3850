<TITLE> KURD REBELS SAY IRAN KILLS THEIR MAN IN BAGHDAD</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> KURD REBELS SAY IRAN KILLS THEIR MAN IN BAGHDAD</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-IRAN-IRAQ-KURDS </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>NICOSIA, Cyprus (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  An Iranian rebel Kurdish party said Iranian government agents assassinated its representative in Baghdad on Thursday. </P>
<P> ``Terrorists of the Islamic Republic of Iran'' assassinated central committee member Ghafour Hamzei in front of his Baghdad home Thursday afternoon, the Democratic Party of Iranian Kurdistan said in a statement sent from its Paris office. </P>
<P> The party, the biggest of several Kurdish groups fighting Tehran's Islamic government, gave no other details. </P>
<P> Two general-secretaries of the party were assassinated in Vienna in 1989 and in Berlin in 1992. It blamed Iranian agents. </P>
<P> Tehran denied the charge and said the killings were the result of political infighting among the rebels. </P>
