<TITLE> Iran says five held in past year for spying</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Iran says five held in past year for spying</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-IRAN-SPYING </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>TEHRAN, Iran (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Security forces in southern Iran have arrested five persons on spying charges in the past year, a newspaper reported Thursday. </P>
<P> Abrar daily quoted a security official in Hormuzgan province on the Gulf coast as saying an alleged intelligence officer for the Confederation of Independent States (CIS), an agent of Iraqi Kurdish parties and three others working for east Asian states were arrested in the Iranian year ending March 20. </P>
<P> The official named the alleged CIS agent as Makarov but did not give details on the nationality of the others. A Russian embassy spokesman in Tehran refused to comment on the report. </P>
