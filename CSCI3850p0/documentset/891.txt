<TITLE> CLINTON URGES NO DELAY ON HEALTH REFORM</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> CLINTON URGES NO DELAY ON HEALTH REFORM</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CLINTON-HEALTH </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  President Clinton Friday urged lawmakers bogged down in partisan bickering on health reform not to give up and to keep working for agreement. </P>
<P> ``I would say to them, keep working, keep working on it because if you delay, you may lose it altogether,'' Clinton told a White House news conference. </P>
