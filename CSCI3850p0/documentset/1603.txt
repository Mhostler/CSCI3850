<TITLE> Clinton calls for worldwide treaty on landmines</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Clinton calls for worldwide treaty on landmines</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CLINTON-UN-MINES </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>UNITED NATIONS (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  President Clinton Monday called for a global treaty to rid the world of 85 million landmines that kill thousands each year. </P>
<P> ``I am proposing a first step toward the eventual elimination of a less visible threat -- 85 million anti-personnel mines, one for every 50 people on the face of the earth,'' he said in his speech to the U.N. General Assembly. ``I ask all the nations to join with us and conclude an agreement,'' he added without giving further details. </P>
