<TITLE> U.S. court backs dismissal of homosexual midshipman</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> U.S. court backs dismissal of homosexual midshipman</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-MILITARY-GAYS </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A divided U.S. court of appeals Tuesday upheld the Naval Academy's dismissal of a midshipman who admitted he was a homosexual. </P>
<P> The U.S. Court of Appeals for the District of Columbia, on a 7-3 vote, said the academy acted properly in dismissing Joseph Steffan after he confirmed he was a homosexual, even though he never admitted he engaged in homosexual conduct. </P>
