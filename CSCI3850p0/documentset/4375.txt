<TITLE> U.S. official to visit Turkey</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> U.S. official to visit Turkey</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-TURKEY-USA </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>ANKARA (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  U.S. Assistant Secretary of State Richard Holbrooke is to visit Ankara on February 20 for a day of talks with the Turkish government, the U.S. embassy said in a statement Thursday. </P>
<P> He had been scheduled to visit to discuss the divided island of Cyprus last month but the trip was put off when his plane broke down in Athens. </P>
