<TITLE> Bid for indictment seen in Whitewater case - report</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Bid for indictment seen in Whitewater case - report</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CLINTON-WHITEWATER </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>LOS ANGELES (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Special counsel Kenneth Starr is expected to seek indictments soon against a number of key players in the financial scandal linked to President Clinton's investment in the Whitewater real estate development, the Los Angeles Times reported Thursday. </P>
<P> While there is no indication that Starr is considering bringing indictments against Clinton or First Lady Hillary Rodham Clinton, he is preparing charges against Arkansas Gov. Jim Guy Tucker and former U.S. Associate Attorney General Webster Hubbell, the newspaper said, citing sources close to the case. </P>
