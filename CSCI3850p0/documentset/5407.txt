<TITLE> French embassy says had bomb claim after crash</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> French embassy says had bomb claim after crash</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-ROMANIA-CRASH-CLAIM URGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>BUCHAREST, April 3 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The French embassy in Bucharest said on Monday an anonymous caller had claimed to have planted a bomb on board a Romanian Airbus which crashed on Friday, killing all 60 people on board. </P>
<P> ``The French embassy received a phone call late on Saturday from a man who spoke unaccented French and who claimed that he had placed a bomb on the Tarom flight to Brussels,'' press attache Joseph Casals told Reuters. He said the call was taken seriously enough to relay it to Romanian authorities. </P>
