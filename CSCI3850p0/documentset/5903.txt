<TITLE> Four tremors hit Philippines</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Four tremors hit Philippines</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-PHILIPPINES-TREMOR </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>MANILA, April 21 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Four successive tremors shook the central Philippines early on Friday but there were no immediate reports of injury and only minor damage, government volcanologists said. </P>
<P> The quakes ranged from intensity three to six on the Richter scale. The strongest was in Palo in Leyte province, about 590 km (370 miles) southeast of Manila. </P>
<P> The epicentre was in Borongan on the island of Samar. </P>
