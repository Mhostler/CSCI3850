<TITLE> Vatican says kidnapped nuns freed in Sierra Leone</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Vatican says kidnapped nuns freed in Sierra Leone</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-POPE-LEONE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>VATICAN CITY, March 21 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Seven Roman Catholic nuns kidnapped by rebels in Sierra Leone last January have been freed, the Vatican said on Tuesday. </P>
<P> Vatican spokesman Father Ciro Benedettini said the Holy See had received word that the nuns had been released but had no more immediate details. </P>
