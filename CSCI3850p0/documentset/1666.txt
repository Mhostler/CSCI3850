<TITLE> Hit-and-run victim dies as bystanders steal money</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Hit-and-run victim dies as bystanders steal money</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-KOREA-GREED </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>SEOUL, South Korea (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A woman knocked down by a taxi was left to die in the street while bystanders scrambled for money she dropped, newspapers reported Thursday. </P>
<P> The 32-year-old woman had been carrying about $2,900 to buy vegetables for her market stall when she was hit at a road crossing early Tuesday morning, the Korea Times and other national papers reported. </P>
<P> The Times quoted a witness as saying that about 20 bystanders, including other cab drivers, ignored the dying woman and rushed instead for her money while the culprit escaped. </P>
<P> The witness said some of the taxi drivers refused to take the woman to a hospital. </P>
<P> The Times report called the incident ``testimony to the naked selfishness of citizens in this society.'' </P>
