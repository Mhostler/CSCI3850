<TITLE> Prosecution seeks dismissal of Simpson jury pool</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Prosecution seeks dismissal of Simpson jury pool</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-SIMPSON-JURY </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>LOS ANGELES (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Prosecutors in the O.J. Simpson murder trial asked a judge Monday to dismiss the current pool of 77 prospective jurors, saying the group has been ``tainted'' by massive publicity surrounding the case. </P>
<P> Deputy District Attorney Marcia Clark proposed that the proceedings first focus on lengthy evidentiary hearings on crucial DNA testing before resuming the jury selection process with a new pool of panelists. </P>
