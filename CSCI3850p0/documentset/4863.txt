<TITLE> Seven people killed as rains provoke landslides in Brazil</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Seven people killed as rains provoke landslides in Brazil</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-BRAZIL-LANDSLIDE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>RIO DE JANEIRO (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Landslides caused by heavy rains in the Brazilian state of Sao Paulo killed a total of seven people, including four children buried while they slept, local television reported Saturday. </P>
<P> The four children, aged between six and 14, were killed in the town of Sao Vicente after mud engulfed their home at 5 a.m. </P>
<P> The children's mother and three other children managed to escape, Globo television said. </P>
<P> Rescue workers and neighbors worked throughout the day in the vain hope of finding any of the children alive but so far have recovered only one body. </P>
<P> In a separate incident elsewhere in the state, another three people -- a couple and their young child -- were also killed when their house was buried under mud, Globo said. </P>
