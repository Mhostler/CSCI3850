<TITLE> Cop sentenced to death over China traffic dispute</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Cop sentenced to death over China traffic dispute</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CHINA-MURDER </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>SHANGHAI, China (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A Chinese policeman who pumped six bullets into a peasant in a dispute over a minor traffic accident has been sentenced to death, the Wen Hui daily reported Thursday. </P>
<P> Policeman Fang Jinguang shot the peasant in the stomach and then emptied his revolver into the man's head and chest as he lay dying on a street in central Henan province, the newspaper said. </P>
<P> The peasant had punched the policeman in the head after the officer's motorbike had brushed against his 8-year-old son and injured the boy's hand. </P>
<P> Executions in China are usually carried out with a single bullet to the base of the skull. </P>
