<TITLE> Russian hijack plane refueled -- Tass</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Russian hijack plane refueled -- Tass</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CIS-RUSSIA-HIJACK-REFUELING </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>MOSCOW (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Officials at Russia's Makhachkala airport in the volatile north Caucasus region have started refueling a hijacked plane, meeting one demand from hijackers holding hostages on board, Itar-Tass news agency said Wednesday. </P>
<P> It quoted officials at the south Russian airport as saying talks were under way with the hijackers who demanded $2 million and safe passage to Iran. He said there was no information on whether the cash had been delivered. </P>
