<TITLE> Somali gunmen ambush U.N. staff, kidnap Somali</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Somali gunmen ambush U.N. staff, kidnap Somali</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-SOMALIA-AMBUSH </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>MOGADISHU, Somalia (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Gunmen ambushed U.N. staff in the Somali capital Wednesday, killing a Somali guard and kidnapping another Somali working for the U.N. refugee agency, U.N. officials said. </P>
<P> They said a British security officer, named as Bill Lindsay of the U.N. Development Program (UNDP), was injured in the hand and two Somali guards were seriously wounded in the ambush. </P>
