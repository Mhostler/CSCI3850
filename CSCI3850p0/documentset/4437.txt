<TITLE> Copper robbers killed by Paris ``silent'' train</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Copper robbers killed by Paris ``silent'' train</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-FRANCE-TRAIN </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>PARIS (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Three men stealing coils of copper wire along a railroad line were killed by a train south of Paris early Sunday, sources at the state railroad company said. </P>
<P> They were hit by a new, silent type of commuter train which they did not see or hear coming. </P>
<P> The men were apparently stealing coils left along the track by railroad workers replacing overhead wires. </P>
