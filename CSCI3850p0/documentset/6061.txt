<TITLE> Only five children believed missing in blast</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Only five children believed missing in blast</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-BLAST-CHILDREN </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>OKLAHOMA CITY (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Officials now believe only five more children may be lost in the wreckage of the bombed-out federal office building here, the Medical Examiner's Office said Wednesday. </P>
<P> Ray Blakeney, spokesman for the Oklahoma City Medical Examiner's Office, said 92 bodies have been recovered, including 14 children. He said 105 people are missing. </P>
