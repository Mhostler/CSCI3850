<TITLE> New York imposes smoking ban</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> New York imposes smoking ban</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-SMOKING-BAN </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>NEW YORK (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The New York City Council voted 36 to 8 Wednesday to approve a ban on smoking in most public places, despite a campaign against the ban by the tobacco lobby. </P>
<P> The ban, which is expected to be approved by the mayor, prohibits smoking in restaurants that seat more than 35, in outdoor stadiums and other public places. </P>
