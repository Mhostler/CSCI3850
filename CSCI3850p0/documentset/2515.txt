<TITLE> Judge blocks school expulsions of illegal immigrants</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Judge blocks school expulsions of illegal immigrants</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-ELECTION-CALIFORNIA-IMMIGRATION URGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>SAN FRANCISCO (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A judge Wednesday temporarily blocked enforcement of a provision of California's voter-approved anti-immigration inititiative that would have denied public education to illegal aliens and their children. </P>
<P> In a ruling in state court, Judge Stuart Pollack issued a temporary restraining order against the education component of Proposition 187, saying ``there are serious legal questions here that need to be resolved.'' </P>
