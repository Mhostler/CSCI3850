<TITLE> ISRAELI SOLDIER KILLED IN SOUTH LEBANON ATTACK</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> ISRAELI SOLDIER KILLED IN SOUTH LEBANON ATTACK</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-LEBANON-ISRAEL-SOLDIER </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>MARJAYOUN, Lebanon (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  One Israeli soldier was killed and another was wounded when pro-Iranian guerrillas rocketed their barracks in the Jewish state's occupation zone in south Lebanon Monday, security sources said. </P>
<P> They said the guerrillas fired rockets and artillery shells onto the barracks in the Rihane area on the edge of the border buffer zone. </P>
<P> The guerrillas also launched similar attacks against three smaller posts manned by Israeli troops or their South Lebanon Army (SLA) militia allies also on the tip of the zone. </P>
<P> Israeli and SLA gunners fired back artillery onto guerrilla-held hills to the north of the zone. </P>
<P> The pro-Iranian Hizbollah (Party of God) said in a statement in the south its fighters attacked the Rihane barracks ``inflicting several casualties among the enemy forces.'' </P>
