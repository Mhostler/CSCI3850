<TITLE> U.S.'s Gore arrives in Israel on Mideast swing</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> U.S.'s Gore arrives in Israel on Mideast swing</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-MIDEAST-GORE-ARRIVAL </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>JERUSALEM, March 23 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  U.S. Vice President Al Gore arrived in Israel from Saudi Arabia on Thursday for talks with Prime Minister Yitzhak Rabin, airport officials said. </P>
<P> Gore, whose Middle East swing has taken him to Egypt, Jordan, Oman and Saudi Arabia, travels to the Palestinian self-rule enclave of Jericho in the West Bank on Friday to meet PLO leader Yasser Arafat. </P>
