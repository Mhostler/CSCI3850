<TITLE> S.Korea test-fires surface-to-air missile</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> S.Korea test-fires surface-to-air missile</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-KOREA-MISSILE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>SEOUL, March 28 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  South Korea has successfully test-fired a short-range surface-to-air missile and will begin mass production in 1998, the defence ministry said on Tuesday. </P>
<P> A ministry spokesman said the Chonma missile was developed by the Agency for Defence Development (ADD) in cooperation with civilian defence contractors to intercept low-flying enemy aircraft. </P>
<P> The Chonma's launching pad and defence radar systems were manufactured using local technology and materials, he said. </P>
<P> The spokesman said the Chonma would be used along with 30-mm anti-aircraft guns to defend tank units and air bases. </P>
