<TITLE> Seagram to buy 80 percent stake in MCA</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Seagram to buy 80 percent stake in MCA</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-SEAGRAM-MCA </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>LOS ANGELES (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Seagram Co is close to buying an 80 percent stake in Hollywood entertainment giant MCA Inc for nearly $7 billion, industry sources and analysts said Thursday. </P>
<P> MCA is owned by Japanese electronics giant Matsushita Electric Industrial Co., which has been trying to get out of the investment. </P>
<P> Seagram is also close to selling its 24.1 percent stake in DuPont Co. back to the chemical company for about $10 billion. That deal is expected to be announced late Thursday. </P>
