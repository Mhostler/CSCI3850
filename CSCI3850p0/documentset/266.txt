<TITLE> IRA SAYS IT KILLED NORTHERN IRELAND WOMAN</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> IRA SAYS IT KILLED NORTHERN IRELAND WOMAN</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-IRISH-EXECUTION </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>BELFAST (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Irish Republican Army (IRA) guerrillas said on Sunday they killed a Northern Ireland woman suspected of being a police informer and dumped her body close to the border with Ireland. </P>
<P> In a statement issued to local media the IRA, fighting a 25-year guerrilla war to end British rule in the province, said it had executed the Belfast woman. </P>
<P> They claimed she was a police informer. </P>
<P> The body was found by a roadside in County Fermanagh, in the south of the British province. Villagers close to the scene said they heard gunshots in the early hours. </P>
<P> Police said they were checking for booby-traps before they removed the body. </P>
