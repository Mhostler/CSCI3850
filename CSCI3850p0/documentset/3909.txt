<TITLE> Human torch threat outside Buckingham Palace</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Human torch threat outside Buckingham Palace</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-BRITAIN-PALACE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>LONDON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A man threatening to set himself on fire at the gates of Buckingham Palace was arrested Tuesday, a police spokesman said. </P>
<P> The man, whose motives were not immediately clear, was detained after dousing himself with gasoline and holding a cigarette lighter above his head. </P>
<P> Earlier this month a man rammed a car into the gates in front of Buckingham Palace, home of Britain's Queen Elizabeth, heightening security concerns. </P>
