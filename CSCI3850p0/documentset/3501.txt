<TITLE> Caned U.S. teen-ager tops the news in Singapore</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Caned U.S. teen-ager tops the news in Singapore</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-SINGAPORE-POLL </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>SINGAPORE (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Michael Fay, the American teen-ager who was caned for vandalism, has been voted the top newsmaker in Singapore for 1994. </P>
<P> The Straits Times newspaper Saturday published the results of a poll of 19,770 readers. Fay grabbed more than 80 percent of the votes, the newspaper said. </P>
