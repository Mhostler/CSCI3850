<TITLE> Crane plunges to the ground, kills three people</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Crane plunges to the ground, kills three people</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CRANE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>LAUGHLIN, Nev (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A crane being used in the construction of a hotel tower crashed to the ground Wednesday, crushing three people to death, officials said. </P>
<P> The free-standing crane mounted on a truck was being used to take down the top section of a huge tower crane at the Riverside Resort and Casino when the accident happened, said Clark County Fire Department spokesman Bob Leinbach. </P>
<P> Both the free-standing crane and the top section of the tower crane crashed to the ground, falling across two vehicles in the parking lot below and killing their drivers, Leinbach said. </P>
<P> A third person walking across the parking lot was also killed, he said. </P>
<P> No one was injured, Leinbach said. </P>
<P> The crane was being used in the construction of a new hotel tower at the Riverside resort in the small gaming town of Laughlin, which is about 80 miles southeast of Las Vegas. </P>
