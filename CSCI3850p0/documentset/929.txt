<P> ``The plane disintegrated completely. We are afraid there were no survivors,'' the sources said, adding that the plane crashed at 2:53 p.m. EDT shortly after takeoff from Agadir airport. </P>
<P> An airport source said Royal Air Maroc domestic flight ATR 630 bound from Agadir to Casablanca ``was at 16,000 feet when it suddenly lost altitude and crashed at about 1900 GMT (3 p.m. </P>
<P> He said rescue teams had been rushed to the scene about 22 miles north of Agadir near the locality of Tizounine. </P>
<P> Officials at Agadir airport confirmed the plane had crashed but declined to give any details. </P>
