<P> ``Leo Delcroix came to me this morning and gave me a letter in which he offered his resignation,'' Dehaene told parliament. ``I have presented (his resignation) to the King who has accepted it.'' </P>
<P> The Belgian magazine Humo alleged earlier this week that Delcroix defrauded the tax authorities in connection with his French villa and had used ``black labor'' to build it. </P>
<P> Earlier this year Delcroix denied he owned a home in France but said Wednesday he had been deliberately vague about it so as not to compromise his chances in the European elections. </P>
<P> Delcroix became defense minister in March 1992. </P>
