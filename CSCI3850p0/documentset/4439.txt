<TITLE> US soldier held over murder of German taxi driver</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> US soldier held over murder of German taxi driver</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-GERMANY-USA-KILLING </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>KAISERSLAUTERN, Germany (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Police in the German city of Kaiserslautern arrested a U.S. soldier on suspicion of having murdered a taxi driver and stealing $20, police said Sunday. </P>
<P> A police statement said the 20-year-old soldier, born in Bogota, Colombia, and stationed with the U.S. Army in the southwest German town of Baumholder, had confessed. </P>
<P> The victim was stabbed about 20 times. </P>
<P> Police said the taxi driver, who picked up a passenger from the U.S. army base in the early hours of Saturday, was found dead by the roadside. </P>
<P> The American soldier was arrested after having been treated in a hospital for injuries to his arm. </P>
<P> ``The suspected culprit has since given a comprehensive confession,'' the police said. They did not name the suspect. </P>
