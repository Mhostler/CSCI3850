<TITLE> Colombia death squad suspected in street killings</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Colombia death squad suspected in street killings</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-COLOMBIA-KILLING </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>BOGOTA, Colombia (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A death squad that stalks street people at night in Colombia's capital was believed to be responsible for the shooting deaths of six people including a child early Sunday, a police official said. </P>
<P> The official said they had arrested two suspected members of the armed squad, which reportedly drove in a jeep around a downtown Bogota park after midnight hunting for the street people, who are often called ``disposables.'' </P>
<P> Police found the bodies in and around Martyrs Park, each of them with a bullet wound in the back of the head. </P>
<P> Such squads, often secretly formed by army and police, are known to operate in the country's major cities. </P>
<P> Local business owners often hire them to kill street people whom they blame for scaring away customers. </P>
