<TITLE> SENEGAL GRANTS ASYLUM TO OUSTED GAMBIAN PRESIDENT</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> SENEGAL GRANTS ASYLUM TO OUSTED GAMBIAN PRESIDENT</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-GAMBIA-PRESIDENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>DAKAR (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Ousted Gambian president Sir Dawda Jawara arrived in Dakar on a U.S. warship Sunday and Senegal said it had granted him political asylum. </P>
<P> ``Following the events of July 22, Senegal, true to its tradition of hospitality, has agreed to grant political asylum to Sir Dawda Jawara and his family,'' said a government statement broadcast by state radio. Young army officers ousted the 70-year-old leader in a bloodless coup Friday. </P>
