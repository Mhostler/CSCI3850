<TITLE> IMF approves second loan to Estonia</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> IMF approves second loan to Estonia</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-FUND-ESTONIA </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The International Monetary Fund Monday approved a second loan valued at about $16.9 million for Estonia under its Systemic Transformation Facility program for countries moving from communism to capitalism. </P>
<P> The IMF said Estonia had been highly successful over the past two years in stabilizing its economy, noting inflation had subsided to 1 to 2 percent per month and output was believed to have grown by about 5 percent in 1994. </P>
<P> The IMF approved Estonia's first loan under the STF on Oct. </P>
