<TITLE> Palace says Charles, Diana have no divorce plans</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Palace says Charles, Diana have no divorce plans</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-BRITAIN-ROYALS-DENIAL </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>LONDON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Buckingham Palace said Tuesday that Prince Charles and Princess Diana had no plans to divorce. </P>
<P> Reacting to press reports of a divorce settlement, a spokesman said: ``As was stated quite clearly when their separation was announced in December 1992, the prince and princess had no plans to divorce. That remains the position.'' </P>
<P> Lawyers for the heir to the throne and his estranged wife also issued a statement saying there was ``no truth'' in reports of a pending divorce agreement involving the couple. The French magazine Voici said the royal couple had agreed a $24.5 million divorce settlement for next year. </P>
<P> The couple married in 1981 and have two sons. </P>
