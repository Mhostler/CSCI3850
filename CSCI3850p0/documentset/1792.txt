<TITLE> Brazil's Cardoso has first-round victory - poll</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Brazil's Cardoso has first-round victory - poll</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-BRAZIL-ELECTION-EXIT 1STLD URGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>SAO PAULO (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Fernando Henrique Cardoso was headed for an easy first-round victory in Brazil's presidential election, exit poll results showed Monday. </P>
<P> The results of the poll conducted by a private research firm, Ibope, showed Cardoso, a free-market advocate, headed for a victory of about seven percentage points over the combined total of all his opponents. </P>
