<TITLE> Gen. Shelton says top four Haitian ``thugs'' arrested</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Gen. Shelton says top four Haitian ``thugs'' arrested</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-HAITI-USA-SHELTON URGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>NEW YORK (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The commander of United States forces in Haiti said Monday that four of the Caribbean nation's ``top thugs'' were in custody, but efforts to disarm all the country's paramilitary force, known as ``attaches,'' are going slower than planned. </P>
<P> Lieutenant General Hugh Shelton told CBS-TV's ``This Morning'' that: ``Here in (Port-au-Prince) the last couple of days we've picked up four of the top thugs or Ninjas or attaches that we were looking for on the street.'' </P>
