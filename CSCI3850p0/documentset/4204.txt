<TITLE> Clinton meets with baseball players, owners</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Clinton meets with baseball players, owners</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-BASEBALL-MEETING </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  President Clinton is meeting with striking baseball players and owners in an attempt to resolve their labor dispute, the White House said Tuesday. </P>
<P> The meeting began shortly after 6 p.m. EST. Clinton called for the meeting after the two sides failed to reach a settlement by the midafternoon deadline he set Monday. A White House spokesman said Clinton was ``exasperated'' by the stalemate. </P>
