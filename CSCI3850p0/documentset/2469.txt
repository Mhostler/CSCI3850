<TITLE> Israel's Rabin, PLO's Arafat meet on elections</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Israel's Rabin, PLO's Arafat meet on elections</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-MIDEAST-MEETING-START </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>EREZ, Gaza Strip (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Israeli Prime Minister Yitzhak Rabin and PLO chairman Yasser Arafat began a meeting in the Gaza Strip Tuesday, hoping to clear the way for Palestinians to take more control of their daily lives. </P>
<P> The leaders, co-winners of this year's Nobel Peace Prize, planned a two-hour meeting to discuss issues holding up their agreement on Palestinian elections and redeployment of Israeli troops in the occupied West Bank due before the balloting. </P>
