<TITLE> Three guerrillas reported killed in south Lebanon</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Three guerrillas reported killed in south Lebanon</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-LEBANON-SOUTH </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>MARJAYOUN, Lebanon (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Three guerrillas were killed north of Israel's south Lebanon occupation zone Tuesday when pro-Israeli militiamen opened fire on them, pro-Israeli militia sources said. </P>
<P> The South Lebanon Army (SLA) militia sources said the guerrillas were killed when SLA gunners fired heavy machineguns at them in the Jabal Rafi'i area, a rugged ridge used by pro-Iranian Hizbollah fighters to raid the border zone. </P>
<P> Hizbollah (Party of God) said in a statement in Beirut its fighters clashed with SLA forces attempting to push out of the border buffer zone at Mlikh near Jabal Rafi'i but it did not mention any casualties. </P>
<P> Hizbollah and other groups attack the zone almost daily. </P>
