<TITLE> Diplomats abandon attempt to reach Sarajevo</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Diplomats abandon attempt to reach Sarajevo</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-YUGOSLAVIA-ENVOYS URGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>SARAJEVO, April 22 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  U.S. and German peace envoys abandoned their attempt to travel from Sarajevo airport into the city on Saturday after Bosnian Serbs objected to their trip, U.N. special envoy Yasushi Akashi said. </P>
<P> ``The diplomats will leave with me and return to Zagreb,'' said Akashi. ``The whole situation is one of deep regret.'' The diplomats were forced to spend the night at the airport terminal because of Bosnian Serb protests at their arrival. </P>
