<TITLE> Canadian Minister to accompany Aristide to Haiti</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Canadian Minister to accompany Aristide to Haiti</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-HAITI-CANADA </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>OTTAWA (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Canadian Foreign Minister Andre Ouellet will accompany President Jean-Bertrand Aristide when he returns to Haiti from exile Saturday, the Canadian government said Friday. </P>
<P> Canada has strongly supported the restoration of democracy in Haiti and Aristide once studied in Montreal. </P>
<P> A small Canadian delegation will be on the plane, due to leave from Washington, D.C., a Canadian government spokesman said. </P>
<P> The American delegation will be led by Secretary of State Warren Christopher. </P>
