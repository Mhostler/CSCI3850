<TITLE> Judge to allow conjugal visits for sequestered jurors</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Judge to allow conjugal visits for sequestered jurors</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-SIMPSON-SEX </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>LOS ANGELES (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The judge in the O.J. Simpson murder trial, responding to pleas from several jurors, said Wednesday he would allow conjugal visits for members of the sequestered panel. </P>
<P> During questioning of jurors who will decide the football legend's fate, Judge Lance Ito said he had ordered the sheriff's department to arrange for spouses to visit their loves ones who are being kept under guard for the duration of the trial. </P>
