<TITLE> UK government wins key EU budget vote</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> UK government wins key EU budget vote</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-BRITAIN-VOTE URGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>LONDON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Prime Minister John Major's government Monday won a crucial parliamentary vote to increase Britain's contribution to the European Union budget. </P>
<P> The government, which said it would resign and call a general election if it were defeated, won by 330 votes to 303. </P>
