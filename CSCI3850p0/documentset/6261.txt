<TITLE> Moderate earthquake hits central Japan</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Moderate earthquake hits central Japan</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-QUAKE-JAPAN </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>TOKYO, May 4 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  An earthquake measuring 4.3 on the open-ended Richter Scale on Thursday shook the area of Japan that was flattened by a giant tremor in January, the Meteorological Agency said. </P>
<P> No damage or casualties were reported, and there was no tsunami (tidal wave) warning. </P>
<P> The earthquake occurred at 5.42 p.m. (0842 GMT), with an epicentre 20 km (12 miles) below the seabed of Osaka Bay. </P>
<P> A meteorological agency spokesman said it was an aftershock of the January 17 earthquake that killed more than 5,500 people in the area around Kobe. </P>
