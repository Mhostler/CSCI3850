<TITLE> CLINTON TAKES A TUMBLE DURING MORNING JOG</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> CLINTON TAKES A TUMBLE DURING MORNING JOG</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CLINTON-JOG </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  President Clinton took a bit of a tumble Saturday during his regular morning jog and scraped a knee and an elbow. </P>
<P> ``He just missed his step,'' said White House spokeswoman Dee Dee Myers. ``I think he is going to have a bruised knee.'' </P>
<P> Unhurt, the president completed his roughly three-mile run that took him around the Lincoln Memorial. </P>
