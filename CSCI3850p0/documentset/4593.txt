<TITLE> Somalia's future up to Somalia - Christopher</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Somalia's future up to Somalia - Christopher</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-SOMALIA-USA </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Somalia's future is now in the hands of its people, Secretary of State Warren Christopher said Monday as U.S. and Italian marines prepared to withdraw U.N. troops from the African country at the close of a bloody, failed mission. </P>
<P> ``The future is really in the hands of the people of Somalia,'' he told reporters during a picture-taking session with Italian Foreign Minister Susanna Agnelli. </P>
