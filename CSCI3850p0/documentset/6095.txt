<TITLE> Israeli soldiers kill Arab near Jerusalem - radio</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Israeli soldiers kill Arab near Jerusalem - radio</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-MIDEAST-SHOOTING </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>JERUSALEM (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Israeli soldiers shot dead a Palestinian at a roadblock on the outskirts of Jerusalem on Thursday, Israel Radio said. </P>
<P> It was not immediately clear why the soldiers opened fire on the 50-year-old Palestinian, who was transporting Arab workers, the radio said. </P>
<P> The army said it was checking the report. </P>
