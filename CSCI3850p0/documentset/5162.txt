<TITLE> British army to end night-time patrols in Belfast</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> British army to end night-time patrols in Belfast</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-IRISH-PATROLS </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>BELFAST, March 24 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The British army will cease all routine patrols in Belfast from midnight, police said on Friday. </P>
<P> The announcement is fresh evidence of the reduced security regime in Northern Ireland as a result of ceasefires declared last autumn by rival Irish nationalist and Unionist guerrilla groups. The army halted daytime patrols in Belfast last month. </P>
