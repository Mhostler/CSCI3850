<TITLE> Two Canadians arrested for sabotaging train</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Two Canadians arrested for sabotaging train</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CANADA-TRAIN </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>OTTAWA (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Two Canadians aged 21 and 15 were arrested for sabotaging an express train that burst into flames, injuring 45 passengers, police said Wednesday. </P>
<P> Police Sergeant Gail Webster said the two men confessed to placing a loose piece of rail across the tracks that punctured the engine's fuel tank and engulfed the front of the train in a ball of fire at Brighton, Ontario, Sunday. </P>
