<TITLE> Clinton may go to Middle East for peace signing</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Clinton may go to Middle East for peace signing</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-MIDEAST-JORDAN-ISRAEL-CLINTON </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  President Clinton may go to the Middle East for the signing ceremony of a peace accord between Israel and Jordan, his chief of staff said Monday. </P>
<P> ``We understand that there may be an invitation in order,'' Leon Panetta said on NBC Television's ``Today Show.'' ''Obviously the president would look on that very seriously.''  He spoke as Israel and Jordan prepared to initial a peace treaty ending their 46-year state of war after two days of talks. </P>
<P> A formal signing ceremony would be held at a later date after some final details are worked out. </P>
