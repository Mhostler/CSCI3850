<TITLE> SOMALIS KILL SEVEN INDIAN U.N. PEACEKEEPERS, WOUND SIX</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> SOMALIS KILL SEVEN INDIAN U.N. PEACEKEEPERS, WOUND SIX</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-SOMALIA-ATTACKS URGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>MOGADISHU, Somalia (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Seven Indian U.N. peacekeepers were killed and six others were wounded in Somalia when militia attacked them with anti-aircraft guns, mortars and small arms on Monday, a U.N. military spokesman said. </P>
