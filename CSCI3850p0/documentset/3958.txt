<TITLE> New York rabbi detained in Auschwitz protest</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> New York rabbi detained in Auschwitz protest</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-AUSCHWITZ-DETAIN </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>OSWIECIM, Poland (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A militant New York rabbi was detained by Polish police Wednesday after refusing to leave a church he had been occupying in a protest next to the former Nazi death camp of Birkenau. </P>
<P> Avi Weiss was dragged from the church after spending seven hours inside. He had been protesting the presence of the church at the place where more than a million Jews were murdered and had threatened to stay during events marking the 50th anniversary of the liberation of the Auschwitz-Birkenau camp complex. </P>
