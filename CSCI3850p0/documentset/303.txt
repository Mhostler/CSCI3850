<TITLE> TIRE FIRE FORCES EVACUATIONS IN INDIANA</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> TIRE FIRE FORCES EVACUATIONS IN INDIANA</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-USA-TIRES </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>EAST CHICAGO, Ind (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A fire in a tire warehouse that has been smoldering for four days Tuesday sent up plumes of hazardous smoke and forced the evacuation of an industrial neighborhood. </P>
<P> One firefighter has been seriously injured battling the blaze that began in this industrial Indiana city, some 20 miles southeast of Chicago. </P>
<P> The fire department evacuated a 16-block area as shifting winds spread smoke over the immediate industrial area and a nearby residential neighborhood in this Lake Michigan town. </P>
<P> Firefighters were still trying to determine the toxicity of the debris cast off by the fire in the four-story building which was filled to capacity with tires. The facility was used to shred, process and store tires. </P>
<P> The cause of the blaze at is under investigation, a fire department spokesman said. </P>
