<TITLE> Bosnian Serbs release remaining 187 U.N. hostages</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Bosnian Serbs release remaining 187 U.N. hostages</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-YUGOSLAVIA-PEACEKEEPERS </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>SARAJEVO, Bosnia-Herzegovina (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Bosnian Serb forces have released the remaining 187 U.N. peacekeepers they had detained last month in retaliation for NATO air strikes, a U.N. official said Saturday. </P>
<P> ``They have been released,'' said spokesman Col. Jan-Dirk Merveldt. </P>
