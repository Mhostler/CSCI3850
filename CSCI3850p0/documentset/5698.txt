<TITLE> Actor-singer Burl Ives near death, friend says</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Actor-singer Burl Ives near death, friend says</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-IVES </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>SEATTLE (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Actor and folksinger Burl Ives, 85, fell into a coma Wednesday at his home in Anacortes, Wash., and was not expected to survive, a family friend said. </P>
<P> The bearded, bear-like Ives is best known for his popular renditions of classic American folk and children's songs and for powerful dramatic roles in movies such as ``The Big Country'' and ``Cat on a Hot Tin Roof.'' </P>
