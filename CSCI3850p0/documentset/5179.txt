<TITLE> WYSE signs $50 mln terminal deal with China firm</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> WYSE signs $50 mln terminal deal with China firm</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CHINA-TERMINALS </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>BEIJING, March 25 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  U.S. computer firm WYSE Technology has signed a $50 million cooperation agreement with Beijing University's Founder Group to manufacture computer terminals and monitors, the China Daily said. </P>
<P> The pact, signed in Beijing on Friday, includes advanced technology transfer to China and setting up of a research and development centre as well as manufacture of terminals for export and the Chinese market, it said. </P>
<P> The Beijing University Founder group is the China sales agent for WYSE, which introduced Chinese-English terminals and related products in 1994, it said. </P>
