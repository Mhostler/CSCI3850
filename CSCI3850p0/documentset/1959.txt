<TITLE> Second Israeli soldier missing, security sources say</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Second Israeli soldier missing, security sources say</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-MIDEAST-ISRAEL-SEARCH </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>JERUSALEM (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Another Israeli soldier has been reported missing and the army is investigating whether he also has been kidnapped by Palestinian guerrillas, security sources said Wednesday. </P>
<P> The soldier, identified by the sources as Yoram Pollack, was a reservist who left his army base in the occupied West Bank Tuesday but never made it to his home in southern Israel. </P>
<P> Another Israeli soldier, Nachshon Waxman, kidnapped Sunday, was being held by the Islamic resistance movement Hamas, which threatened to kill him unless Israel releases up to 200 Islamic and PLO prisoners by Friday evening. </P>
<P> Waxman was abducted while hitchiking in Israel. It was not immediately known if Pollack also had been hitchiking home. </P>
