<TITLE> Building blast and blaze injures 18 people</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Building blast and blaze injures 18 people</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-EXPLOSION 1STLD </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES>(Updates number injured)</NOTES>
<DATELINE>NEW YORK (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  An explosion shook a five-story building in Manhattan injuring 18 people, a fire department spokesman said Tuesday. </P>
<P> Spokesman George Semidey said the blast occurred in Manhattan's Murray Hill neighborhood - not far from the Empire State Building - at 11:10 p.m. EST Monday. </P>
<P> The blast set off a fire that took more than hour to bring under control, Semidey said. </P>
<P> Two civilians were injured in the blast. Some 16 firefighters were also hurt - with five needing to be hospitalized at New York Hospital-Cornell Medical Center's burn unit. </P>
<P> There was no immediate information on the cause of the blast. </P>
