<TITLE> U.S. defense secretary to visit Haiti</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> U.S. defense secretary to visit Haiti</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-HAITI-USA-PERRY </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Defense Secretary William Perry and the chairman of the military Joint Chiefs of Staff will visit Haiti Saturday to meet with U.S. forces there, defense officials said Friday. </P>
<P> The officials, who asked not to be identified, said Perry and General John Shalikashvili will fly to Port-au-Prince early Saturday and return to Washington that night. They will meet with senior U.S. military officers and troops in Haiti, part of a force of some 15,000 Americans that will soon be in the Caribbean nation to restore deposed President Jean-Bertrand Aristide to power. </P>
