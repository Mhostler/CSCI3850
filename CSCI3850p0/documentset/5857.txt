<TITLE> Gore urges indefinite extension of NPT</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Gore urges indefinite extension of NPT</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-NUCLEAR-GORE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>UNITED NATIONS (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Vice President Al Gore Wednesday urged 178 countries to seize a ``one-time option'' and extend the nuclear Non-Proliferation Treaty (NPT) indefinitely. </P>
<P> Gore made his appeal at a United Nations conference called to decide whether to prolong the 25-year-old treaty -- a cornerstone of the struggle to prevent atomic war -- forever or for a limited period. </P>
