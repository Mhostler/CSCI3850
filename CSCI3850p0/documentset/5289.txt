<TITLE> Egypt hangs two for attempted murder of novelist</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Egypt hangs two for attempted murder of novelist</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-EGYPT-HANGING </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>CAIRO, March 29 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Egypt hanged two Moslem militants on Wednesday for the attempted murder of Nobel prize-winning author Naguib Mafouz, security sources said. </P>
<P> They said Mohamed Nagi Mohamed Mustafa, who stabbed the 83-year-old author in the neck, and accomplice Mohamed al-Mahlawi were executed in a Cairo prison. </P>
