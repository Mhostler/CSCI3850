<TITLE> IBM halts shipments of Pentium-based PCs</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> IBM halts shipments of Pentium-based PCs</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-COMPUTERS-PENTIUM </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>SOMERS, NY (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  International Business Machines Corp. said Monday it was halting shipments of Pentium-based personal computers because of flaws in the Intel Corp. chips. </P>
<P> IBM said the risk of error in the high-speed computer chips may be higher than Intel has described. Intel, the world's largest chipmaker, said recently that its Pentium chips had flaws but said any risk of error was exceedingly small, affecting only advanced mathematical calculations. </P>
