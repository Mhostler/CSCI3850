<SUBJECT> BC-PAPUA-CRASH -2 SYDNEY </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE></DATELINE>
<P> The aircraft disappeared shortly after take-off at lunchtime Saturday. It was bound for Selbang, a village 25 miles east of Tabubil and the home of most of the passengers, the emergency officials in Papua New Guinea said. </P>
<P> Rescuers found the aircaft in dense mountainous jungle. There were no survivors. </P>
<P> A spokesman for Ok Tedi Mining Ltd.said police had not yet released the names of the victims and it was not clear if any mine employees were on board. </P>
<P> Emergency officials in Papua New Guinea said it was the worst aircraft crash in the country since 1948. </P>
