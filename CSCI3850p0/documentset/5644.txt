<TITLE> American, Carol Bellamy, chosen as UNICEF head</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> American, Carol Bellamy, chosen as UNICEF head</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-UN-UNICEF-BELLAMY </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>UNITED NATIONS (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Carol Bellamy, the director of the U.S. peace corps and a former New York City Council president, was chosen as head of the U.N. Children's Fund (UNICEF), the United Nations announced on Monday. </P>
<P> In selecting Bellamy, Secretary-General Boutros Boutros-Ghali turned down several European candidates, notably Elizabeth Rehm, the former Finnish defense minister. The European Union had made a strong bid for the post, traditionally held by an American. </P>
