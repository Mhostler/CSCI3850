<TITLE> Canada to send 120 peacekeepers to Rwanda</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Canada to send 120 peacekeepers to Rwanda</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-RWANDA-CANADA-TROOPS </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>OTTAWA (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Canada said Monday it will send 120 troops to Rwanda this month to supply logistical support to the United Nations peacekeeping force based there. </P>
<P> The peacekeepers are going in response to a request from the United Nations that Canada maintain a presence in Rwanda, Defense Minister David Collenette said in a statement. </P>
<P> The Canadian government also said it is bringing back to Canada a group of 360 communication specialists who were sent to Rwanda in June on a six-month mission. </P>
