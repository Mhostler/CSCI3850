<TITLE> India policeman kills boy for disturbing sleep</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> India policeman kills boy for disturbing sleep</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-INDIA-SIESTA </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>NEW DELHI, May 16 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  An irritated policeman in the north Indian state of Uttar Pradesh killed an 11-year-old boy who disturbed his siesta, the Press Trust of India (PTI) said on Tuesday. </P>
<P> PTI said police officials arrested Constable Satendra Singh Yadav for throwing Santosh and his friend Rakesh off a building in the city of Kanpur on Tuesday after the boys created a racket and woke Yadav from his afternoon nap. </P>
<P> Santosh died instantly while Rakesh was admitted to hospital with injuries, PTI said. </P>
