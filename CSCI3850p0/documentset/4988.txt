<HEADLINE> Inmate gulps razor blades in suicide bid</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-ARGENTINA-RAZOR </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>BUENOS AIRES (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  An inmate in an Argentine jail attempted suicide by swallowing two razor blades, prison officials said Friday. </P>
<P> The prisoner, identified as Santiago Landaburu, was taken to a hospital. There was no word on his condition. </P>
