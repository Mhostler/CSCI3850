<TITLE> Clintons soak up Moroccan culture at school banquet</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Clintons soak up Moroccan culture at school banquet</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CLINTON-CHELSEA </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  President Clinton soaked up some Moroccan culture without leaving Washington Friday night. </P>
<P> He and Hillary Rodham Clinton and their daughter Chelsea attended a ``Medieval Banquet in the Alhambra Palace'' at Chelsea's school, Sidwell Friends. </P>
<P> The event, planned with help from Moroccan ambassador to the United States Mohammed Benaissa, included Mediterranean music and a cast of regional characters from the Medieval past ranging from Moors to Spanish caliphs. </P>
<P> Clinton was celebrating the midpoint of his four-year term. He was inaugurated Jan. 20, 1993. </P>
