<TITLE> Clinton retreats on border fee proposal</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Clinton retreats on border fee proposal</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-BORDERS </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  President Clinton Wednesday backpeddled on a proposal to impose a fee on border crossings from Canada and Mexico, making the plan a ``local option.'' </P>
<P> ``Rather than propose a fee for all border areas, (Clinton) wants to let individual states on the border, working with local communities, decide whether the fee should be imposed,'' Budget Director Alice Rivlin said in a letter to Congress. </P>
