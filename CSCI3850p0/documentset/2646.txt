<TITLE> U.S. vice president says GATT must not be delayed</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> U.S. vice president says GATT must not be delayed</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-TRADE-GATT-GORE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Vice President Al Gore said on Wednesday the upcoming congressional vote on a new world trade accord ``is a critical vote for U.S. leadership in the world'' and must not be delayed. </P>
<P> ``Two weeks from now we reach a pivotal point,'' Gore said at a news conference boosting the new GATT trade pact. ``The Congress will decide whether the United States will continue to lead the world on global economic issues, or not. That's really what it comes down to.'' </P>
