<TITLE> EX-CLINTON AIDE NUSSBAUM DEFENDS WHITEWATER TALKS</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> EX-CLINTON AIDE NUSSBAUM DEFENDS WHITEWATER TALKS</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-WHITEWATER-NUSSBAUM </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Former White House counsel Bernard Nussbaum Thursday defended his contacts with Treasury officials over a government investigation related to the Whitewater affair. </P>
<P> Nussbaum, who resigned last March under pressure after disclosures about the contacts, told the House Banking Committee: ``In my view ... these meetings were proper.'' </P>
