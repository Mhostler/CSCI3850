<TITLE> U.N. threatens Bosnian Serbs with air strikes</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> U.N. threatens Bosnian Serbs with air strikes</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-YUGOSLAVIA-THREAT URGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>SARAJEVO, Bosnia-Herzegovina (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The United Nations signalled a major change in policy in Bosnia saying it was ready to ask for NATO air strikes against Bosnian Serb artillery which targets civilians inside ``safe areas.'' </P>
<P> ``Attacks which come from outside a safe area and which deliberately target civilians will meet a resolute response from us including the use of air power,'' said Colum Murphy, a spokesman for Lt. Gen. Rupert Smith, the U.N. commander in Bosnia. </P>
