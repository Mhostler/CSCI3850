<TITLE> Clinton naming panel to study Gulf War syndrome</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Clinton naming panel to study Gulf War syndrome</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CLINTON-VETERANS </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  President Clinton will name a panel of doctors, scientists and military veterans to try to find a cause and treatment for mysterious maladies afflicting some who served in the Gulf War, the White House said Monday. </P>
<P> White House spokesman Mike McCurry said Clinton planned to announce the move in a speech to the mid-winter meeting of the Veterans of Foreign Wars at 11 a.m. EST. </P>
