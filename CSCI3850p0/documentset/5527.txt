<TITLE> One killed, three injured in NYC building collapse</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> One killed, three injured in NYC building collapse</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-COLLAPSE 1STLD </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES>(Updates with death of construction worker)</NOTES>
<DATELINE>NEW YORK (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A five-story building under demolition collapsed early Thursday, killing a construction worker who fell onto a ramp to the nearby Lincoln Tunnel and injuring three other people. </P>
<P> The 32-year-old unidentified man died about six hours after the accident. Two other injured construction workers were in stable condition. A woman who was passing by the site and stopped to help also was taken to a local hospital. </P>
<P> Workers were demolishing the narrow building when a side wall and a rear wall collapsed, officials said. </P>
<P> The cause of the collapse was unknown, said Buildings Department spokesman Vahe Tiryakian. </P>
<P> The building was located at 38th Street and Ninth Avenue, near the Lincoln Tunnel which runs under the Hudson River and connects New York City and New Jersey. </P>
