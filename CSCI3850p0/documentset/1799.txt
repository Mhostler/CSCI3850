<TITLE> Clinton welcomes Mandela to White House</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Clinton welcomes Mandela to White House</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-SAFRICA-MANDELA-ARRIVAL </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  President Clinton welcomed South African President Nelson Mandela to the White House Tuesday, saluting his ``stunning achievement'' in transforming his country from apartheid to a multi-racial democracy. </P>
<P> ``Now all over the world, there are three words which, spoken together, express the triumph of freedom, democracy and hope for the future. They are 'President Nelson Mandela,''' Clinton said, greeting Mandela on the White House South Lawn. </P>
