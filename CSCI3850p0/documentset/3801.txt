<TITLE> Quake collapses buildings in central Japan</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Quake collapses buildings in central Japan</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-QUAKE-JAPAN 3RDLD </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES>  (Updates throughout, adds byline)</NOTES>
<DATELINE>TOKYO (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  At least two people died and dozens were injured when a powerful earthquake rolled through central Japan Tuesday morning, collapsing buildings and setting off fires in the cities of Kobe and Osaka. </P>
<P> The Japan Meteorological Agency said the earthquake, which measured 7.2 on the open-ended Richter scale, rumbled across Honshu Island from the Pacific Ocean to the Japan Sea. </P>
<P> The worst hit areas were the port city of Kobe and the nearby island of Awajishima where in both places dozens of fires broke out and up to 50 buildings, including several appartment blocks, collapsed. </P>
