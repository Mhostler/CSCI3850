<TITLE> Judges imposes fines on two Simpson attorneys</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Judges imposes fines on two Simpson attorneys</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-SIMPSON-FINES (NEWSBREAK) </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>LOS ANGELES (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The judge in the O.J. Simpson trial Friday imposed fines of $950 each on two of the football legend's attorneys for late disclosure of evidence that prosecutors said raised questions about the credibility of a key defense witness. </P>
<P> At the end of Friday's court session, Judge Lance Ito ordered lead defense lawyer Johnnie Cochran and attorney Carl Douglas to pay the fines. Ito accused Cochran of making ``untrue representations to the court in reckless disregard for the truth.'' </P>
