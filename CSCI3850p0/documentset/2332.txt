<TITLE> China province selects best toilets</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> China province selects best toilets</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CHINA-TOILET </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>BEIJING (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The central China province of Anhui has held a competition to select its best 14 public toilets, the Economic Information Daily said Tuesday. </P>
<P> It said the provincial government appointed a group of 20 experts to make the selection on the basis of design, external appearance, brightness and method of waste disposal. </P>
<P> It said towns and cities all over Anhui put forward their best candidates and 29 were chosen as ``outstanding toilets.'' The final 14 were selected from these. </P>
<P> The city of Fuyang had three of the winning 14 and its municipal government has awarded the three prizes of $1,180, the paper added. </P>
<P> China's public toilets are notorious for their filth and bad smell and are a constant target of public criticism. </P>
