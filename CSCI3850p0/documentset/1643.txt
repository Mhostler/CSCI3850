<TITLE> Sunken ferry's ramp not closed, survivor says</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Sunken ferry's ramp not closed, survivor says</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-FINLAND-FERRY-RAMP </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>STOCKHOLM (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A crew member who survived the Finnish ferry disaster in which more than 700 people are feared to have died in the Baltic Sea said one of the forward loading ramps was not shut properly, the Swedish news agency TT reported. </P>
<P> Crew member Henrik Sillaste told TT water rushed in through the open space and the boat began to sway. </P>
<P> Sillaste, an Estonian, told TT: ``Water rushed in up to my calves. Then the boat began to sway.'' </P>
<P> He said the water poured in through one of the ramps which cars and trucks used to enter and leave the roll-on roll-off Estonia ferry. </P>
<P> The vessel sank early Wednesday, and hundreds are feared to have died. </P>
