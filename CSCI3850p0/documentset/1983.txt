<TITLE> Decibel factor and the silence of the fans</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Decibel factor and the silence of the fans</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-HONGKONG-GLOVES </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>HONG KONG (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Thousands of fans at a Hong Kong pop concert will don gloves to applaud their idols Sunday to keep down the noise after complaints by local residents, the South China Morning Post reported Thursday. </P>
<P> Organizers will hand out 17,500 pairs of white gloves at a government-sponsored concert and will urge fans to cut down on screaming and yelling in the new Hong Kong Stadium after protests over the din at previous concerts, the newspaper said. </P>
<P> Neighbors complained that the noise broke the legal 70-decibel limit, even though surveys show jackhammers and everyday street noise are often above acceptable levels. </P>
