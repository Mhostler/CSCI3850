<TITLE> Bundesbank interest rates</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Bundesbank interest rates</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-GERMANY-BUNDESBANK-RATES URGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>FRANKFURT, March 30 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The Bundesbank said on Thursday it was cutting its discount rate to four percent from 4.50 percent, effective from this Friday. </P>
<P> The surprise news gave the dollar an immediate boost on foreign exchange markets. </P>
<P> The German central bank also announced that it was reducing its key money market rate, the repo, to 4.50 percent from 4.85 percent from next week. </P>
