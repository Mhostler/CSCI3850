<TITLE> Tennis-Muster wins Mexican Open</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Tennis-Muster wins Mexican Open</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-TENNIS-MEXICAN </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>MEXICO CITY (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Austrian Thomas Muster, the second seed, won his third consecutive Mexican Open title when he beat unseeded Brazilian Fernando Meligeni 7-6 (7-4), 7-5 Sunday. </P>
<P> Muster won his 24th career title, 23 of them on clay, and has won 17 consecutive clay court finals in which he has played, stretching back to May 1990. </P>
<P> Meligeni was appearing in his first ATP Tour final. </P>
<P> This was the first Tour event of 1995 played on clay. </P>
