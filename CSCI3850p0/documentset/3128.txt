<TITLE> Algeria says troops killed six Muslim guerrillas</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Algeria says troops killed six Muslim guerrillas</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-ALGERIA-VIOLENCE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>TUNIS, Tunisia (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Algerian security forces fatally shot six Muslim rebels during a search operation in the western town of Oued Berkeche, the Algerian news agency APS said Sunday. </P>
<P> Three of those killed in Oued Berkeche, 277 miles west of Algiers, were from the neighboring town of Sidi-Bel-Abbes and were suspected of involvement in several murders, said APS, quoting a statement by security officials. </P>
<P> The clash in Oued Berkeche took place Friday. </P>
<P> More than 400 Muslim militants have been killed by security forces since the begining of November, according to official statements. </P>
<P> Between 10,000 and 20,000 people have been slain in Algeria's political violence since authorities cancelled a January 1992 general election which Muslims were poised to win. </P>
