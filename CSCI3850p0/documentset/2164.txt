<TITLE> Baseball-Padres name Bruce Bochy manager</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Baseball-Padres name Bruce Bochy manager</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-BASEBALL-PADRES </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>SAN DIEGO, Calif.(Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The San Diego Padres Friday named third base coach Bruce Bochy as their new manager after Jim Riggleman accepted the managerial position with the Chicago Cubs. </P>
<P> Riggleman, who managed the Padres for the past three seasons, was named the 10th Cubs manager in as many years earlier Friday. </P>
<P> The 39-year-old Bochy spent the last two seasons as San Diego's third base coach. </P>
<P> Bochy was a successful minor league coach from 1988 until 1992 when he led the Padres Double-A farm club in Wichita to the Texas League championship. </P>
<P> Bochy had a career .239 batting average in nine years in the major leagues. </P>
