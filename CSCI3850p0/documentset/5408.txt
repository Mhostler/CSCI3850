<TITLE> Romanian investigators suspect explosion on Airbus</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Romanian investigators suspect explosion on Airbus</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-ROMANIA-CRASH-EXPLOSION </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>BUCHAREST, April 3 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Air accident investigators probing Friday's crash of a Romanian Airbus in which 60 people were killed confirmed on Monday they were looking at the theory that there might have been an explosion aboard the aircraft. </P>
<P> ``We have several reasons which suggest that there could have been an explosion on board the plane before it crashed, but nothing can be said for certain for the time being,'' a Romanian member of the investigating team told Reuters. </P>
