<TITLE> Gingrich wants national dialogue on balanced budget</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Gingrich wants national dialogue on balanced budget</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CONGRESS-CONTRACT-SPEECH (EMBARGOED) </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>RELEASE AT 8 p.m. EDT  WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P> ( House Speaker Newt Gingrich pledged Friday that Social Security would be ``off the table'' as Congress worked at balancing the nation's budget in seven years. </P>
<P> In an extraordinary televised address, Gingrich called for Americans' participation in a new dialogue to create a partnership to remake the government and balance the budget. </P>
