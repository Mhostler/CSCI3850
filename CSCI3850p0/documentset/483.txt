<TITLE> ARMY SAYS REBELS BLEW UP TOWERS IN GUATEMALA</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> ARMY SAYS REBELS BLEW UP TOWERS IN GUATEMALA</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-GUATEMALA-REBELS </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>GUATEMALA CITY, Guatemala (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Leftist rebels blew up three electricity towers Wednesday and plunged part of Guatemala into darkness, the army said Friday. </P>
<P> The attacks caused an extensive blackout in an area 60 miles west of Guatemala City, the army said. </P>
<P> The government and guerrilla commanders of the Guatemalan National Revolutionary Unit have been negotiating an end to the country's 33-year civil war for more than three years, but the rebels continue to carry out sporadic attacks against economic targets. </P>
