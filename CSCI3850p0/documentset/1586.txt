<TITLE> Eight Haitians killed, U.S. sailor wounded in gunfight</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Eight Haitians killed, U.S. sailor wounded in gunfight</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-HAITI-GUNFIGHT URGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>CAP HAITIEN, Sept 24 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  U.S. Marines shot dead nine Haitians outside police headquarters in this northern coastal city after an American sailor was slightly wounded in the leg, military officials said. </P>
<P> In Port-au-Prince, a U.S. military spokesmen listed nine Haitian casualties after Marines opened fire following gunshots from police headquarters at 7:25 p.m. EDT. </P>
