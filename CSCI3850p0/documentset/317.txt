<TITLE> LEGIONNAIRE'S BACTERIA FOUND ABOARD CRUISE SHIP</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> LEGIONNAIRE'S BACTERIA FOUND ABOARD CRUISE SHIP</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-USA-SHIP </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>NEW YORK (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The bacteria that causes Legionnaire's disease has been found aboard a cruise ship after at least three passengers came down with the potentially deadly disease, the cruise line announced Wednesday. </P>
<P> Celebrity Cruises said the federal Centers for Disease Control and Prevention has confirmed the presence of Legionella bacteria on board its ship Horizon, now docked in Bermuda. It said the approximately 1,200 passengers on board will be flown home even though the ship has been cleansed since the tests were done. </P>
<P> In addition to the confirmed cases, there at least 10 other suspected cases of infection by the bacteria. </P>
