<SUBJECT> BC-GERMANY-BUNDESBANK-RATES -2 FRANKFURT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES>:</NOTES>
<DATELINE></DATELINE>
<P> There had been some speculation of a rate cut this week following renewed weakness of the dollar. However, most economists had doubted that the central bank would actually announce a change. </P>
<P> The Bundesbank made clear in a statement that it had taken the step to ease monetary policy following a slowdown in German M3 money supply growth, the central bank's key indicator for monetary policy. </P>
<P> The discount rate, which forms the floor for money market rates in Germany, had been unchanged since May 13, 1994. </P>
