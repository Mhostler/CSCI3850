<TITLE> Two arrests made in Michigan over Oklahoma bomb-ABC</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Two arrests made in Michigan over Oklahoma bomb-ABC</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-BLAST-ARREST-RAID </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>NEW YORK, April 21 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Police investigating the Oklahoma City bombing have arrested two people in a raid on a farmhouse in Decker, Michigan used by a paramilitary group, ABC news said. </P>
<P> The report came after Justice Department sources said one of two suspects in the case had been captured in Oklahoma state. </P>
