<TITLE> IRANIAN PILGRIMS KILLED IN BUS CRASH - PAPER</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> IRANIAN PILGRIMS KILLED IN BUS CRASH - PAPER</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-IRAN-CRASH </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>TEHRAN, Iran (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Twenty-four Iranian pilgrims were killed and 16 injured when a bus taking them home from an Iranian shrine collided with a truck, an Iranian newspaper reported Saturday. </P>
<P> The group was on its way back from Mashad, 720 miles east of Tehran, to Abad village south of the capital when the accident happened on Friday, the Kayhan newspaper said. </P>
<P> Thousands of pilgrims visit the holy shrine at Mashad of Imam Reza, where a bomb exploded on June 20 killing 24 people. Reza, a grandson of the Prophet Mohammad, is the only one of the ``Fourteen Infallibles'' revered by Shi'ite Muslims who is buried in Iran. </P>
