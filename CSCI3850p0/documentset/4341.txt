<TITLE> Mexico's Zedillo halts army in Chiapas, calls for peace</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Mexico's Zedillo halts army in Chiapas, calls for peace</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-MEXICO-CHIAPAS-ZEDILLO URGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>MEXICO CITY (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  President Ernesto Zedillo said Tuesday he had ordered the Mexican army to halt offensive actions in the southern state of Chiapas and called on rebels to seek a political solution to the conflict there. </P>
<P> Zedillo also said he would send an amnesty law to Congress Wednesday for any rebels who wish to give up their arms. </P>
