<TITLE> Cuban Foreign Minister to visit Canada next month</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Cuban Foreign Minister to visit Canada next month</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CUBA-CANADA </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>HAVANA, Cuba (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Cuban Foreign Minister Roberto Robaina will visit Canada March 19-25, the Foreign Ministry said Thursday. </P>
<P> The minister's visit will follow a trip to Cuba this month by Canada's deputy foreign minister for Latin American and Caribbean affairs, Catherine McCallion, ministry spokesman Rafael Dausa told reporters. </P>
<P> The spokesman said McCallion met with senior Cuban officials, including Robaina and Deputy President Carlos Lage, during her trip, but did not give further details. </P>
<P> Canadian companies are among leading foreign investors in the Caribbean island, especially in mining. </P>
