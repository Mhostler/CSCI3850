<TITLE> Mitsubishi Bank, Bank of Tokyo confirm merger plan</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Mitsubishi Bank, Bank of Tokyo confirm merger plan</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-JAPAN-BANK-PLAN </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>TOKYO, March 28 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Two elite Japanese banks, Mitsubishi Bank Ltd and Bank of Tokyo Ltd, said on Tuesday they have reached a basic agreement to merge, although details are still being worked out. </P>
<P> The marriage between the two -- rumoured for years -- would create the world's biggest bank with assets of more than 72 trillion yen ($808 billion), financial analysts said. </P>
