<TITLE> Boxing-McCall to defend heavyweight crown against Holmes</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Boxing-McCall to defend heavyweight crown against Holmes</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-BOXING-MCCALL </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>NEW YORK (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Oliver McCall will make his first defense of the World Boxing Council heavyweight title he won last month against former champion Larry Holmes in January, promoter Don King announced Wednesday. </P>
<P> McCall, who has a record of 25-5, won the crown Sept. 24 when he stopped Britain's Lennox Lewis in the second round of their title bout in London. </P>
<P> Holmes, who was heavyweight champion for more than seven years, will be fighting for the title he first won in 1978. </P>
<P> The 61-4 Holmes turns 45-years old next week. McCall, a former sparring partner of Mike Tyson, is 29. </P>
<P> The bout will take place Jan. 21, but no site has been selected, King said. </P>
