<TITLE> Bad weather delays space shuttle landing</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Bad weather delays space shuttle landing</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-SPACE-SHUTTLE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>CAPE CANAVERAL, Fla (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  NASA said it was delaying the space shuttle's return by at least 90 minutes Monday because of cloudy skies and a forecast for thunderstorms in Florida. </P>
<P> The next landing opportunity for Discovery and its crew will come at 3:55 p.m. EDT, after one more orbit of Earth, the space agency said. </P>
