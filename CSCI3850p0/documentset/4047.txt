<TITLE> British writer Gerald Durrell dies at 70</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> British writer Gerald Durrell dies at 70</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-BRITAIN-DURRELL </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>LONDON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  British writer and zoologist Gerald Durrell has died at the age of 70, the BBC reported Monday. </P>
<P> It said Durrell, known for books such as ``My Family and Other Animals,'' died at his home on the English Channel Island of Jersey. </P>
