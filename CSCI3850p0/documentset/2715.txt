<TITLE> Cab Calloway dead at 86</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Cab Calloway dead at 86</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CALLOWAY 1STLD </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES>    (Eds: Confirms death, CORRECTS age to 86 pvs New York)</NOTES>
<DATELINE>WILMINGTON, Del (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Cab Calloway, one of the great big band leaders of the golden era of jazz, died Friday night, according to a spokeswoman at a retirement community. He was 86. </P>
<P> The spokeswoman said Calloway died at 9:30 p.m. EST at the medical facility of the Cokesbury Retirement Village in nearby Hosckessin. </P>
<P> Calloway had been ailing since suffering a stroke in June. </P>
<P> The spokeswoman said there will be a memorial service sometime after Thanksgiving, according to family members who were with Calloway when he died. </P>
