<TITLE> ARAFAT TO VISIT JERICHO TUESDAY - POLICE</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> ARAFAT TO VISIT JERICHO TUESDAY - POLICE</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-MIDEAST-ARAFAT-JERICHO </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>JERUSALEM (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  PLO chairman Yasser Arafat will go to Jericho Tuesday for the first time since the start of self-rule, the Palestinian police commander of the West Bank town said Sunday. </P>
<P> Brigadier General Haj Ismael Jabr told reporters: ``Arafat will visit Jericho Tuesday morning.'' A senior security official in the enclave said Arafat would arrive in Jericho from Gaza and leave the same day for Jordan. </P>
