<TITLE> Indian army chief dies after heart attack</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Indian army chief dies after heart attack</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-INDIA-ARMY </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>NEW DELHI, India (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  India's army chief, Bipin Chandra Joshi, died Friday after a heart attack, the Press Trust of India news agency said. </P>
<P> Joshi, 58, had complained of breathlessness earlier Friday, the agency reported. He died later at a New Delhi military hospital, it quoted an army spokesman as saying. </P>
<P> Joshi was the first Indian chief of army staff to die in office. He became army chief in July 1993. </P>
