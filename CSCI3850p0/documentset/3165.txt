<TITLE> Nine Russians killed in Chechnya so far - Interfax</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Nine Russians killed in Chechnya so far - Interfax</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CIS-CHECHNYA-CASUALTIES </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>MOSCOW (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Nine Russian servicemen have been killed in rebel Chechnya since President Boris Yeltsin sent in troops to end the territory's bid for independence, Interfax news agency reported Tuesday, quoting a government spokesman. </P>
<P> Giving the first word on Russian casualties since the military operation was launched Sunday, spokesman Valentin Sergeyev said another 14 Russian soldiers had been wounded. </P>
