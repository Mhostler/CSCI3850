<TITLE> Argentina envoy believes Cedras going to Panama</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Argentina envoy believes Cedras going to Panama</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-HAITI-CEDRAS-UN </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>UNITED NATIONS (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Argentina's U.N. ambassador Emilio Cardenas said Tuesday he believed Haiti's deposed military ruler, Lieutenant-General Raoul Cedras, would settle in Panama. </P>
<P> Cardenas, in answer to a question, said Cedras would not be going to Argentina, as had been rumoured. </P>
<P> U.S. Ambassador Madeleine Albright said on Monday Cedras was heading for a South American country but would not disclose which one. </P>
