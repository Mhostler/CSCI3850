<TITLE> TORNADO IN SOUTH CAROLINA INJURES 20</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> TORNADO IN SOUTH CAROLINA INJURES 20</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-WEATHER-TORNADO 1STLD </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES> (Eds: adds details)</NOTES>
<DATELINE>LEXINGTON, SC, (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A tornado injured up to 20 people Tuesday, damaged scores of buildings and forced the evacuation of this South Carolina city's central business district, police said. </P>
<P> The twister, a result of the winds generated by the remnants of tropical storm Beryl, damaged a shopping center and several factories and touched off gas leaks that forced the evacuation in the central area of the city. </P>
<P> Lexington, a suburb of Columbia, the state capitol, has a population of 25,000. Officials said five buildings were totally destroyed and there were reports of people trapped in a shopping center. </P>
<P> Tornado warnings have been issued for 42 of the state's 46 counties. </P>
