<TITLE> Algeria says its forces kill 22 Muslim militants</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Algeria says its forces kill 22 Muslim militants</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-ALGERIA-VIOLENCE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>TUNIS (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Algerian security forces hunted down and killed 22 Muslim rebels in the past three days in nine towns and villages, Algeria's official news agency APS, monitored in Tunis, said Tuesday. </P>
<P> More than 40,000 people have been killed in Algeria's civil strife pitting Muslim militants against the army-backed authorities who in January 1992 cancelled a general election the Islamists were poised to win. </P>
