<TITLE> Simpson lawyer asks for delay in trial</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Simpson lawyer asks for delay in trial</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-SIMPSON-DELAY URGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>LOS ANGELES (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  O.J. Simpson's lead defense attorney, Robert Shapiro, Wednesday asked a judge for up to a year's delay in his client's double murder trial because of the massive publicity surrounding the case, and said his client should be freed on bail. </P>
