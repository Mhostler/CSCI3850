<DATELINE></DATELINE>
<P> The official said the European Commission was expected to give EU ambassadors a list of possible retaliatory actions against Ottawa at a meeting Thursday, but that no action was expected to be taken. </P>
<P> The Commission, the EU's executive body, was asked to draw up the list by ambassadors after Canada seized the Spanish fishing vessel Estai in international waters March 9. </P>
<P> The British official said trade sanctions would be a violation of World Trade Organization rules. Other EU nations, including Germany, were also opposed to trade sanctions, the official said. </P>
