<TITLE> Christopher moves at U.N. to end Haiti sanctions</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Christopher moves at U.N. to end Haiti sanctions</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-HAITI-UN </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>UNITED NATIONS (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher formally introduced a resolution in the Security Council Thursday to lift all sanctions against Haiti when ousted President Jean-Bertrand Aristide returns shortly to his country. </P>
<P> ``The time has come to prepare for the resumption of normal economic activities in Haiti,'' he told a council meeting at which nearly half of the members were represented by their foreign ministers or other senior diplomats. </P>
<P> ``The United States and Haiti have introduced, with President Aristide's support, a resolution to lift completely U.N. sanctions when President Aristide returns,'' Christopher said. </P>
