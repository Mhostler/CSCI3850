<TITLE> Iraq says U.N. vote on troop movements positive</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Iraq says U.N. vote on troop movements positive</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-IRAQ-RESOLUTION URGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>BAGHDAD (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Iraq said Monday it found positive elements in the U.NN Security Cuncil resolution barring deployment of its troops in the south near the border with Kuwait. </P>
<P> ``It (resolution) contains relatively obvious positive aspects,'' the government newspaper al-Jumhourya said in a front-page editorial signed by editor-in-chief Salah al-Mukhear. </P>
