<TITLE> Clinton urges fast passage of anti-terror law</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Clinton urges fast passage of anti-terror law</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-BLAST-CLINTON </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  President Clinton Friday urged Congress to shun political quibbling and quickly pass a law strengthening federal powers to combat terrorism, saying, ``We must not dawdle or delay.'' </P>
<P> ``We must not allow politics to drag us into endless quibbling over an important national item,'' Clinton said in pushing for fast passage of anti-terror legislation following the Oklahoma City bombing. ``We must not dawdle or delay. Congress must act and act promptly.'' </P>
