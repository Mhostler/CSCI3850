<TITLE> Russia objects to U.N. tribunal on Bosnian leaders</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Russia objects to U.N. tribunal on Bosnian leaders</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-YUGOSLAVIA-TRIBUNAL-RUSSIA URGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>MOSCOW (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A senior Russian foreign ministry official said Tuesday the decision by a U.N. tribunal to name Bosnian Serb leaders as suspects in future war crimes trials could endanger a peace settlement in the region. </P>
<P> Interfax news agency quoted the official, whom it did not name, as saying: ``Such a step will harm efforts to achieve a peace settlement in the Balkans.'' </P>
