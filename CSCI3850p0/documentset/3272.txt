<TITLE> U.S. may take part in Somalia evacuation - Perry</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> U.S. may take part in Somalia evacuation - Perry</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-SOMALIA-USA URGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>MILDENHALL AIR BASE, England (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The United States is preparing to send warships containing several thousand marines to Somalia and President Bill Clinton is expected to decide within days whether to commit them to help evacuate U.N. forces there, U.S. Defense Secretary William Perry said Friday. </P>
<P> ``We are getting ourselves in a state of readiness to do this,'' Perry told reporters flying home with him from a visit to Moscow. He said a marine amphibious group including the helicopter ship Essex would soon leave the Gulf for Somalia. </P>
