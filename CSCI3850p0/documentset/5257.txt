<TITLE> At least 100 reported dead in Iraqi Kurd clashes</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> At least 100 reported dead in Iraqi Kurd clashes</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-IRAQ-KURDS </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>ANKARA, March 28 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  An Iraqi Kurdish opposition group said on Tuesday at least 100 people had died in clashes between two Iraqi Kurdish factions in northern Iraq. </P>
<P> Shazad Saib, Ankara representative of the Patriotic Union of Kurdistan (PUK), said forces from the rival Kurdistan Democratic Party (KDP) had attacked several PUK positions in the key city of Arbil in northern Iraq on Monday. </P>
