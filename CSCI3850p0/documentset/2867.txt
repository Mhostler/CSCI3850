<TITLE> Exit polls predict Norway to reject EU</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Exit polls predict Norway to reject EU</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-EUROPE-NORWAY-EXIT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>OSLO, Norway (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Norwegians voted to reject membership in the European Union Monday, according to two exit polls broadcast on television moments after polls closed. </P>
<P> One poll, on the private TV2 channel, forecast a wafer-thin majority of 50.2 to 49.8 percent against membership, while an exit poll broadcast on state NRK television forecast a more solid majority against the EU of 52.6 percent to 47.4 percent. </P>
