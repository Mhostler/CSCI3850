<TITLE>  CUBANS LEAVE OIL TANKER AND GO HOME - STATE TV</TITLE>
<HEADLINE>  CUBANS LEAVE OIL TANKER AND GO HOME - STATE TV</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CUBA-TANKER-LEAVE URGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>MARIEL, Cuba (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A group of some 500 Cubans who had tried to go to the United States by boarding an oil tanker at the port of Mariel west of Havana left the vessel voluntarily Monday, state television said. </P>
<P> The group had occupied the Maltese-flagged ``Jusarra'', docked at Mariel, Sunday afternoon. But authorities had stressed that the vessel would be refused permission to leave the port. State television said the group left the boat voluntarily and went home. </P>
