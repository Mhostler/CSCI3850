<TITLE> Two Israelis stabbed near West Bank settlement</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Two Israelis stabbed near West Bank settlement</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-MIDEAST-STABBING </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>HEBRON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Palestinians stabbed and axed two Israeli settlers near the Jewish settlement of Kiryat Arba in the occupied West Bank Thursday, settlers said. </P>
<P> They said Palestinians attacked the two in an Arab-owned hardware store near the settlement. One was stabbed in the head and the other in the chest. </P>
<P> Zvi Katzover, a settlers leader, said the Israelis were unarmed. He said he understood there were at least two attackers. </P>
<P> Palestinians in the store took the two Israelis to a nearby Israeli army roadblock where they were treated before being taken to hospital, settlers said. </P>
