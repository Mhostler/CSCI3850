<TITLE> Muslims said to want delay in lifting arms embargo</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Muslims said to want delay in lifting arms embargo</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-YUGOSLAVIA-USA-EMBARGO </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>UNITED NATIONS (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Bosnian Muslims have backed away from their long-held demand for an immediate lifting of the U.N. arms embargo, U.S. officials said Monday. </P>
<P> While nothing has been decided, they said, the Clinton administration and the Muslim-led government in Bosnia are exploring a delay in moves to lift the embargo beyond an October 15 deadline set by President Clinton. </P>
