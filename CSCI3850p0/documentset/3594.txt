<TITLE> NFL-Lions give Fontes two-year contract extension</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> NFL-Lions give Fontes two-year contract extension</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-NFL-LIONS </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>PONTIAC, Mich.(Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Detroit Lions head coach Wayne Fontes received a vote of confidence Thursday in the form of a two-year contract extension through the 1997 season, the club announced. </P>
<P> Terms of the extension were not disclosed. </P>
<P> The 55-year-old Fontes, whose team lost a wild card playoff game 16-12 to the Green Bay Packers last weekend, had one year left on his original contract. </P>
<P> Fontes, who just completed his sixth full season as head coach of Detroit, led the Lions to the playoffs three times in the last four years. </P>
<P> Detroit is 52-53, including playoffs, in Fontes' six-plus seasons as head coach. </P>
<P> Detroit won the NFC Central division in 1991 and 1993 under Fontes and finished tied for second at 9-7 this season. </P>
<P> Fontes took the Lions within one step of their first Super Bowl after the 1991 season. But Detroit lost the NFC championship game to the Washington Redskins. </P>
