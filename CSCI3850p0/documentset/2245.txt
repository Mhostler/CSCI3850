<TITLE> Brazil police lay siege for governor's son</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Brazil police lay siege for governor's son</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-BRAZIL-SIEGE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>RIO DE JANEIRO, Brazil (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Police have laid siege to the official residence of the governor of northeastern Alagoas state where his fugitive son, convicted of drug offenses, is thought to be hiding out, a police officer said Wednesday. </P>
<P> ``There is a warrant out for the arrest of the governor's son and we believe he is in the residence,'' police inspector Edilson Santos said in a telephone interview. </P>
<P> Around 50 armed police officers have been waiting since Tuesday for a local judge to issue a warrant to enter the building and arrest Geraldo Bulhoes, Santos said. </P>
<P> Bulhoes has been convicted to six years imprisonment for drugs-related offenses, he said. </P>
