<TITLE> Strong tremor rocks northern Japan</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Strong tremor rocks northern Japan</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-QUAKE-JAPAN </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>TOKYO (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  An earthquake rocked northern Japan Saturday in an area hit by a similiar quake last week. </P>
<P> A spokesman for Japan's Meteorological Agency said the quake measured seven on the Richter scale, a tremor capable of causing damage. </P>
<P> The agency issued a tidal wave warning for the eastern coast of Japan stretching from the northernmost island of Hokkaido to Nagoya in central Japan. </P>
<P> The spokesman said there had been no immediate reports of casualties or damage from the quake whose epicenter was located out in the Pacific Ocean. </P>
<P> In last week's quake, three people died and several hundred were injured in an area around the town of Hachinohe about 300 miles north of Tokyo. </P>
<P> The spokesman said Saturday's tremor was believed to be an aftershock of last week's quake, which measured 7.5 on the Richter scale. </P>
