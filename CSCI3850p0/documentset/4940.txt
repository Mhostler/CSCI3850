<SUBJECT> BC-MIDEAST-CLINTON-PEACE -2 WASHINGTON </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE></DATELINE>
<P> Clinton said he and Hassan had discussed the new agreement between Israel and Syria to resume direct talks and were also heartened by new ``energy'' in Israeli-Palestinian dealings and progress between Israel and Jordan. </P>
<P> ``His majesty's visit comes at a time of renewed hope. This is a very encouraging development,'' Clinton said in reference to the prospective Israeli-Syrian talks, agreed upon during a Middle East shuttle mission by U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher. </P>
<P> Clinton added: ``His majesty and I agreed that one key to peace is bringing tangible economic benefits to the people of the Middle East.'' </P>
