<TITLE> Blacksmith behads mother-in-law in Hungary</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Blacksmith behads mother-in-law in Hungary</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-HUNGARY-KILLING </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>SALGOTARJAN, Hungary (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A blacksmith who made swords as a hobby has been charged with beheading his mother-in-law in front of his father, daughter and others with a samurai-style blade he made himself, police said Thursday. </P>
<P> They said the incident occurred apparently during a family argument when the accused, Pal Magulya, visited his estranged wife Wednesday morning. </P>
