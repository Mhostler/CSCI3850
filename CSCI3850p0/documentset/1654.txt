<TITLE> Secretary-general of Mexico's ruling party killed</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Secretary-general of Mexico's ruling party killed</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-MEXICO-SHOOTING-DEAD 1STLD </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES>(Eds: Changes attribution to doctors, adds details)</NOTES>
<DATELINE>MEXICO CITY (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The secretary-general of Mexico's ruling party, Francisco Ruiz Massieu, died Wednesday after being shot by a lone gunman as he left a downtown hotel meeting with party officials, doctors said. </P>
<P> ``He arrived at the hospital in critical condition with a bullet in the left side of the neck ... He was declared dead at 10:30 in the morning,'' Dr Felipe Maldonado Campos of the Hospital Espaol told reporters. </P>
<P> Ruiz Massieu, a close friend of President Carlos Salinas de Gortari and a former governor of the southwestern state of Guerrero, had been designated to be the majority leader for the ruling Institutional Revolutionary Party (PRI) in the new legislature scheduled to hold its first session next month. </P>
<P> It was the second assassination of a major political figure in Mexico this year. </P>
