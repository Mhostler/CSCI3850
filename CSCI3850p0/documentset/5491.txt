<TITLE> Quebec to hold separatation referendum in fall</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Quebec to hold separatation referendum in fall</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CANADA-QUEBEC </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>QUEBEC CITY (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Quebec premier Jacques Parizeau said a referendum on the separation of the French-speaking province from Canada will be held in the autumn. </P>
<P> ``The moment of truth will be this fall,'' Parizeau, leader of the separatist Parti Quebecois, said in a speech to a business group. He did not give a specific date. </P>
