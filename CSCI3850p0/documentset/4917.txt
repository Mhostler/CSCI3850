<TITLE> NLRB complaint filed against baseball owners</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> NLRB complaint filed against baseball owners</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-BASEBALL-COMPLAINT (NEWSBREAK) </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The National Labor Relations Board has filed an unfair labor practices complaint against Major League baseball owners in the latest development of the seven-month-old strike, baseball players union chief Donald Fehr told reporters Tuesday. </P>
<P> Fehr said before an official announcement was to be made that the complaint was filed on behalf of the National Labor Relations Board by its chief NLRB counsel, Fred Feinstein. Feinstein was due to have an official announcement later Tuesday. </P>
