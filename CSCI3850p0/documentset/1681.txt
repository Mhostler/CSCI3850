<TITLE> Harry Saltzman, producer of Bond films, dies at 78</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Harry Saltzman, producer of Bond films, dies at 78</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-FRANCE-SALTZMAN </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>PARIS (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Canadian-born film producer Harry Saltzman, who co-produced the first and most profitable James Bond films, has died at the American Hospital in Neuilly outside Paris, hospital sources said Thursday. </P>
<P> Saltzman, who was 78, died Wednesday, the sources said. </P>
<P> Working with associate Albert ``Cubby'' Broccoli, Saltzman produced 11 Bond thrillers from 1962 to 1974 including ``From Russia with Love,'' ``Goldfinger'' and Thunderball.'' </P>
<P> Saltzman, who also produced ``The Ipcress File'' which launched actor Michael Caine's international career, had been in virtual retirement since 1980. </P>
