<TITLE> Toys R Us plans coupon promotion - paper</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Toys R Us plans coupon promotion - paper</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-USA-TOYS </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>NEW YORK (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Toys R Us Inc will try to stir up early holiday shopping by issuing discount coupons that will be good through October, the Wall Street Journal reported in its Monday editions. </P>
<P> The company, which issued discount coupons in November, told the paper that it will distribute booklets holding $309 worth of coupons in various newspapers next Sunday to millions of homes. </P>
<P> The coupon program last year raised November sales by 10 percent over that of November 1992 and boosted 1993 holiday sales by 7.4 percent over a year earlier, the paper said. </P>
<P> Many discount retailers honored Toys R Us coupons, setting off a price war, the paper said. </P>
