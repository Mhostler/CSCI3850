<TITLE> Art donations net Greenpeace $270,000</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Art donations net Greenpeace $270,000</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-BRITAIN-ART </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>LONDON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A group of art lovers said Thursday it had raised $270,000 for the environmental group Greenpeace through the sale this week of work donated by its members. </P>
<P> The organization Art for our Planet, describing itself as a ``group of artists, art dealers and collectors who are committed to saving the planet from environmental destruction,'' said the money was raised in auctions in Zurich and London. </P>
<P> It said the highest price paid was for an Andy Warhol painting ``The Scream'' sold Thursday at the London auctioneers Sotheby's. </P>
<P> The work, donated by Swiss collector and dealer Ernst Beyeler, sold for $81,630. </P>
<P> The group said the money would help Greenpeace's fleet of ships which travel the world's oceans to highlight threats to the marine environment. </P>
