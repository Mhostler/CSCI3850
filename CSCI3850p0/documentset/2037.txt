<TITLE> At least four killed in British rail crash</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> At least four killed in British rail crash</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-BRITAIN-RAIL </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>LONDON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  At least four people were killed when two passenger trains collided in southern England Saturday, rescue workers said. </P>
<P> A railway spokesman said the accident occurred near Cowden station on the border of the counties of Kent and Sussex on a morning that had seen thick fog in the area. </P>
<P> An ambulance service spokesman said 13 people were taken to the hospital and some passengers were trapped in the wreckage. </P>
