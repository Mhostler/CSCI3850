<TITLE> Pop stars to design clothes for charity</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Pop stars to design clothes for charity</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-BRITAIN-FASHION </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>LONDON, April 30 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Pop stars David Bowie and Phil Collins are to become fashion designers to raise funds for children in Bosnia. </P>
<P> The charity War Child said Bowie and Collins, along with Lou Reed, Iggy Pop and Peter Gabriel, had agreed to design clothes that will be auctioned at a London sale in June. </P>
<P> War Child said it hopes that up to 100,000 pounds ($161,700) will be raised to help children who have suffered from the conflict in former Yugoslavia. </P>
