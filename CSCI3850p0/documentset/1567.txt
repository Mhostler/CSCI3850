<TITLE> Rebel bomb causes damage, injuries</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Rebel bomb causes damage, injuries</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-PERU-BOMB (PICTURE) </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>LIMA, Peru (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Suspected Maoist guerrillas detonated a car bomb outside a chocolate factory Friday, causing minor injuries and damage, bomb squad police said. </P>
<P> Police said a car bomb packing about 180 pounds of dynamite exploded at the P&A D'Onofrio S.A. plant in an industrial section of Lima. </P>
<P> Some people were taken to hospital with cuts and other minor injuries, police said. A television report put the number of injured at 12. </P>
<P> The explosion blasted a hole in the wall of the factory and blew in windows in nearby homes. </P>
<P> Police said pamphlets left at the scene indicated the bombing was carried out by the Shining Path guerrilla group. </P>
<P> More than 27,000 people have died in 14 years of guerrilla war in Peru. </P>
