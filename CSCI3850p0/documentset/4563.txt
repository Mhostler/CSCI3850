<TITLE> Sen. Arlen Specter announces presidential run</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Sen. Arlen Specter announces presidential run</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-POLITICS-SPECTER </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>DES MOINES, Ia.(Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Pennsylvania Sen. Arlen Specter announced Saturday he will formally declare his candidacy for the Republican presidential nomination this spring. </P>
<P> Specter, a moderate from Pennsylvania and the only supporter of abortion rights in the Republican field, made the announcement at a news conference here. </P>
<P> ``After 100 days of exploratory travels, I have decided that I will make a formal announcement this spring of my candidacy for the Republican nomination,'' he said. </P>
<P> He did not specify further when he would make his declaration. </P>
<P> His announcement came just the day after Sen. Phil Gramm of Texas became the first Republican to announce a bid. </P>
<P> Specter, 65, is the only Jewish candidate among six known Republican contenders and also the only one from an eastern state. </P>
