<TITLE> Some 232,000 pilgrims arrive in Saudi for haj</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Some 232,000 pilgrims arrive in Saudi for haj</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-SAUDI-MUSLIMS </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>DUBAI (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Some 232,000 Muslim pilgrims have arrived in Saudi Arabia to perform the annual pilgrimage to Mecca which is due to start in late April, the Saudi Press Agency said Monday. </P>
<P> The official agency said about 229,000 of the pilgrims already in the kingdom had arrived by air to perform the ritual which reaches its climax in the second week of May. </P>
<P> Some 2.5 million Muslims, including more than one million from abroad, performed the haj last year, according to Saudi Arabian newspapers. </P>
<P> The Iranian news agency IRNA reported earlier Monday that the first batch of some 69,000 Iranian pilgrims would start heading to Saudi Arabia, home to two of Islam's holiest shrines, Wednesday. </P>
