<TITLE> Gunman kills two in Greek court</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Gunman kills two in Greek court</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-GREECE-SHOOTING </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>IOANNINA, Greece (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A gunman killed two people and wounded three others seriously in a Greek courtroom Monday before being shot himself, police said. </P>
<P> The man, identified as Constantinos Bitos, 42, burst into the court in this northwestern border city and shot to death state prosecutor Petros Spyrou. He also killed a policeman. </P>
<P> Four people wounded in the court house were hospitalized and undergoing surgery, including Bitos. </P>
<P> Police said they had no explanation for the shooting. </P>
