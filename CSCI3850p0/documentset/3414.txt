<TITLE> Police say plane blocked by gunmen in Algiers</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Police say plane blocked by gunmen in Algiers</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-FRANCE-ALGERIA-PLANE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>PARIS (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Four armed men were preventing an Air France passenger aircraft from taking off from the airport at Algerian capital of Algiers for Paris Saturday, French airport police said. </P>
<P> ``Four unidentified armed men have blocked the plane and are preventing it from taking off,'' a police duty officer told Reuters. It was not immediately clear if the four were on board the plane or were on the runway, he said. </P>
<P> The Airbus plane, flight AF 8969 for Paris, had been due to take off at 11:15 a.m and arrive in the French capital two hours later. </P>
<P> Algeria, a former French colony, has been wracked with violence recently as Islamic fundamentalists stepped up their campaign against the government. </P>
