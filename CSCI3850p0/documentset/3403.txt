<TITLE> Two Israeli soldiers killed in Lebanon -- radio</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Two Israeli soldiers killed in Lebanon -- radio</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-LEBANON-ISRAEL-TOLL </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>JERUSALEM (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Israel radio reported that two Israeli soldiers were killed and seven injured Friday in fierce fighting with pro-Iranian guerrillas in southern Lebanon. </P>
<P> The soldiers were killed in attacks by Hizbollah (Party of God) guerrillas on Israeli posts on the edge of its self-declared security zone, the radio said. </P>
<P> Security sources in southern Lebanon said earlier that one Israeli soldier was killed, while Hizbollah said three died. </P>
