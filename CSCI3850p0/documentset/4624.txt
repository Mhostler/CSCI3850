<TITLE> Perry says Somalia operation ``going very well''</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Perry says Somalia operation ``going very well''</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-SOMALIA-USA-PERRY </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The deployment of U.S. and Italian marines in Mogadishu to protect the withdrawal of U.N. forces from Somalia is ``going very well'', U.S. Defense Secretary William Perry said Tuesday. </P>
<P> ``The mission is Somalia is going very well at this point,'' he told reporters in response to questions. ``All of the reports that I have on this say it is going very well indeed.'' </P>
