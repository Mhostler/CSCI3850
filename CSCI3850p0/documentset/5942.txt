<TITLE> Ugandan rebels slaughter 82 people, army</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Ugandan rebels slaughter 82 people, army</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-UGANDA-KILLINGS URGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>KAMPALA, Uganda (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Rebels of the Lord's Resistance Army (LRA) have killed 82 civilians they abducted in northern Uganda two days ago, a Ugandan military officer said on Saturday. </P>
<P> ``I can confirm to you that Joseph Kony's rebels killed at least 82 people Thursday, 73 of them in one go,'' the officer in the government National Resistance Army (NRA) told Reuters. </P>
