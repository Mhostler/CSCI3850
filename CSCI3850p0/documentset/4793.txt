<TITLE> New PGA Tour event for Vancouver</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> New PGA Tour event for Vancouver</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-GOLF-TOUR </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>PONTE VEDRA, Fla (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  After an absence of 37 years, the PGA Tour will return to western Canada with the addition of the Greater Vancouver Open to the 1996 PGA Tour schedule. </P>
<P> The $1 million event, to be played the week of Aug. 22-28 at a course to be named, is the first new full-field event added to the Tour since The International in 1986. </P>
<P> The 1996 Vancouver event will be played opposite the World Series of Golf, a limited field event in Akron, Ohio. </P>
