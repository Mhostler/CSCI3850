<TITLE> ISRAEL TV SAYS IRAQ OFFERS TO MAKE PEACE</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> ISRAEL TV SAYS IRAQ OFFERS TO MAKE PEACE</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-ISRAEL-IRAQ-PEACE URGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>JERUSALEM (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Israel television reported Friday that Iraq had expressed readiness to make peace with the Jewish state, whose cities Baghdad targeted with Scud missiles in the 1991 Gulf War. </P>
<P> No official comment was available on the surprise report relayed to Israelis accustomed to perceiving Iraqi President Saddam Hussein's government as a hard-line foe. </P>
