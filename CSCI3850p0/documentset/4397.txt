<TITLE> French singer Paradis arrested for drugs possession</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> French singer Paradis arrested for drugs possession</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CANADA-PARADIS </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>MONTREAL (Reuter</DATELINE>
<P>  French singer and actress Vanessa Paradis was arrested at Montreal's Dorval airport on Thursday and charged with possession of hashish, the Canadian Press (CP) news agency reported. </P>
<P> U.S. custom officers say they found about three grams (85 ounces) of hashish in her luggage, according to CP. </P>
<P> Paradis was on her way to New York City, it said. </P>
<P> She was was released after posting a $500 bond and was ordered to appear in court in Montreal next month, CP said. </P>
<P> CP quoted reports as saying Paradis then got on a plane to go back home to Paris. </P>
<P> French radio reported Paradis had been in in Canada to promote film elisa with actor Gerard Depardieu. </P>
