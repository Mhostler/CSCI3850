<TITLE> FINAL GAME BEFORE STRIKE DEADLINE ENDS</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> FINAL GAME BEFORE STRIKE DEADLINE ENDS</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-BASEBALL-STRIKE URGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>NEW YORK, Aug 12 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The final Major League baseball game on Thursday's schedule ended shortly before 1:00 am EDT in Oakland, unofficially beginning a strike by Major League players. </P>
<P> Players Association official Gene Orza said no announcement of a strike would be made and technically the strike will begin when players fail to show up for Friday's games. </P>
<P> No negotiations are scheduled between representatives of the players and owners, virtually assuring baseball's eighth work stoppage in the last 22 years. </P>
