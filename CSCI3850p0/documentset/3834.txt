<TITLE> Simpson judge dismisses two jurors</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Simpson judge dismisses two jurors</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-SIMPSON-JURORS </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>LOS ANGELES (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The judge in the O.J. Simpson case dismissed two jurors Wednesday, one for failing to disclose he had met the football legend at a business function and the other because she was still in an abusive relationship with her boyfriend. </P>
