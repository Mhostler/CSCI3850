<TITLE> U.S. condemns killing of Mexico's PRI leader</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> U.S. condemns killing of Mexico's PRI leader</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-MEXICO-USA 1STLD </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P> (Eds: Updates with White House comments, paras 4-5)  The United States Thursday condemned the fatal shooting of Francisco Ruiz Massieu, the secretary-general of Mexico's ruling political party. </P>
<P> ``The United States condemns the senseless shooting death yesterday of Jose Francisco Ruiz Massieu, secretary-general of Mexico's Institutional Revolutionary Party (PRI) and a prominent member of the newly elected Chamber of Deputies,'' State Department spokeswoman Christine Shelly said in a statement. </P>
<P> Shelly also extended condolences to Ruiz Massieu's family, friends and the Mexican nation. </P>
<P> President Clinton's spokeswoman Dee Dee Myers told reporters the United States was prepared to try to help Mexico clear up the crime if needed. </P>
<P> ``Political violence in Mexico is a source of concern as we go forward,'' she said. </P>
