<TITLE> Israeli satellite came down in sea - source</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Israeli satellite came down in sea - source</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-SPACE-RUSSIA-SATELLITE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>MOSCOW, March 29 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  An Israeli satellite and two Russian ones launched on Tuesday from a cosmodrome in Russia came down in the Sea of Okhotsk in the Russian Far East shortly after take-off, a space official said on Wednesday. </P>
<P> The official, who spoke to Reuters on condition he was not named, had access to details of the operation at Plesetsk cosmodrome on Tuesday. He said the satellites had never got into orbit and came down probably about 10 minutes after they were launched. </P>
