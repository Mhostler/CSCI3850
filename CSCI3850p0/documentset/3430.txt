<TITLE> N.Korea deploys more rockets at border -- report</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> N.Korea deploys more rockets at border -- report</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-KOREA-NORTH </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>SEOUL (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  North Korea recently deployed about 40 new multiple rocket launchers near the demilitarized zone separating the two Koreas, a South Korean newspaper reported Monday. </P>
<P> The Chosun Ilbo, quoting Seoul's defense ministry, said the North could assault Seoul directly with the 240 mm rockets. </P>
<P> A ministry spokesman declined to comment on the report. </P>
<P> The newspaper quoted the ministry as saying the number of multiple rocket launchers deployed near the demilitarized zone rose to 160 with the reinforcements. </P>
<P> It also said the North had obtained two MI-26 transport helicopters from Russia under a contract to buy four of the aircraft. The MI-26 is the largest helicopter in the world. It can carry 100 people or 20-tonnes of cargo. </P>
<P> The newspaper said the North has also added 10 more high-speed assault boats to the 130 it has already. </P>
