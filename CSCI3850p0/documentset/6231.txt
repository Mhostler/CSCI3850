<TITLE> Frankfurt post office blast caused by bomb</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Frankfurt post office blast caused by bomb</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-GERMANY-EXPLOSION-BOMB URGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>FRANKFURT, May 3 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  An explosion which killed one postal worker and injured 11 others at a Frankfurt mail sorting office on Wednesday was caused by a parcel bomb, the city's state prosecutors' office said in a statement. </P>
