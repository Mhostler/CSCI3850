<TITLE> Disney record producer departs</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Disney record producer departs</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-DISNEY </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>LOS ANGELES (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A music producer with Walt Disney Co.'s Hollywood Records who female employees reportedly had complained sexually harassed them has left the company, a spokeswoman said Friday. </P>
<P> Sue Sawyer, a spokeswoman for Hollywood Records, said the producer left the company March 9. She declined to say whether he left voluntarily or was fired. </P>
<P> ``I can only say he's no longer with the company,'' Sawyer said. She declined to confirm published reports that the producer was fired after female employees accused him of sexual harrassment. </P>
<P> The Los Angeles Times Friday reported that executives of Hollywood Records said the producer had been fired following complaints of sexual harassment. </P>
