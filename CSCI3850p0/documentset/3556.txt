<TITLE> Venezuelan army seizes cocaine</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Venezuelan army seizes cocaine</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-DRUGS-VENEZUELA </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>CARACAS (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The Venezuelan army Tuesday seized 247 pounds of cocaine hidden inside suitcases bound for Miami from Maiquetia International Airport outside Caracas. </P>
<P> Army commander Euclides Rojas told reporters the cocaine was found in gift-wrapped packets smeared with mustard to distract sniffer dogs at customs. </P>
<P> Three employees of the local airline Viasa, which was storing the suitcases, were being held for questioning. </P>
<P> It was not known where the drugs came from. </P>
<P> Venezuela is an important transit point for traffickers sending drugs from Colombia and other South American nations to international markets. </P>
