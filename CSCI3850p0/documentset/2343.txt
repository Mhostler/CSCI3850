<TITLE> British Art Historian Pope-Hennessy Dies At 80</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> British Art Historian Pope-Hennessy Dies At 80</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-OBIT-HENNESSY </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>ROME (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  British art historian Sir John Pope-Hennessy, one of the world's leading authorities on Italian Renaissance work, has died at his Florence home at the age of 80, Italian television reported Tuesday. </P>
<P> Pope-Hennessy died Monday night, the report said. It did not give the cause of death. </P>
<P> Oxford-educated Pope-Hennessy began his working life in 1939 on the staff of the Victoria and Albert Museum in London, where he was director from 1967-73. </P>
<P> He occupied the same position at the British Museum from 1974-76 and the following year he became consultative chairman in the department of European paintings at New York's Metropolitan Museum. </P>
<P> In later years he divided his time between the United States and Florence. His many books include studies of Sienese painting and Renaissance artists Uccello and Fra Angelico. He had recently completed a study of Italian sculptor Donatello. </P>
