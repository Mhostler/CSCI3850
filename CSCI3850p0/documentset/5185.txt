<TITLE> Manila detains four Chinese boats in Spratlys</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Manila detains four Chinese boats in Spratlys</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-PHILIPPINES-BOATS </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>MANILA, March 25 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The Philippine navy on Saturday boarded and detained four Chinese fishing boats in the Spratly islands in the latest incident between countries claiming the South China Sea archipelago, the government said. </P>
<P> The Chinese ships were captured by two Philippine navy ships near Alicia reef in the part of the Spratlys claimed by Manila, a presidential palace statement said. </P>
