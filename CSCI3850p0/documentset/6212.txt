<TITLE> U.S. ambassador says Zagreb rocketed by Serbs</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> U.S. ambassador says Zagreb rocketed by Serbs</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-YUGOSLAVIA-CROATIA-ROCKETS URGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>ZAGREB, Croatia (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Rebel Serb missiles caused explosions which killed four people and wounded 120 in Zagreb Tuesday, U.S. ambassador Peter Galbraith said. </P>
<P> He told Reuters there was no doubt the Croatian capital was hit by Orkan rockets equipped with shrapnel-spraying cluster bombs, three of which crashed near his embassy. </P>
