<TITLE> Robbers steal $848,000 from Citibank ATM branch</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Robbers steal $848,000 from Citibank ATM branch</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-USA-ROBBERY </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>NEW YORK (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Two men entered the back room of an ATM branch of Citibank Monday morning disguised as security guards and stole $848,000, police said. </P>
<P> The branch, on Williams Street in Manhattan, has 12 automated teller machines. The robbers appeared at the back door just as the employees were putting money into the machines. </P>
<P> The two knocked on the door, and the employees, thinking they were security guards, let them into the room. One fired a shot into the air and then hit one of the Citibank workers with his gun, police said. </P>
<P> The employee was taken to a local hospital and later released. </P>
<P> The robbery occurred in the morning and by the afternoon the police said they had no suspects. </P>
