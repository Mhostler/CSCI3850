<TITLE> S.AFRICA GUNMEN KILL FAMILY OF FIVE</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> S.AFRICA GUNMEN KILL FAMILY OF FIVE</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-SAFRICA-GUNMEN </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>JOHANNESBURG, South Africa (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Gunmen killed a family of five, including two children, in an attack on their township home east of Johannesburg Friday night, police said Saturday. </P>
<P> Police said Floyd Mabaso, 26, his wife Agnes, 21, their year-old son Toby, three-year-old daughter Ngobilie, and grandmother Emily Mabaso, 65, were shot dead in Wattville by two attackers armed with an AK-47 assault rifle and a handgun. </P>
<P> ``We call upon the community to assist us in apprehending the killers who destroyed a family in cold blood,'' said Captain Govindsy Mariemuthoo, adding the motive for the killings was unknown. </P>
