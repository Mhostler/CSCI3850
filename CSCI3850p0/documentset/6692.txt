<TITLE> Clinton urges NRA to give up funds it raised</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Clinton urges NRA to give up funds it raised</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CLINTON-NRA </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  President Clinton Friday urged the National Rifle Association to give money it raised from a controversial fundraising letter to the families of police officers killed in the line of duty. </P>
<P> ``They ought to give up the ill-gotten gains,'' Clinton said while posing for pictures with police officials. He urged the NRA to ``turn the money over'' to organizations that help the families of police officers who were killed. </P>
