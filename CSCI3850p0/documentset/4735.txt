<TITLE> Villeneuve wins Miami IndyCar race</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Villeneuve wins Miami IndyCar race</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-MOTOR RACING-INDY-WINNER </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>MIAMI (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Canadian Jacques Villeneuve won the season-opening IndyCar race in Miami Sunday, driving a Reynard Ford-Cosworth. </P>
<P> Mauricio Gugelmin of Brazil was second in a Reynard Ford-Cosworth and American Bobby Rahal finished third in a Lola Mercedes-Benz. </P>
