<TITLE> Ethics committee files charges against Packwood</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Ethics committee files charges against Packwood</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-PACKWOOD-CHARGES </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The Senate Ethics Committee said Wednesday there was substantial evidence to find that Sen. Bob Packwood may be guilty of sexual misconduct in 18 incidents. </P>
<P> The committee adopted a resolution calling for a full investigation into the charges against the Oregon Republican as well as other conduct by him that may have brought discredit on the U.S. Senate. </P>
