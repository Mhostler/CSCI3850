<TITLE> ARAFAT LEAVES FOR HOMELAND IN GAZA, JERICHO</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> ARAFAT LEAVES FOR HOMELAND IN GAZA, JERICHO</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-MIDEAST-ARAFAT-DEPARTURE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>CAIRO, Egypt (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  PLO leader Yasser Arafat left Cairo Tuesday on his way to a permanent home in the Palestinian self-rule areas of Gaza and Jericho, ending 27 years of exile in various Arab capitals. </P>
<P> Arafat, accompanied by his wife, Suha, and senior PLO aides flew by plane from Cairo airport to the Mediterranean Egyptian town of el-Arish from where he would travel to Gaza. </P>
<P> It was not immediately known whether Arafat would cross by land through the Rafah crossing or by helicopter to Gaza. </P>
<P> He spent his last night of exile at the presidential Orouba palace in Cairo. </P>
