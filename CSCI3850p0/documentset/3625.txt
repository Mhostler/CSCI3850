<TITLE> Old shell kills two young Moroccan shepherds</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Old shell kills two young Moroccan shepherds</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-MOROCCO-SHELL </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>RABAT, Morocco (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Two brothers aged 9 and 13 died when they threw an old artillery shell into a fire and it exploded in a remote Atlas mountain village, the Moroccan news agency said Saturday. </P>
<P> The shell dated from the days of French colonial times, 1912-56, it said. </P>
