<TITLE> Two trains collide; two injured, one man missing</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Two trains collide; two injured, one man missing</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-COLLISION </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>LOS ANGELES (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Two freight trains burst into flames after colliding and derailing east of Los Angeles Wednesday, injuring at least two crew members and leaving one man missing, officials said. </P>
<P> The brakes failed on one train and it crashed into the other in the Cajon Pass, a mostly rural area about 40 miles east of Los Angeles, a California Department of Forestry spokesman said. </P>
<P> According to the spokesman, one crew member was unaccounted for and that there were unconfirmed reports that he was killed in the crash. </P>
