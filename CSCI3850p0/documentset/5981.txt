<TITLE> Britain names new envoy to United States</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Britain names new envoy to United States</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-BRITAIN-DIPLOMACY </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>LONDON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The head of Britain's Permanent Representation to the European Union, Sir John Kerr, is to be Britain's new ambassador to Washington, the Foreign Office announced Monday. </P>
<P> Kerr, 53, has held the delicate job of representing Britain in Europe since 1990. He held the post of Head of Chancery in Washington between 1984 and 1987. </P>
<P> Kerr will take up his new job in Washington in August. </P>
