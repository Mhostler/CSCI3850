<TITLE> China jails chemist who sold medicine secret</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> China jails chemist who sold medicine secret</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CHINA-MEDICINE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>BEIJING, March 30 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A court in the northern city of Tianjin has jailed for two years a pharmacist who sold the secret recipe to a Chinese heart disease remedy to unlicensed dealers, the Health News said on Thursday. </P>
<P> The formula for the Fast and Effective Heart Remedy Pills, a renowned traditional herbal medicine widely used to prevent and cure angina and other heart problems, was a state secret, the newspaper said. </P>
<P> A Tianjin court jailed pharmacist Xie Qing for two years for selling the formula to a group of illicit medicine dealers who used it to manufacture 130 kg (286 lbs) of the remedy. </P>
<P> The dealers, who made about 22,000 yuan ($2,600) selling the drug, were sentenced to terms ranging from one to 2 3/8 years, and were each fined 2,000 yuan ($240), it said. </P>
<P> The ingredients and formula of traditional Chinese medicines, particularily of famous brand medicines, are a state secret and revealing them seriously harms the fundamental interests of the state, the newpaper commented. </P>
<P> Traditional medicines are commonly used in China, often in tandem with western-style drugs, to treat many types of illnesses. </P>
