<TITLE> Seven killed in Austrian bus crash</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Seven killed in Austrian bus crash</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-AUSTRIA-CRASH </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>VIENNA, Austria (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  At least seven people were killed and up to 20 were injured Monday when a German bus crashed into a stalled Austrian truck on a foggy expressway 75 miles west of Vienna. </P>
<P> Austrian police said the bus, bringing about 50 Yugoslav guest workers back to Germany after the New Year holiday, hit the truck near the town of Amstetten shortly after 4 a.m. </P>
<P> The Automobile Club of Austria said the bus was ripped open and passengers flung out onto the highway were crushed by oncoming cars. ``It was horrible, it looked like a battlefield,'' said a club official at the scene. </P>
<P> Austrian radio said the truck had stalled on a foggy section of the highway. Police were unable to immediately confirm any details about the crash. </P>
<P> The Automobile Club said the bus driver survived but was badly injured and could not be questioned. The expressway was closed for several hours. </P>
