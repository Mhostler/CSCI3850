<TITLE> Israel's Peres expects to see Christopher</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Israel's Peres expects to see Christopher</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-ISRAEL-USA-CHRISTOPHER </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>BUDAPEST (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Israel's Foreign Minister Shimon Peres said Friday he planned to meet Secretary of State Warren Christopher on the margins of the CSCE summit in Hungary. </P>
<P> ``If the Secretary of State will be here we will probably meet as well,'' Peres told a news conference outside a meeting of the Socialist International grouping of left-wing parties. </P>
<P> Peres said he would stay in Budapest for the two-day summit of the 53-member Conference on Security and Cooperation in Europe, which starts Monday. Peres said he would leave Monday evening. </P>
<P> He said he wanted to discuss with CSCE officials the possibility of extending the group's work in mediation and conflict resolution to the Middle East. </P>
