<TITLE> Teacher jailed in plot to kill ex-husband</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Teacher jailed in plot to kill ex-husband</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-USA-TEACHER </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>JEFFERSON, Wis.(Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A 45-year-old high school teacher, charged with hiring three teen-age boys to murder her ex-husband, was held in jail Friday on $150,000 bond. </P>
<P> Two of the three boys, aged 15 to 17, confessed to the April 3 shooting of Ruben Borchardt while he slept. A third boy believed to have moved to Texas was being sought. </P>
<P> The boys were confronted by authorities this week after leaking word of the murder. </P>
<P> Diane Borchardt allegedly hired one of the boys from the study hall she was monitoring, offering as payment her wedding and engagement rings, two cars and the promise of $20,000 from a life insurance policy on her ex-husband. She later gave them a map of her former husband's house, authorities said. </P>
<P> Prosecutors said they would seek to try the youths as adults. </P>
