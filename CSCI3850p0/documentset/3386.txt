<TITLE> Clinton nominates new ambassador to Lesotho</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Clinton nominates new ambassador to Lesotho</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CLINTON-LESOTHO </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  President Clinton Thursday nominated Bismarck Myrick, the U.S. consul general in Cape Town, South Africa, as U.S. ambassador to the African kingdom of Lesotho. </P>
<P> Myrick, a heavily decorated Army infantry commander in Vietnam who worked his way up through the ranks after enlisting as a private, is a senior member of the U.S. Foreign Service . </P>
<P> The 54-year-old Myrick has been U.S. consul general in Cape Town since October 1993. He served in the same position in Durban from 1990 to 1993. </P>
<P> Lesotho is a landlocked country surrounded by South Africa. </P>
