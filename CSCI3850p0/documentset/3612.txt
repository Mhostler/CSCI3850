<TITLE> Man decapitated by elevator door</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Man decapitated by elevator door</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-DECAPITATION </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>NEW YORK (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A man was decapitated by an elevator Friday as he was trying to release the foot of an elderly woman that was stuck in the door, police said. </P>
<P> ``A man in his late 20s was helping the woman when the door closed on his head, decapitating the victim,'' Police Detective Ralph St. Just said. ``The elevator was going up and the body fell down the elevator shaft.'' </P>
<P> He said the woman was not injured in the incident in a New York City government office building in the Bronx but was treated in the hospital for shock. Police gave no further details. </P>
