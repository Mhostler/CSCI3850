<TITLE> BOY GETS DETENTION AFTER 282 ARRESTS</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> BOY GETS DETENTION AFTER 282 ARRESTS</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-BRITAIN-DELINQUENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>LONDON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A British boy arrested 282 times in five years was finally jailed after he stabbed a man in the head with a screwdriver, the Independent newspaper reported Tuesday. </P>
<P> The 15-year-old has a record for burglary, theft, car crime and violence, including knifing a shopkeeper in the neck and attacking two police officers in the foyer of a court. </P>
<P> The Welsh boy, whose arrests have averaged one a week since he was 10, was jailed on Monday for eight months. He cannot be named for legal reasons. </P>
