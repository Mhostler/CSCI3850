<TITLE> Explosion rips through ecstatic pro-democracy march</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Explosion rips through ecstatic pro-democracy march</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-HAITI-BOMB 1STLD URGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES> (Eds: updates with at least two deaths)</NOTES>
<DATELINE>PORT-AU-PRINCE (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  An explosion ripped through an ecstatic crowd of pro-democracy marchers Thursday, wounding about a dozen Haitians, at least two of them fatally, eyewitnesses said. </P>
<P> Haitians on the scene said a grenade was tossed into the crowd as hundreds of people marched past the Port-au-Prince docks. Bleeding victims staggered through the streets, asking for help. A photographer on the scene said a band leading the procession was hit by the explosion. </P>
