<TITLE> 15 firemen, 3 others injured after building explosion</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> 15 firemen, 3 others injured after building explosion</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-EXPLOSION 2NDLD </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES>(Adds details)</NOTES>
<DATELINE>NEW YORK (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Fifteen firefighters and three other people were injured after a fireball explosion shook a fire-story building in Manhattan, officials said Tuesday. </P>
<P> Spokesman George Semidey said the blast occurred in Manhattan's Murray Hill neighborhood - not far from the Empire State Building - at 11:10 p.m. EST Monday. </P>
<P> Another fire department spokesman, Cosmo Romeo, said firefighters were in the process of controlling a one-alarm fire in a building housing a restaurant when a ball of fire exploded inside the building, possibly as a result of fumes or gas in the basement. </P>
<P> Fifteen firefighters, one fire patrolman and two civilians were hurt as a result of the fireball explosion and were being treated for burns and other injuries, Romeo said. </P>
<P> There were no life-threatening injuries, he added. </P>
