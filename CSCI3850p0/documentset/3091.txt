<TITLE> White House doesn't peacekeepers to move now</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> White House doesn't peacekeepers to move now</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-YUGOSLAVIA-USA-PANETTA </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The White House does not expect the United Nations to move its peacekeepers from Bosnia now, White House chief of staff Leon Panetta said Thursday. </P>
<P> ``We at this point do not expect the U.N. to remove the peacekeepers,'' Panetta told reporters. ``There's been no decision to move the peacekeepers.'' </P>
