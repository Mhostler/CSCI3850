<TITLE> Tropical storm moves towards eastern Cuba</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Tropical storm moves towards eastern Cuba</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CUBA-RAIN </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>HAVANA, (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Tropical Storm Gordon moved closer to the southeast tip of Cuba Sunday and Cuban weather officials predicted continued heavy rain in the region. </P>
<P> Civil defense authorities have been on alert since last week for possible flooding. </P>
<P> The center of the storm, carrying 40-mph winds, was just south of Santiago de Cuba early Sunday. </P>
