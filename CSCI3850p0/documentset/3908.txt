<TITLE> Earthquake rocks Iran port at mouth of Gulf</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Earthquake rocks Iran port at mouth of Gulf</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-IRAN-EARTHQUAKE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>NICOSIA, Cyprus (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  An earthquake Tuesday jolted suburbs of Iran's Bandar Abbas, a port at the entrance to the Gulf where an oil refinery and aluminium smelter are being built. </P>
<P> The Iran news agency IRNA said the quake measuring 5.1 on the Richter scale, powerful enough to cause heavy damage in populated areas, occurred at 7:45 a.m. (11 p.m. EST Monday). </P>
<P> Its epicenter was about 600 miles southeast of Tehran. </P>
<P> There were no immediate reports of loss of life or damage to property at the port, site of construction of a 300,000-barrel-per-day refinery and 120,000-ton-per-year aluminium smelter. </P>
