<P> Yeltsin said in his address to the General Assembly that the treaty would be part of a more simplified world security system under the auspices of the United Nations. </P>
<P> The end of the Cold War has not brought peace to the world, he told delegates, saying that separatist conflicts racked the globe and large stocks of nuclear weapons posed especially grave problems. </P>
<P> He said Russia also wanted the major nuclear powers to agree on a permanent test ban and an extension to the nuclear Non-Proliferation Treaty, which is due to be renegotiated next year. He also proposed that the major powers hold a meeting to discuss offering security guarantees to non-nuclear states. </P>
