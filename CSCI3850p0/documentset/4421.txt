<TITLE> Two die as ``suicide'' car hits Serb bus in Austria</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Two die as ``suicide'' car hits Serb bus in Austria</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-AUSTRIA-CRASH </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>VIENNA, Austria (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Two people were killed and 27 were injured when a car deliberately crashed into a busload of Serbians early Saturday in southeastern Austria, police said. </P>
<P> A police spokesman said the 22-year-old Austrian car driver, apparently on a suicide mission, crashed into the front of the bus on a state highway between Altenmarkt and Fuerstenfeld near the Hungarian border. </P>
<P> The car driver and one bus passenger were killed. The car and bus were destroyed in a fire that followed the crash. </P>
<P> A friend of the motorist reported to a local police station after the crash that he had talked of killing himself. </P>
<P> Initial police reports said the bus had Swiss license plates and was headed toward Serbia. </P>
