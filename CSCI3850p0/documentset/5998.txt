<TITLE> Explosion rocks Sarajevo, several wounded</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Explosion rocks Sarajevo, several wounded</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-YUGOSLAVIA-EXPLOSION </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>SARAJEVO, Bosnia-Herzegovina (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  At least five people were wounded in a blast which rocked the besieged Bosnian capital of Sarajevo Monday evening, hospital sources said. </P>
<P> ``It was an exceptionally strong explosion even by Sarajevo standards,'' local reporters said. </P>
