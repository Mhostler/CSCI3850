<TITLE> Croatian-Serb truce talks end without agreement</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Croatian-Serb truce talks end without agreement</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-YUGOSLAVIA-CROATIA-TALKS </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>ZAGREB, Croatia (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  U.N.-mediated talks aimed at halting fighting in a Serb-held enclave of Croatia attacked by government troops ended Monday night without agreement, U.N. officials said. </P>
<P> Negotiators for Croatia and the separatist Krajina Serbs were to consult with their respective leaderships and the United Nations hoped to reconvene talks Tuesday to flesh out details of a cease-fire, a spokesman said. </P>
