<TITLE> N.KOREA TO DISCUSS RESUMPTION OF U.S. TALKS-RADIO</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> N.KOREA TO DISCUSS RESUMPTION OF U.S. TALKS-RADIO</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-KOREA-USA-TALKS </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>TOKYO (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  North Korea said Wednesday it will hold talks with U.S. officials next week arranging a resumption of their high-level dialogue in Geneva. </P>
<P> Radio Pyongyang, monitored in Japan by Radiopress, said that the two sides had agreed to a meeting in New York after Sunday's funeral of North Korean President Kim Il-sung to arrange for the resumption. </P>
<P> The dialogue, aimed at resolving the confrontation over Pyongyang's suspected nuclear weapons drive, opened last Friday, only to be suspended the following day when news broke of Kim's death. </P>
<P> Radio Pyongyang said Wednesday that the head of the North Korean delegation to Geneva, First Deputy Foreign Minister Kang Sok Ju, had returned home to attend the funeral. </P>
