<TITLE> Japan's trade surplus narrowed slightly in March</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Japan's trade surplus narrowed slightly in March</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-JAPAN-TRADE URGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>TOKYO, April 14 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Japan's contentious trade surplus narrowed slightly in March to $13.84 billion, the Finance Ministry said on Friday. </P>
<P> The customs-cleared trade surplus decreased from $13.90 billion in March 1994. But the politically sensitive trade surplus with the United States widened to $5.08 billion from $4.94 billion a year earlier. </P>
