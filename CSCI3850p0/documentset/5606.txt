<TITLE> Tiny Liechtenstein votes to join Euro-market</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Tiny Liechtenstein votes to join Euro-market</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-EUROPE-LIECHTENSTEIN-EEA </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>VADUZ, Liechtenstein, April 9 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The tiny Alpine principality of Liechtenstein said ``yes'' in a weekend referendum to joining the huge European Economic Area (EEA), according to the preliminary offical result on Sunday. </P>
<P> Official tallies from all 11 districts showed 55.9 percent of voters supported joining the free trade bloc stretching from the Arctic to the Mediterranean on May 1. </P>
