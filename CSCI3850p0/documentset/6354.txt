<TITLE> Argentine candidate accuses Cavallo of swindling</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Argentine candidate accuses Cavallo of swindling</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-ARGENTINA-CAVALLO </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>BUENOS AIRES, Argentina (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The Radical Party candidate in Argentina's May 14 presidential elections Sunday accused Economy Minister Domingo Cavallo of ``swindling'' and of making profits of $30 million in a car import scheme. </P>
<P> Speaking in the far southern island province of Tierra del Fuego, Horacio Massaccesi said Cavallo had signed a deal with the provincial economy minister and a local politician for the storage and resale of imported cars, the state-controlled Telam news agency reported. </P>
<P> ``The minister Cavallo is part of this swindle against the people of Tierra del Fuego,'' Massaccesi said. </P>
<P> Massaccesi is trailing in third place in polls for next Sunday's elections. </P>
