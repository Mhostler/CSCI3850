<TITLE> One civilian killed in Israeli shelling</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> One civilian killed in Israeli shelling</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-LEBANON-SOUTH </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>NABATIYEH, Lebanon (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A Lebanese civilian was killed and one wounded Thursday in Israeli shelling of southern Lebanese villages, security sources said. </P>
<P> They said Israeli gunners shelled villages on the Iqlim al-Toufah mountain ridge, killing 41-year-old Hassan Ali Karaki and wounding 75-year-old Rhida Yassin. </P>
<P> In retaliation, Muslim militants fired mortar bombs into an Israeli outpost in Sujud and an SLA barracks in al-Rihan in Israel's south Lebanon occupation zone. </P>
<P> Iklim al-Toufah is a bastion of the pro-Iranian Hizbollah (Party of God), used by its guerrillas to attack Israeli troops and their local militia allies in a bid to oust them from the south. </P>
