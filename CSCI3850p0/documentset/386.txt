<TITLE> RABIN, HUSSEIN SHAKE HANDS TO END HOSTILITY</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> RABIN, HUSSEIN SHAKE HANDS TO END HOSTILITY</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-MIDEAST-SUMMIT-HANDSHAKE URGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The leaders of Israel and Jordan shook hands Monday at the White House, ending nearly a half-century of antagonism and signalling their intent to make a formal peace. </P>
<P> Israeli Prime Minister Yitzhak Rabin and Jordan's King Hussein grasped hands enthusiastically at the start of a Rose Garden ceremony welcoming them to the White House as a beaming President Clinton looked on. </P>
