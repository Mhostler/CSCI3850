<TITLE> Croat kills wife and other man on subway</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Croat kills wife and other man on subway</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-GERMANY-SHOOTING </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>MUNICH, Germany (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A Croat man killed his wife and shot her companion in a Munich subway station Friday before critically wounding himself with a bullet to the stomach, police said. </P>
<P> The 41-year-old man, who had separated from his spouse, lay in wait in the metro station and fired on the victims with a revolver, police said. </P>
<P> His 37-year-old wife, also a Croat, died on the spot. The other man, a 38-year-old Croat, was hit twice in the head and was critically ill. </P>
<P> The woman had filed a complaint against her husband for restraining her against her will and harassment. The man had ``practically held her prisoner,'' a police spokesman said. </P>
