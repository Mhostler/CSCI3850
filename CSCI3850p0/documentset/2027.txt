<TITLE> Special mediator named to negotiate on baseball</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Special mediator named to negotiate on baseball</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-BASEBALL-MEDIATOR -2 WASHINGTON </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE></DATELINE>
<P> ``These negotiations are tough, with difficult issues that need to be resolved,'' Reich said in a statement. ``But baseball fans across the country want these two sides to reach an agreement.'' </P>
<P> Reich said the time was ``right'' to appoint a special mediator to end the dispute that denied the World Series to baseball fans for the first time since 1904. </P>
<P> Reich said Usery, who served as labor secretary under President Gerald Ford, is considered the nation's top mediator of labor disputes. </P>
<P> Last fall, Usery was asked to end a coal operators strike and in he played a key role in bringing together the United Auto Workers, General Motors and Toyota in establishing a joint venture, Reich said. </P>
