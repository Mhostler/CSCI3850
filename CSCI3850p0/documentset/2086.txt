<TITLE> U.N. convoy reported under fire near Gorazde</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> U.N. convoy reported under fire near Gorazde</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-YUGOSLAVIA-CONVOY </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>SARAJEVO, Bosnia-Herzegovina (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A United Nations aid convoy came under fire near the eastern Bosnian town of Gorazde Tuesday and one driver was believed to have been killed and another wounded, a U.N. spokesman said. </P>
<P> U.N. spokesman Col. Tim Spicer said the casualty reports were not confirmed and he could not say who fired at the convoy operated by the U.N. High Commissioner for Refugees. </P>
