<TITLE> Danvers named to post at National Security Council</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Danvers named to post at National Security Council</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CLINTON-DANVERS </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  White House National Security Adviser Anthony Lake Thursday announced the appointment of William Danvers as senior director for legislative affairs at the National Security Council. </P>
<P> Since last June Danvers had been acting senior director for the office, which keeps track of legislation affecting national security issues in Congress. </P>
<P> Prior to going to the National Security Council, Danvers was a senior advisor to Strobe Talbott when Talbott, now deputy secretary of state, was ambassador-at-large to the former Soviet repubics. </P>
