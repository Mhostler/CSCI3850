<TITLE> Israel's Rabin, Jordan's king initial treaty</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Israel's Rabin, Jordan's king initial treaty</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-MIDEAST-PEACE URGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>JERUSALEM (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Leaders of Israel and Jordan initialled a draft peace treaty Monday, cementing an end to their countries' 46-year state of war in a live Israel Radio broadcast from Amman. </P>
<P> Egypt, which signed a treaty with Israel in 1979, is the only other Arab country to make peace with the Jewish state. </P>
