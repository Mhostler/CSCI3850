<TITLE> Clinton hasn't decided on Russia summit</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Clinton hasn't decided on Russia summit</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CLINTON-SUMMIT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  President Clinton has not yet made a decision on whether to attend May 8 celebrations in Moscow to mark the end of World War II, White House spokesman Mike McCurry said Thursday. </P>
<P> McCurry declined to link the decision to the conflict in Chechnya but said that conflict was a ``factor in bilateral relations.'' He spoke with reporters who asked his reaction to a letter from 63 congressmen asking Clinton to boycott the meeting unless there is an end to the Chechnya crisis. </P>
