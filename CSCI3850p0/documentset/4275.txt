<TITLE> Many Americans want Clinton to stop at one term-poll</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Many Americans want Clinton to stop at one term-poll</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CLINTON-POLL </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  More than half of adult Americans think President Clinton should retire at the end of his term, according to survey results published Friday by CNN/USA Today/Gallup. </P>
<P> The poll of 1,010 adult Americans found that 55 percent believe Clinton should be a one-term president while 38 percent think he should seek re-election. </P>
<P> It also found that if the election were held today, Sen. Robert Dole, R-Kan., would beat Clinton 51 percent to 44 percent. </P>
<P> In a three-way race with Texas billionare Ross Perot, Dole again was favored by 41 percent to Clinton's 39 percent and Perot's 18 percent. </P>
<P> The poll had a margin of error of plus or minus three percentage points. </P>
