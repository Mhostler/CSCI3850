<TITLE> Alternate jurors chosen in Simpson case</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Alternate jurors chosen in Simpson case</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-SIMPSON-JURY </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>LOS ANGELES (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A panel of alternate jurors consisting of seven blacks, four whites and one Hispanic was chosen Thursday to back up the main jury of eight blacks, two Hispanics, one white and one mixed-race person in the O.J. Simpson double-murder trial. </P>
