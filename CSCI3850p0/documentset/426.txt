<TITLE> BRITAIN TO GIVE JEWISH TARGETS 24-HOUR PROTECTION</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> BRITAIN TO GIVE JEWISH TARGETS 24-HOUR PROTECTION</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-BRITAIN-BLAST-POLICE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>LONDON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Britain is to give round the clock armed police protection to prominent Israeli and Jewish sites after two bomb attacks in less than 12 hours, a police spokesman said. </P>
<P> Assistant Police Commissioner David Veness told a news conference that rigid parking restrictions would also be imposed around what could be viewed as potential targets for bomb attacks by Middle East extremists. </P>
