<TITLE> Graf pulls out of Brighton indoor tournament</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Graf pulls out of Brighton indoor tournament</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-GRAF </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>BRIGHTON, England (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Top seed and world number one Steffi Graf has pulled out of next week's Brighton international women's indoor tennis tournament, organizers said Tuesday. </P>
<P> Graf, six times Brighton champion, has been suffering from a back injury and has not played since losing to Spain's Arantxa Sanchez Vicario in last month's U.S. Open final. </P>
<P> The German's withdrawal leaves defending champion and world number four Jana Novotna of the Czech Republic the top seed. </P>
