<TITLE> Senate will vote on budget amendment Feb. 28</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Senate will vote on budget amendment Feb. 28</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CONGRESS-BUDGET </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The Senate will vote on the balanced budget amendment to the Constitution on Tuesday, Feb. 28, Senate Majority Leader Robert Dole, R-Kan., has announced. </P>
<P> Dole and Senate Minority Leader Thomas Daschle, D-S.D., said late Thursday that the vote will occur after 2:15 p.m. on that day, following votes on proposed changes to the amendment. </P>
