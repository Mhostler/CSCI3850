<TITLE> Suspected militants kill three Egyptian policemen</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Suspected militants kill three Egyptian policemen</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-EGYPT-MILITANTS </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>ASSIUT, Egypt (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Gunmen thought to be Moslem militants killed three policemen on guard outside a resthouse for state security police officers in southern Egypt, security sources said Friday. </P>
<P> The gunmen shot and wounded them Thursday evening in the town of Beni Mazar, in the Nile Valley 105 miles south of Cairo, and the policemen died later in hospital. </P>
<P> The gunmen fled in a pickup truck. </P>
<P> Beni Mazar is in the province of Minya, the center this year of the conflict between the police and the militant Gama'a al-Islamiya (Islamic Group), which wants to overthrow the government and set up a strict Islamic state. </P>
<P> Some 670 people have been killed in political violence in Egypt since the Gama'a started fighting in 1992. </P>
