<TITLE> REPUBLICANS TO ALLOW SENATE VOTE ON HEALTH REFORM</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> REPUBLICANS TO ALLOW SENATE VOTE ON HEALTH REFORM</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-HEALTH-CONGRESS-VOTE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Senate Minority Leader Bob Dole said Senate Republicans will stop talking on health reform and allow a vote Tuesday night on the first amendment to the complex bill, avoiding an all-night session. </P>
<P> ``We're prepared to have a vote at about 6:30 (p.m. EDT)'' on a pending amendment on child health care, Dole said following a Republican caucus. </P>
