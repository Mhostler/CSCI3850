<TITLE> Policeman killed in southern Egypt</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Policeman killed in southern Egypt</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-EGYPT-POLICE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>CAIRO, Egypt (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Suspected Muslim militants in southern Egypt shot and killed a policeman, the latest casualty in the long-running battle between authorities and militants trying to set up a purist Islamic state. </P>
<P> Security sources said Saturday the militants shot the policeman, Obeyda Abdel-Muttaleb, as he walked in the vegetable market in the town of Nagi Hammadi, about 312 miles south of Cairo. He died instantly. </P>
<P> About 462 people have been killed and more than 800 wounded since the militant campaign began in 1992. </P>
