<TITLE> Bombay police ban riots film after Moslem protest</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Bombay police ban riots film after Moslem protest</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-INDIA-MOVIE-BAN </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>BOMBAY, April 7 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Indian police banned the screening of a controversial film about Hindu-Moslem riots in Bombay due for release on Friday after Moslem leaders protested that it implied they had instigated the bloodshed. </P>
<P> A police spokesman said the order applied only to Bombay and barred the screening of the the film ``Bombay,'' set against the backdrop of 1993 riots, in which 800 people were killed in India's commercial capital. </P>
