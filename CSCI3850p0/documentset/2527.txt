<TITLE> Lost Turner painting sells for nearly $50,000</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Lost Turner painting sells for nearly $50,000</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-BRITAIN-PAINTING </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>LONDON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A lost watercolor by 19th century British landscape painter J.M.W. Turner, found in the living room of an English couple who thought it was a copy, sold for $46,600 Thursday, auctioneers Sotheby's said. </P>
<P> The view of of Sisteron in France was bought by an anonymous buyer for almost twice the estimated price. The painting was last seen in public when it was sold at auction in 1894. </P>
