<TITLE> Poll says 72 pct of Russians do not trust Yeltsin</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Poll says 72 pct of Russians do not trust Yeltsin</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-RUSSIA-CHECHNYA-POLL </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>MOSCOW (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  An opinion poll released Sunday showed 72 percent of Russians do not trust President Boris Yeltsin, who launched a bloody war with the breakaway region of Chechnya last month. </P>
<P> The poll, carried out for independent Russian television channel NTV, showed only eight percent of the 1,300 people polled on Jan. 20 trusted the increasingly unpopular leader. </P>
<P> A further 13 percent said they had ambiguous feelings toward him. </P>
<P> A poll of 1,341 people Jan. 7 showed 66 percent of people did not trust the Russian leader, with only 11 percent backing him. </P>
<P> Hundreds of Russian soldiers and Chechens have died since Yeltsin sent in the troops Dec. 11 to crush Chechnya's three-year independence bid. </P>
