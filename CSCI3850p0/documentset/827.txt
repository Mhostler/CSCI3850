<P> Battles at that time subsided swiftly after the warning by the U.N. Protection Force commander in Bosnia. </P>
<P> The spokesman told reporters front lines east of Breza inside the ``Total Exclusion Zone'' shook with 240 artillery shell and mortar bomb explosions in a five-hour period on Tuesday. After a two-hour pause, detonations resumed at more sporadic intervals. </P>
<P> Two Bosnian Serb tanks were spotted violating the zone east of government-held Breza. UNPROFOR said it would protest to the Serb high command, but was otherwise evaluating the situation and no immediate counter-measures were planned. </P>
<P> Last week's fighting in the TEZ, most of which is held by Serbs, was apparently provoked by Muslim-led government troops trying to pin down Serb units so they could not counter a separate Bosnian army offensive in central Bosnia. </P>
