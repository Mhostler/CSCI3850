<TITLE> BOMB EXPLODES AT ISRAELI EMBASSY IN LONDON</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> BOMB EXPLODES AT ISRAELI EMBASSY IN LONDON</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-BRITAIN-BOMB 1STLD URGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES>    (Eds: Updates with details)</NOTES>
<DATELINE>LONDON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A powerful bomb exploded on the grounds of the Israeli embassy in west London Tuesday, a London ambulance service spokesman said. </P>
<P> Police said they believed three people had been injured in the blast, which rocked nearby buildings, but none were seriously hurt. </P>
