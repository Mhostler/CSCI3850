<TITLE> Argentina to extradite Italian wanted on drugs charge</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Argentina to extradite Italian wanted on drugs charge</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-DRUGS-ARGENTINA-ITALY </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>BUENOS AIRES (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Argentina is to extradite an Italian suspected of heading a Milan-based cocaine and heroin smuggling network operating in Colombia, Argentina, Spain and Switzerland, court sources said Friday. </P>
<P> They identified him as Pasquale Mollica, born in Calabria in 1948, and said he was arrested in Buenos Aires in November 1993 after an Italian court put out a warrant for his arrest. </P>
