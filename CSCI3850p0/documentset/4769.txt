<TITLE> Three-year-old boy found alone with mother's corpse</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Three-year-old boy found alone with mother's corpse</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CORPSE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>CHICAGO (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Police said Tuesday they found a toddler alone in an apartment where his mother's three-day-old corpse was lying in a bedroom. </P>
<P> The three-year-old boy had told a neighbor his mother was asleep Friday but police were called to the apartment Monday because of complaints of a foul odor. </P>
<P> The boy was fairly healthy. His mother, Lashone Lewis, 25, was pronounced dead at the scene. There were no signs of violence and authorities said she may have died of natural causes. </P>
