<TITLE> French song composer Gaste dead at 88</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> French song composer Gaste dead at 88</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-FRANCE-GASTE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>PARIS (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  French popular song composer Loulou Gaste, who wrote the top hits of his singer wife Line Renaud, died Sunday at the age of 88, relatives said. </P>
<P> Television said he died of cancer at his home in suburban Paris. He had composed about 1,000 songs for such stars as the late Yves Montand. </P>
<P> His wife's biggest success, ``Ma Cabane au Canada'' (My hut in Canada), was a household tune in France in the 1950s. </P>
