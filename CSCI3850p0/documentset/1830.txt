<TITLE> Britain's oldest man dies at 109</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Britain's oldest man dies at 109</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-BRITAIN-OLD </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>LONDON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Britain's oldest man, 109-year-old William Procter, has died at a nursing home -- just two weeks after the death of Britain's oldest woman. </P>
<P> Procter, who served as a soldier in both world wars and recalled seeing 19th century monarch Queen Victoria, died Wednesday night, two weeks after 113-year-old Rebecca Hewison. </P>
<P> Procter's granddaughter, 60-year-old June Stephens, said: ``He was quite proud to be the oldest man. He couldn't really believe that he had lived so long.'' </P>
