<TITLE> Albright criticizes UN for turning down air strikes</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Albright criticizes UN for turning down air strikes</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-YUGOSLAVIA-BOSNIA-USA </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>UNITED NATIONS (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  U.S. Ambassador Madeleine Albright on Monday criticized a U.N. decision ruling out NATO airstrikes against Serb shelling of a Sarajevo suburb that killed 10 people. </P>
<P> ``I fail to understand the logic behind turning down such a request given the kinds of activities that have taken place in and around Sarajevo in the last 24 to 28 hours,'' she told reporters. </P>
