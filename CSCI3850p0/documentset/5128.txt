<TITLE> Hong Kong police snare soft-drink spiker suspect</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Hong Kong police snare soft-drink spiker suspect</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-HONGKONG-SPIKER </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>HONG KONG (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Hong Kong police said Thursday they had arrested a suspect in a spate of mysterious drink-spiking cases in the tourist district of Kowloon. </P>
<P> The ``Kowloon Spiker'' has struck more than a dozen times, injecting a hypnotic drug into store-shelf drinks, trailing the buyer, then relieving the dazed victim of their cash, according to investigators. </P>
<P> ``The (arrested) man is suspected to be in connection with a kidnapping case and the spiked drink case,'' said a police spokeswoman who added that the suspect was still being questioned. </P>
