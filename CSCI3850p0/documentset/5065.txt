<TITLE> One killed, three injured in NY building collapse</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> One killed, three injured in NY building collapse</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-COLLAPSE 1STLD </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES> (Adds details)</NOTES>
<DATELINE>NEW YORK (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A wall of a Harlem tenement building collapsed Tuesday, killing one person and injuring at least three others, police said. </P>
<P> Police Detective Julio Martinez said some people were trapped in the rubble and firefighters were searching the building by hand and with dogs for any more victims. </P>
<P> ``There are reports of people trapped in the building and we have one dead and two injured sent to Harlem Hospital so far and one injured to St Luke's Hospital,'' Martinez said. </P>
<P> A New York Fire Department spokesman said part of the six-story residential building, believed to be the outside wall, collapsed into an alley. He said it was not yet known what caused the wall to give way. </P>
