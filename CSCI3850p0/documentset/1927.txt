<TITLE> Iraq ambassador says troops moving north</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Iraq ambassador says troops moving north</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-IRAQ-USA-HAMDOON </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Iraq's ambassador to the United Nations said Tuesday that Iraqi troops are being withdrawn but darkness may slow their movement north away from the border with Kuwait. </P>
<P> ``The troops have received orders to withdraw,'' Ambassador Nizar Hamdoon said on ABC's ``Good Morning America.'' He said ``it may take a few hours, I am not a military guy,'' but said they would be moved ``somewhere else in the country, but surely to the north, farther away from the borders.'' </P>
