<TITLE> CHRISTOPHER SEES ISRAEL-JORDAN PEACE WITHIN MONTHS</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> CHRISTOPHER SEES ISRAEL-JORDAN PEACE WITHIN MONTHS</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-MIDEAST-SUMMIT-CHRISTOPHER </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Secretary of State Warren Christopher said Monday he expects Jordan and Israel to reach a peace agreement in a ``matter of months.'' </P>
<P> Christopher said a joint declaration to be signed by the two nations later Monday at the White House ``will accelerate the process toward'' that treaty. </P>
