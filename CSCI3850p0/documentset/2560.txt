<TITLE> Sprinting great Wilma Rudolph is dead at 54</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Sprinting great Wilma Rudolph is dead at 54</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-RUDOLPH-DEATH </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>NASHVILLE, Tenn.(Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Wilma Rudolph, the Olympic gold medalist who overcame polio, scarlet fever and pneumonia to become one of the world's greatest athletes, died of cancer at her home Saturday, officials said. She was 54. </P>
<P> Her sports career reached its highest peak at the 1960 Olympics in Rome, where she overcame a twisted ankle to win three gold medals. </P>
