<P> At 0206 GMT, the dollar stood at 86.77/87 yen and 1.3755/65 marks compared to its New York close on Friday of 86.55/65 yen and 1.3725/35 marks. The U.S. currency had hit a post-World War Two global low of 86.30 yen in New York. </P>
