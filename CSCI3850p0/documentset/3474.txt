<TITLE> Arkansas judge rules in favor of Clinton</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Arkansas judge rules in favor of Clinton</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CLINTON-JONES </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>NEW YORK (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A Federal judge in Arkansas ruled on Wednesday that a sexual harassment lawsuit brought against President Bill Clinton would not go to trial until Clinton is out of office, CNN reported. </P>
<P> The judge in Little Rock, Arkansas, said the court would be allowed to take testimony and other procedures in the lawsuit filed by Paula Corbin Jones, a former Arkansas state employee. </P>
