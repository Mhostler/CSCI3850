<TITLE> Balladur concedes defeat in French election</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Balladur concedes defeat in French election</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-FRANCE-BALLADUR URGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>PARIS (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Conservative Prime Minister Edouard Balladur conceded defeat in France's presidential election Sunday and called on his supporters to vote for fellow Gaullist Paris mayor Jacques Chirac in the run-off. </P>
