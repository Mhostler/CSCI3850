<TITLE> Injured Courier out of world's richest tournament</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Injured Courier out of world's richest tournament</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-COURIER </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>MUNICH, Germany (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Jim Courier has pulled out of next week's $6 million Grand Slam Cup -- the world's richest tennis tournament -- because of a foot injury. </P>
<P> The American has been replaced in the 16-man event, which starts in Munich Tuesday with the winner collecting $1.5 million, by Ukraine's Andrei Medvedev. </P>
