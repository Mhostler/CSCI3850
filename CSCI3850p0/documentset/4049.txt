<TITLE> Israel sends bomber accomplice to prison for life</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Israel sends bomber accomplice to prison for life</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-MIDEAST-BOMBER </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>JERUSALEM (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  An Israeli military court sentenced a Palestinian to life in prison plus 10 years for helping a suicide bomber kill eight Israelis in April last year, the army said Monday. </P>
<P> The court said Mohammed Kmayel, in his teens, helped plan the car bombing in the city of Afula in northern Israel. </P>
<P> Israel Television said Kmayel denied involvement and accused interrogators of extracting his confession by force. </P>
<P> ``Not everyone wants to die the death of a saint. I, for one, don't believe that I must die so I won't do it,'' Kmayel told Israel Television. </P>
<P> The Islamic militant Hamas group claimed responsibility for the the car bomb attack near a bus stop which killed eight Israeli civilians and wouded dozens. </P>
<P> Another militant Palestinian group, Islamic Jihad, said its members were responsible for a suicide bombing north of Tel Aviv last week in which 21 Israelis were killed. </P>
