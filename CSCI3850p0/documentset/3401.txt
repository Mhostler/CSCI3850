<TITLE> Russia puts troop death toll in Chechnya at 44</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Russia puts troop death toll in Chechnya at 44</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-RUSSIA-CHECHNYA-CASUALTIES </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>MOSCOW (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A total of 44 Russian soldiers have been killed since the start of the military intervention in rebel Chechnya, Interfax news agency said Friday, quoting official figures. </P>
<P> The agency, giving figures issued at a meeting of state and government officials presided over by First Deputy Prime Minister Oleg Soskovets, said 116 soldiers and officers had been wounded since the operation was launched Dec. 11. </P>
<P> It gave no figures for the estimated number of Chechen separatist fighters or civilians killed in the incursion which is aimed at ending the North Caucasus territory's bid for independence. </P>
<P> Eyewitnesses said at least 20 civilians were killed Thursday when jets launched missile and bomb strikes on the Chechen capital of Grozny. </P>
