<TITLE> Israeli sources say kidnapped soldier killed -- CNN</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Israeli sources say kidnapped soldier killed -- CNN</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-MIDEAST-ISRAEL-DEATHS 1STLD </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>NEW YORK (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Israeli security sources said three Israeli soldiers, including kidnapped Corporal Nachshon Waxman, were killed in a raid on Friday in the West Bank, CNN reported. </P>
<P> The network, monitored in New York, said three kidnappers from the Palestinian group Hamas were also killed in the raid by Israeli troops to try and rescue Waxman. </P>
<P> The raid happened hours after Hamas had extended the 1900 GMT deadline to kill Waxman unless the Israeli government released 200 Palestinian prisoners. </P>
