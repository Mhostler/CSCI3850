<TITLE> Four arrested for spraying mace onto crowded subway</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Four arrested for spraying mace onto crowded subway</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-MACE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>NEW YORK, March 23 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Four people were arrested Thursday for spraying mace onto a crowded rush-hour subway train, police said. </P>
<P> Six people were affected by the mace, none seriously, said Transit Police spokesman Thomas Russo. </P>
<P> The incident occurred at about 5:45 p.m. on a southbound local No. 1 train at the 110th Street station on Manhattan's Upper West Side, police said. </P>
<P> The four apparently sprayed the subway car with mace as they left, but precise details of the attack were not immediately available, police said. Their identities were not immediately know. </P>
