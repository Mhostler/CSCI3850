<TITLE> Wholesale prices jump 0.5 percent in April</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Wholesale prices jump 0.5 percent in April</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-ECONOMY-PRICES </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Wholesale prices showed the biggest jump in five months in April, the Labor Department said Thursday, as gasoline and vegetable prices surged and raw material costs posted the largest rise in 1 3/8 years. </P>
<P> The Producer Price Index rose 0.5 percent last month after being flat in March. While prices of a variety of goods rose, food prices fell a second straight month, with beef and veal prices taking their biggest tumble in nearly two decades. </P>
