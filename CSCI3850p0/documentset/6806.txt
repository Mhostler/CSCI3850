<NOTES>:</NOTES>
<DATELINE></DATELINE>
<P> Gou was taken away by four policemen from her Beijing home, her mother said by telephone. Police gave no reason. </P>
<P> ``I'm worried. My child's health is not good. She did not do anything,'' the mother said. Gou has heart problems and suffered a mild stroke when she was questioned by police last year. </P>
<P> Gou, 33, wife of imprisoned dissident Xiao Biguang, was detained for three hours on Tuesday. </P>
<P> Police boost security in Beijing each year in the weeks leading up to the emotive June 4 anniversary. </P>
<P> They have repaired video cameras overlooking Tiananmen Square, where the protests were centred, and warned dissidents not to go out or hold meetings. </P>
<P> Many dissidents said that in recent years they were taken out of Beijing for several weeks for the June 4 anniversary and kept in hotels before being freed. </P>
