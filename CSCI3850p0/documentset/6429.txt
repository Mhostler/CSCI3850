<TITLE> Fire destroys files at HQ of Menem's top rival</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Fire destroys files at HQ of Menem's top rival</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-ARGENTINA-FIRE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>BUENOS AIRES, Argentina (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A fire destroyed files at the campaign headquarters of Argentine President Carlos Menem's top rival in elections scheduled for this weekend, police said Wednesday. </P>
<P> The fire swept through a floor where Jose Bordon's aides and strategists meet daily to plan their bid to thwart Menem's drive for re-election, police sources told the state news agency Telam. </P>
<P> The agency quoted Bordon as saying he suspected arson. </P>
<P> Bordon, who is running for the center-left Frepaso alliance, has accused Menem's camp of planning physical violence against him. </P>
<P> Neither he nor his aides could be reached immediately. </P>
