<TITLE> UNHCR planning mass Kurd evacuation in N. Iraq</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> UNHCR planning mass Kurd evacuation in N. Iraq</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-TURKEY-KURDS-EVACUATION </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>GENEVA, March 25 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The U.N. refugee agency is planning to evacuate up to 2,000 Turkish Kurd refugees from the Iraqi town of Zakho, out of the way of Turkish troops, a spokesman said on Saturday. </P>
<P> Rupert Colville said the U.N. High Commissioner for Refugees had a convoy of 50 vehicles including trucks and minibuses ready to evacuate the Kurds from Zakho to Atrush, more than 100 km (60 miles) by road to the south. U.N. guards will escort the convoy. </P>
