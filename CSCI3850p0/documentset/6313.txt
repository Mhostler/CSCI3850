<TITLE> Polish parliamentarian shoots aggressive drunk-PAP</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Polish parliamentarian shoots aggressive drunk-PAP</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-POLAND-SHOOTING </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WARSAW, Poland (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A senior Polish parliamentarian shot and wounded a drunk army cadet who struck him over the head early Friday, PAP news agency reported. </P>
<P> It said that Kazimierz Modzelewski, head of the lower house foreign economic relations committee, was ending a midnight stroll with colleagues in the northern town of Torun when they were accosted by the young man who refused to leave them alone. </P>
<P> One of the group was knocked down in the scuffle. When Modzelewski intervened the man struck him and threatened to kill him, the agency quoted the local prosecutors' office as saying. </P>
<P> Modzelewski warned he had a pistol then shot at the ground, wounding the cadet in the thigh. Police detained the young man, identified only as Maciej M. and took him to a hospital. </P>
<P> Modzelewski, a member of the ruling Democratic Left Alliance (SLD), had a permit for the gun and was sober, the agency quoted the prosecutor as saying. </P>
<P> ``Modzelewski acted during the incident in defense of a woman accompanying them, who was molested by Maciej M.'' PAP reported a fellow parliamentarian, Kazimierz Pantak, as saying. </P>
