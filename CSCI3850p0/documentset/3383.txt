<TITLE> U.S. freelance photographer killed in Grozny</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> U.S. freelance photographer killed in Grozny</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CHECHNYA-PHOTOGRAPHER </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>GROZNY, Russia (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A U.S. freelance photographer was killed by a bomb blast in the capital of the breakaway Chechen republic Thursday, Western reporters said. </P>
<P> Cynthia Elbaum was photographing the aftermath of a Russian air force raid when she was caught by a fresh blast, they said. </P>
<P> The U.S. embassy in Moscow confirmed that Elbaum, 28, had been killed. She was one of at least 20 people who died in heavy bombing of Grozny Thursday as Moscow stepped up its efforts to crush the region's three-year independence bid. </P>
