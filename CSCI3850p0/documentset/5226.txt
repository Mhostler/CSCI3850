<TITLE> Clinton confers with New Zealand leader</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Clinton confers with New Zealand leader</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CLINTON-BOLGER </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  President Clinton met Monday with Prime Minister Jim Bolger of New Zealand for talks on trade, investment opportunities and other issues. </P>
<P> White House Press Secretary Mike McCurry said the Oval Office session covered ``a variety of issues of concern to both governments, chief among them trade and investment opportunites.'' </P>
<P> The visit was the first by a New Zealand prime minister to the White House in 11 years. </P>
