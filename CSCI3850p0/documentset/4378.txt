<TITLE> Republicans win study of Clinton defense strategy</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Republicans win study of Clinton defense strategy</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CONGRESS-DEFENSE-VOTE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The Republican-controlled House Thursday supported creation of a commission to determine if President Clinton's ``two wars'' defense strategy should be replaced. </P>
<P> The House rejected 211-207 a Democratic effort to kill the commission promised in the Republicans' ``Contract With America.'' </P>
