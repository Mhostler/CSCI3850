<TITLE> EUROPEAN PARLIAMENTARIANS DROWN ZHIRINOVSKY SPEECH</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> EUROPEAN PARLIAMENTARIANS DROWN ZHIRINOVSKY SPEECH</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-AUSTRIA-ZHIRINOVSKY </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>VIENNA, Austria (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  European parliamentarians booed and banged their fists when Russian ultra-nationalist Vladimir Zhirinovsky declared in a speech Tuesday that Russia would win a Third World War, the Austrian news agency APA reported. </P>
<P> The agency said Zhirinovsky made the remark to about 500 parliamentarians at a meeting organized by the Conference on Security and Cooperation in Europe. ``Russia will win the Third World War definitely and without any doubt,'' said Zhirinovsky, a member of the Russian parliamentary delegation. </P>
