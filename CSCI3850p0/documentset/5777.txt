<TITLE> India to test medium range missile next week</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> India to test medium range missile next week</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-INDIA-MISSILE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>NEW DELHI, April 16 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  India is to test fire its surface-to-air medium range missile from its test range in the eastern state of Orissa any time between April 17 and 24, the Press Trust of India (PTI) said. </P>
<P> PTI quoting offical sources said late on Saturday that the locally-developed Akash missile will be test fired from its range at Chandipur in Orissa. </P>
<P> The sources said there was every liklihood that two Akash missiles would be tested within that period. They will be the seventh and eighth tests in the series. </P>
<P> Akash missile was last test fired successfully from the Chandipur range on August 14, 1990. </P>
