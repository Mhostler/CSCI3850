<TITLE> Italian senator caught speeding at 134 mph</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Italian senator caught speeding at 134 mph</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-ITALY-SPEED </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>VENICE, Italy (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Italian senator Stefano Stefani has been forced to pull over from life in the fast lane after being fined for driving 134 mph. </P>
<P> The top highway speed limit in Italy is 81 mph. </P>
<P> Police caught Stefani, 57, a leading member of the Northern League party, speeding on a motorway near Venice earlier this month, the ANSA news agency reported Thursday. </P>
<P> He was handed a fine of $320 and risks having his driving license suspended for a month. </P>
<P> Ansa quoted the legislator as saying he did not realize he was driving so fast and normally does not do so. </P>
