<TITLE> Algeria says hijackers surrender, free passengers</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Algeria says hijackers surrender, free passengers</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-HIJACK-SPAIN-ALGERIA 1STLD </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>TUNIS, Tunisia (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Three hijackers who forced an Algerian plane to fly to Majorca Sunday have freed all passengers and surrendered to Spanish authorities, Algeria's official news agency APS said in a report from Madrid. </P>
<P> The agency, monitored in Tunis, gave no source for its report. </P>
<P> The Spanish national radio reporting from Palma de Majorca in Spain said the three hijackers freed all remaining passengers and crew but did not say if they had surrendered. </P>
<P> No other details were immediately available. </P>
