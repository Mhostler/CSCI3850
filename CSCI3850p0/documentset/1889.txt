<TITLE> Christopher arrives in Israel</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Christopher arrives in Israel</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-MIDEAST-CHRISTOPHER-ARRIVAL </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>TEL AVIV, Israel (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Secretary of State Warren Christopher, facing a crisis in the Gulf, arrived in Israel Sunday in a renewed effort to break the Israeli-Syrian deadlock over the occupied Golan Heights. </P>
<P> Christopher made no comment at Tel Aviv airport before driving to Jerusalem. Meetings with Israeli leaders were scheduled to begin Monday. </P>
