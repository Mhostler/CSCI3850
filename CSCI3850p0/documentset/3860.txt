<TITLE> Israeli helicopters rocket post in south Lebanon</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Israeli helicopters rocket post in south Lebanon</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-LEBANON-ISRAEL-RAID </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>NABATIYEH, Lebanon (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Israeli helicopters Friday rocketed and strafed with cannon fire an abandoned Lebanese army barracks in south Lebanon where guerrillas were believed to be hiding, security sources said. </P>
<P> The sources said two helicopter gunships fired at least one rocket and about 1,000 rounds of 800-mm cannon at the barracks near the market town of Nabatiyeh facing Israel's occupation zone in south Lebanon. </P>
<P> There was no immediate word on casualties. </P>
<P> Israeli troops killed three guerrillas in an overnight firefight in a wadi bordering the zone, bringing to eight the number killed in two days, the sources said earlier Friday. </P>
