<TITLE> Uprising at prison by illegal aliens</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Uprising at prison by illegal aliens</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-UPRISING </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>PHOENIX, Ariz.(Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  About 100 illegal aliens in a medium-security federal prison staged an uprising Monday to protest the food and restrictions on inmates, a prison official said. </P>
<P> The prisoners, held at the Federal Corrections Facility in Eloy, 65 miles southeast of Phoenix, were carrying baseball bats when they came into the cafeteria for supper, a dispatcher at the prison said. </P>
<P> The dispatcher said officials and guards brought the uprising under control in less than two hours. </P>
<P> There were no reports of injuries. </P>
<P> The illegal aliens are being held at the prison for the U.S. Immigration and Naturalization Service. </P>
<P> Last month inmates at Eloy staged a riot over the issues of food and excessive restrictions on inmate movements. </P>
