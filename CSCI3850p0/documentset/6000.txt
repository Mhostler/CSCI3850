<TITLE> Photographers arrested at Oklahoma bomb site</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Photographers arrested at Oklahoma bomb site</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-BLAST-PHOTOGRAPHERS </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>OKLAHOMA CITY (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Police Monday arrested two men trying to take photographs of bodies inside the bomb-blasted Oklahoma federal building, apparantly intending to sell the pictures to tabloid television shows. </P>
<P> Police did not disclose the identities of the men but said one had gained access to the wrecked building several times posing as a volunteer firefighter. </P>
<P> Police said that on the first day of the disaster one of the men removed an Oklahoma City Fire Department jacket, pants and helmet from the scene. </P>
<P> Police said he returned wearing the equipment and carrying ''several disposable cameras.'' He was arrested after he was seen shooting pictures of the bodies. </P>
<P> A second person was also arrested taking pictures, but details of that arrest were not disclosed. </P>
<P> It was not clear if the men were to be charged. </P>
