<TITLE> 70 killed in train collision in Egypt - sources</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> 70 killed in train collision in Egypt - sources</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-EGYPT-COLLISION URGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>CAIRO, April 15 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Seventy people were killed when two Egyptian trains collided on the Cairo-Alexandria line in the Nile Delta on Saturday, security sources said. </P>
<P> The collision took place near the town of Quweisna, about 55 km (35 miles) north of Cairo, they said. </P>
