<TITLE> Colombian bus drives off cliff, killing seven</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Colombian bus drives off cliff, killing seven</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-COLOMBIA-ACCIDENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>BOGOTA, Colombia (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A bus drove off a mountain road and plunged down a steep cliff in central Colombia killing seven of its passengers and wounding 30 others, police said Monday. </P>
<P> The accident in central Colombia occurred, they said, after the bus' brakes failed. The bus was driving to the town of San Diego from nearby Samana along a windy, unpaved road in mountainous Caldas province early Sunday when it went off the cliff. </P>
<P> The passengers were going to a political rally for a candidate in the Oct. 30 mayoral elections of San Diego, located about 95 miles northwest of Bogota. </P>
