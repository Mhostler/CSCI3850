<HEADLINE> Palestinian killed in West Bank violence</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-MIDEAST-CLASH </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>RAMALLAH, West Bank (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Israeli soldiers shot dead a Palestinian during unrest Tuesday in the occupied West Bank, a local hospital said. </P>
<P> Witnesses said Mahmoud Raled Hamayel, 34, was shot in the chest in the town of Ramallah and buried soon after. </P>
