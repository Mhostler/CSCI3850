<TITLE> Menendez brothers to be tried before single jury</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Menendez brothers to be tried before single jury</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-MENENDEZ-TRIAL </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>LOS ANGELES (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A judge ruled Monday that Erik and Lyle Menendez will be tried together before a single jury in their retrial on charges of murdering their wealthy Beverly Hills parents. </P>
<P> Superior Court Judge Stanley Weisberg made the decision despite arguments from the brothers' attorneys that they could not get a fair trial with one jury. Their first trial ended in a mistrial in January 1994 after separate juries deadlocked on charges against them. </P>
