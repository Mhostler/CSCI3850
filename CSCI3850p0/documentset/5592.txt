<TITLE> Aerolinas Argentinas pilots to hold Easter week strike</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Aerolinas Argentinas pilots to hold Easter week strike</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-ARGENTINA-PILOTS </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>BUENOS AIRES, Argentina (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Pilots of the privatized airline Aerolineas Argentinas will hold a 24-hour strike Tuesday which will affect domestic and international flights, the pilots' union said Saturday. </P>
<P> The industrial action will come just before the Easter weekend, with public holidays beginning in Argentina Thursday. </P>
<P> The 440 Aerolineas pilots and co-pilots are protesting the airline's refusal to renew a productivity agreement which ended March 31, under which their pay was boosted 20-40 percent for an additional 15 hours of work a week. </P>
