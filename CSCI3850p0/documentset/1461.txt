<TITLE> Serbs order heavy guns back from Sarajevo - U.N.</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Serbs order heavy guns back from Sarajevo - U.N.</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-YUGOSLAVIA-WEAPONS </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>SARAJEVO (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The United Nations said the Bosnian Serb military command had ordered its forces Sunday to remove all heavy weapons remaining in the 12-mile exclusion zone around Sarajevo. </P>
<P> U.N. military spokesman Major Koos Sok said there was some concern that an upsurge of fighting Sunday afternoon apparently initiated by the Muslim-led Bosnian government army might cause the Serbs to reconsider the order. </P>
