<TITLE> But will there be a line?</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> But will there be a line?</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-BATHROOMS </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>CHICAGO (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Midway Airlines said Wednesday its planes will feature women-only restrooms, a first in commercial aviation. </P>
<P> The second restroom on board the six Fokker 100 jets the airline operates will be for both men and women. </P>
<P> The carrier said the ladies room will feature an extra mirror, a hand-painted mural, soap and lotion dispensers and flowers. </P>
<P> Chicago-based Midway operates flights to New York, Philadelphia, Boston, Denver, Dallas and Allentown, Pa. </P>
