<TITLE> Iraqi official denies making sabotage accusation</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Iraqi official denies making sabotage accusation</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-IRAQ-AMERICANS-DENIAL </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>BAGHDAD, March 27 (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Iraqi speaker of parliament Saddi Mehdi Saleh denied on Monday that he said two Americans arrested in Iraq this month were planning to carry out acts of sabotage. </P>
<P> He said a report carried by Reuters had misquoted him. </P>
<P> Saleh said their aim in crossing into Iraq illegally from Kuwait was to prolong U.N. sanctions against Iraq. </P>
