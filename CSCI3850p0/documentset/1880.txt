<SUBJECT> BC-IRAQ-KUWAIT-MILITARY - 2 KUWAIT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE></DATELINE>
<P> ``The Interior Ministry announced that the northern region spreading from al-Sabiyah (16 miles north of Kuwait City) to al-Salmi (where its border meets with Iraq and Saudi Arabia) as a military zone,'' it said. </P>
<P> The area isolates Kuwait's northern region adjacent to its border with Iraq from the rest of the country. </P>
<P> The ministry asked Kuwaitis who own property, mostly farms, in the largely uninhabited area to report to the police on Sunday, but it did not elaborate. </P>
<P> Iraqi troops are reported massing near the border with Kuwait which sparked fears of a repeat Iraqi invasion of the emirate in 1990. </P>
