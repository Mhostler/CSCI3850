<TITLE> S.Korea raises death toll from boat fire to 29</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> S.Korea raises death toll from boat fire to 29</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-KOREA-BOAT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>SEOUL, South Korea (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Divers on Wednesday recovered four more bodies from a South Korean lake, bringing to 29 the death toll from a boat fire Monday. </P>
<P> Rescue officials revised the number of passengers and crew aboard the boat which caught fire on a pleasure cruise to 132, which means one person is still missing. </P>
<P> The fire at Chungju Lake 75 miles southeast of Seoul occurred three days after a major river bridge in Seoul collapsed during the morning rush hour, killing 32 people. </P>
<P> Police believe the boat fire started in the vessel's engine room. The boat, which was designed for 127 people, was quickly engulfed by flames. </P>
<P> Police said the captain of the 54-ton vessel survived and was being questioned. In all, 20 survivors are in the hospital. </P>
