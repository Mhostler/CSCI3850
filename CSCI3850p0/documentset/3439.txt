<TITLE> S.African police kill two men carrying toy guns</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> S.African police kill two men carrying toy guns</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-SAFRICA-TOYS (REFILING) </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>JOHANNESBURG, South Africa (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  South African police said on Monday they had shot dead two men over the Christmas weekend in the belief the toy guns they were carrying were real. </P>
<P> A statement said the men were killed in separate incidents in black townships outside Johannesburg, one Saturday and one Sunday. Both had threatened people with the toy guns, it said. </P>
