<TITLE> Death toll rises to 36 in Turkish coal mine blast</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Death toll rises to 36 in Turkish coal mine blast</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-TURKEY-MINE </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>ANKARA, Turkey (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The death toll from a coal mine blast in Turkey rose to 36 Tuesday, Anatolian news agency said. </P>
<P> One miner is still missing. </P>
<P> The agency said 21 bodies were brought to the surface on Tuesday from the mine in Sorgun town of Yozgat province, where an explosion Sunday caused a cave-in. </P>
<P> Ten workers were injured by the explosion believed to have been caused by methane gas. </P>
