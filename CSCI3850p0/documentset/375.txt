<TITLE> U.S. MILITARY BEGINS AIR DROPS FOR RWANDAN REFUGEES</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> U.S. MILITARY BEGINS AIR DROPS FOR RWANDAN REFUGEES</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-RWANDA-USA-AIRDROP URGENT </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>BONN, Germany (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  The U.S. military has begun air dropping supplies to Rwandan refugees stranded in the Zairean border town of Goma, a spokesman said Sunday. </P>
<P> ``We have already air dropped 60,000 pounds of supplies,'' Commander Ron Morse, spokesman of the U.S. army's European Command in Stuttgart, told Reuters. </P>
