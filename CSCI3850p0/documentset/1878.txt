<TITLE> Christopher to visit Kuwait on Middle East trip</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Christopher to visit Kuwait on Middle East trip</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-IRAQ-USA-CHRISTOPHER </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>ANDREWS AIR FORCE BASE, Md (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  Secretary of State Warren Christopher announced Saturday, just before departing on a Middle East journey, that he would visit Kuwait during the trip to express solidarity with the emirate. </P>
<P> Amid reports of Iraqi troop movements along the Kuwaiti border, Christopher said his visit ``will provide a very strong expression of our resolve to stand with Kuwait in this uncertain situation.'' </P>
