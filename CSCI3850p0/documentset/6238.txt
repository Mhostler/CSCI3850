<TITLE> Bonn journalist sentenced as East German spy</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Bonn journalist sentenced as East German spy</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-GERMANY-SPY </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>DUESSELDORF, Germany (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A Bonn-based journalist who earned hundreds of thousands of dollars providing information to former East Germany about West German politics was sentenced to 2 3/8 years in jail Wednesday. </P>
<P> The Duesseldorf regional court ruled that Lutz Kuche, who used the cover name David Bakker, had spied for East Germany for 24 years. </P>
<P> He earned at least $647,000 between 1970 and 1989 by selling information about German conservatives. </P>
