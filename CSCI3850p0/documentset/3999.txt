<TITLE> Mexico aid package proving hard sell - Gingrich</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Mexico aid package proving hard sell - Gingrich</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-MEXICO-USA-GINGRICH </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  House of Representatives Speaker Newt Gingrich said on Friday that winning congressional approval of a $40 billion U.S. bailout for Mexico has proved much harder than expected but he still thought it possible. </P>
<P> The Georgia Republican said President Bill Clinton had not yet made a persusaive case for the rescue plan but added: ``I don't want to be pessimistic. I think that it is possible to get something done but it is much harder work than I thought it would be.'' </P>
