<SUBJECT> BC-ARGENTINA-IRAN-PARAGUAY 2 ASUNCION </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE></DATELINE>
<P> Asked by reporters at the scene whether the man held was connected with the car bomb that destroyed Argentina's main Jewish group headquarters on July 18, the judge was evasive, saying the information was confidential. </P>
<P> ``The charges derive from economic crimes, but there are certain connections with other illicit activities that occurred here in Ciudad del Este and in Argentina. That information is confidential,'' the magistrate said. </P>
<P> ``In the next few hours we will study it carefully with the Interpol agents,'' Judge Monges added. </P>
<P> An Argentine judge earlier this week ordered an international manhunt for four Iranian officials who a witness linked to the Buenos Aires attack. Iran denies any participation in the blast. </P>
