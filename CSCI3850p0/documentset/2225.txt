<TITLE> Stand still, dummy, or they'll tax you</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Stand still, dummy, or they'll tax you</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-ARGENTINA-DUMMY </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>BUENOS AIRES (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  An Argentine man pulled on a dress and pretended to be a dummy to escape tax inspectors raiding the textile sweatshop where he worked, one of the inspectors said Tuesday. </P>
<P> Most of the factory's workers bolted through the back door when tax inspectors hunting for national insurance evaders swooped on the factory in the Buenos Aires suburb of Flores. </P>
<P> ``But in a corner there was a curvaceous mannequin in a huge white dress with some scruffy size-45 espadrilles (casual shoes made of cloth and straw) peeping out,'' one of the inspectors told reporters. </P>
