<TITLE> Tanker overturns on Washington beltway</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Tanker overturns on Washington beltway</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-TANKER </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  A tanker overturned on Interstate 495 late Thursday, spilling its load of 8,900 gallons of gasoline and stranding hundreds of motorists heading home to the Virginia suburbs of the nation's capital. </P>
<P> Local television reports said the accident occurred around 6 p.m., shutting down part of one of the main highways in the area at the height of afternoon rush hour. </P>
<P> The stations, citing reports by witnesses, said the tanker overturned as it entered the beltway in Annadale, Va. </P>
<P> A Fairfax County fire official told the local ABC affiliate WJLA-TV the highway would likely reopen in time for the morning commute back to Washington. </P>
<P> He said officials were trying to contain the gasoline, some of which spilled into a small river in the area. </P>
