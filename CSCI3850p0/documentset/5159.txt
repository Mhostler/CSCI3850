<TITLE> Clinton getting annual physical checkup</TITLE>
<HEADLINE> Clinton getting annual physical checkup</HEADLINE>
<SUBJECT> BC-CLINTON-HEALTH </SUBJECT>
<AUTHOR></AUTHOR>
<TEXT>
<NOTES></NOTES>
<DATELINE>WASHINGTON (Reuter) </DATELINE>
<P>  President Clinton Friday was to spend close to five hours at Bethesda Naval Hospital getting his annual physical examination. </P>
<P> A White House spokeswoman, Ginny Terzano, described the checkup as a routine one that would cover all aspects of the president's health. </P>
<P> Clinton has been in good health throughout his two years as president. He gets regular exercise and has a strong appetite. </P>
<P> Clinton was to fly by helicopter to Bethesda Naval Hospital in the morning and return to the White House shortly after noon. </P>
